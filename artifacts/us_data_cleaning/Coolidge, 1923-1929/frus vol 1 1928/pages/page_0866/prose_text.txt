Certain Delegations were of opinion that as regards State manufacture, publicity should be given in terms of total value per category and that publicity in regard to State manufacture must be brought into line with the general conditions which will be laid down by the Convention for the Limitation of Armaments. Certain Delegations, while accepting that remark, consider that it must not be interpreted in such a way as to subordinate the convening of a Conference on Private Manufacture to that of the General Conference on the Reduction and Limitation of Armaments. 3. The French Delegation, while abstaining from voting upon this Article, stated that its Government was ready to consider in regard to the State manufactures referred to in the present Article, any publicity corresponding to the supervised limitations which may be fixed by the Convention for the Reduction and Limitation of Armaments.
