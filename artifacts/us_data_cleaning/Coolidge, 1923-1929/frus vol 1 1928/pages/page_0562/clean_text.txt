This I did, at the same time including in a second note, also dated August 27, a copy of the official French text of the Treaty, in compliance with the Department’s Instruction dated August 21 received from the American Embassy in Paris.35 The Austrian Foreign Office on the same day gave a translation of the note for publication, and this appeared in the afternoon editions of August 28. Secretary General Peter, acting for Dr. Seipel in the latter’s absence, informed me that the Federal Chancellor was most anxious to have the note published at the earliest possible moment, and acting under his instructions the Austrian Foreign Office insured its publication in the local press on the day the note was received.
