701.2325/50: Telegram
[Document 420]
The Ambassador in Peru (Moore) to the Secretary of State
[Paraphrase]
LIMA, August 23, 1928—1 p.m. 
[Received 1:44 p.m.]
97. I have been requested by the Minister for Foreign Affairs to ask for the
good offices of the United States for the formal agrément from the
Government of Chile for Cesar A. Elguera as Ambassador to Chile. As
soon as I am informed of the receipt of the Chilean reply, the name will be
made public here.
MOORE
