819.796/17: Telegram [Document 684] The Chargé in Venezuela (Engert) to the Secretary of State [Paraphrase] CARACAS, July 3, 1928—5 p.m. [Received 10:16 p.m.] 71. My telegram No. 70, July 3, 4 p.m. I have been given to understand that the United States has been rather slow in entering this field,17 and that it cannot hope to keep out foreign interests indefinitely. ENGERT 17 See telegram No. 23, July 11, 7 p.m., to the Chargé in Venezuela, p. 787 [Pg. 787 includes portions of Doc. 606, Doc. 607, and Doc. 608].
