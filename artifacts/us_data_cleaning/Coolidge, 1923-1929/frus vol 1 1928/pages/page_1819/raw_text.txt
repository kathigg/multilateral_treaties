820.7961 Dept. of Commerce Flight/13: Telegram
[Document 650]
The Secretary of State to the Minister in Bolivia (Cottrell)
WASHINGTON, January 20, 1928—5 p.m.
2. Department’s No. 1, January 7.90 Department of Commerce states that
Duke Banks is local representative of the Curtiss Airplane and Motor
Corporation and desires Legation and Consulate to cooperate with him in
promoting interest in American aircraft products and aeronautics in Bolivia.
Please comply and inform Consulate.
KELLOGG
