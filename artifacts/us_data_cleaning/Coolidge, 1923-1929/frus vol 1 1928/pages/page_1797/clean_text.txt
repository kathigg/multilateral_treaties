810.796/10: Telegram [Document 636] The Chargé in Peru (Hanna) to the Secretary of State LIMA, April 19, 1928—6 p.m. [Received April 21—11 p.m.] 29. Department’s telegram 6, January 25, 7 p.m. President Leguia assures me that the persistent reports that the Peruvian Government has signed contract with German aeronautical interests to operate in Peru are not true and says that the present intention is to enter into contracts with both American and German interests and let them compete.… Detailed report concerning Woolman’s negotiations are contained in despatch mailed April 18th.81 HANNA 81 Presumably despatch No. 932, Apr. 16, supra.
