Concerning the plan of payment proposed by the International Relief Bonds Committee the Federal Government have the honour to make the following statement: The International Relief Bonds Committee have informed the Austrian Federal Government that they accept in principle their proposal. They start, however, from the supposition that from 1943 onward up to 1968 1250 million schillings must be paid in all, while the proposal of the Federal Government provides a total payment from 1943 to 1968 of 1000 million schillings.
