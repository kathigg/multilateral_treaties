(4) The countries of the Union which, by virtue of the present

written declaration indicating the countries in whose case
protection is restricted, and indicating also the restrictions to
which the rights of authors within the jurisdiction of such country
are subjected. The Government of the Swiss Confederation shall
immediately communicate this fact to all the countries of the
Union.
ARTICLE 6 BIS
(1) Independently of the author’s copyright, and even after
assignment of the said copyright, the author shall retain the right
to claim authorship of the work, as well as the right to object to
every deformation, mutilation or other modification of the said
work, which may be prejudicial to his honor or to his reputation.
(2) It is left to the national legislation of each of the countries of
the Union to establish the conditions for the exercise of these
rights. The means for safeguarding them shall be regulated by the
legislation of the country where protection is claimed.
