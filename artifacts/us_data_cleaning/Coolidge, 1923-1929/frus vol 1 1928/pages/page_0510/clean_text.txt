I am conscious of the abiding sentiment of the Government of Spain and of the Spanish people in favor of peace and of their desire to further any movement which aims at peace. I am also most anxious to express the sentiments of sympathy and of deep regard which both the American Government and the people of this country have for the Spanish Government and the Spanish people. I hope that Spain will be one of the first countries to adhere to the treaty, in this way obtaining for that great nation the same rights, and placing her in same position as any other signatory.
