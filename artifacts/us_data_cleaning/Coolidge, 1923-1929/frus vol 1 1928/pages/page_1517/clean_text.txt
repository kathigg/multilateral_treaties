724.3415/248: Telegram [Document 464] President Coolidge to President Guggiari WASHINGTON, December 12, 1928. I have received Your Excellency’s telegram of December 9 in relation to the regrettable conflict that has taken place between Paraguay and Bolivia and, in thanking Your Excellency for advising me regarding this matter, I desire to express the most fervent hope that the difficulty between your country and Bolivia will soon be composed to the entire satisfaction of both parties. CALVIN COOLIDGE
