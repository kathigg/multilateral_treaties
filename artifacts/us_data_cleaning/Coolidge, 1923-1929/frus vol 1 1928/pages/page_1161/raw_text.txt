of agreements such as those contemplated by the Joint Resolution, to be

Resolution there are enclosed herewith a “Notice to Bearers of Passports,”
Sections III and IV of which relate to the status of naturalized American
citizens; a copy of a letter of June 19 [9?], 1915, to the late Senator Henry

alien parents and of foreign born persons naturalized in this country, and a
copy of a letter of August 18, 1915, to Messrs. Hubbard and Hubbard of
Wheeling, West Virginia,50 concerning the liability for military service in
