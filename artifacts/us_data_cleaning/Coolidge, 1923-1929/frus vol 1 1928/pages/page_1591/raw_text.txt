The Minister in Honduras (Summerlin) to the Secretary of State
TEGUCIGALPA, February 13, 1928—2 p.m. 
[Received 8:11 p.m.]
27. My telegram number 26, February 13, 11 a.m. The Minister for Foreign
Affairs has just shown me copies of telegrams from Honduranean
authorities at Omoa and Cortes reporting that a Guatemalan force of 1 chief
and 50 men has invaded Honduranean territory at Chachagualilla, captured
the chief of the small guard there, and are now occupying that outpost.
Coello stated that his Government has protested vigorously against this
invasion and outrage and has requested immediate restoration of
Honduranean force at Chachagualilla and withdrawal of Guatemalan force.
Repeated to Guatemala.
SUMMERLIN
