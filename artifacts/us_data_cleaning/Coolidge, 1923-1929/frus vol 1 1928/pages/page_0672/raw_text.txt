5. This Government regards the procedure whereby a notice of
adherence is given and ratification deposited by a non-signatory
power before the treaty comes into force as an alternative to and
as consistent with the procedure whereby such formal acts of
adherence are deposited after the treaty comes into force between
the signatory powers, in view of which interpretation no reason is
seen for withdrawal of your notes of September 24 and 28 which,
if Mexican Government so desires, may be merely acknowledged
by that Government.
