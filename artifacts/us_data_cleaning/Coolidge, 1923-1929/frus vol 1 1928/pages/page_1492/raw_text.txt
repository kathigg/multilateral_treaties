711.2412 Anti-War/6: Telegram
[Document 442]
The Minister in Bolivia (Kaufman) to the Secretary of State
LA PAZ, October 12[11?], 1928—1 p.m. 
[Received October 12—10:30 a.m.]
46. Bolivia has signed today and delivered to the Legation the multilateral
treaty which will be forwarded by pouch.53a The President and the Acting
Minister of Foreign Affairs Palacios requested my presence at the Palace
yesterday afternoon and informed me that Paraguay has violated the mutual
understanding in both [sic] construction of three forts in disputed territory.
Both requested me to notify the Department in the effort to use good
influence with Paraguayan Minister in the hope of stopping construction
until such time as the matter can be arranged amicably. My answer was that
I would convey the foregoing message to the Department.
KAUFMAN
53a See despatch No. 58, Oct. 12, 1928, from the Chargé in Bolivia, p. 220
[Pg. 220 is part of Doc. 185].
