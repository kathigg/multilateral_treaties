724.3415/259: Telegram
[Document 468]
The Ambassador in Argentina (Bliss) to the Secretary of State
[Paraphrase]
BUENOS AIRES, December 12, 1928—8 p.m. 
[Received 9:20 p.m.]
95. Foreign Minister states that the Government of Argentina is doing
nothing at the present time in the way of mediation and has no objection to
any action which the Conference may take.
WHITE for BLISS
