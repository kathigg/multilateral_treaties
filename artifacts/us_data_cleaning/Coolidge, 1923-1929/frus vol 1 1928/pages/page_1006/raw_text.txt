(3) These coefficients are multiplied by the number of countries of each
class, and the sum of the products thus obtained furnishes the number of
units by which the total expense is to be divided. The quotient gives the
amount of the unit of expense.
(4) Each country shall declare, at the time of its accession, in which of the
above-mentioned classes it demands to be placed, but it may always
ultimately declare that it intends to be placed in another class.
(5) The Swiss Administration shall prepare the budget of the Bureau and
superintend its expenditures, make necessary advances and draw up the
annual account, which shall be communicated to all the other
administrations.
