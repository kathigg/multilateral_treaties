724.3415/268: Telegram
[Document 472]
The Secretary of State to the Minister in Bolivia (Kaufman)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, December 14, 1928—4 p.m.
36. Your telegram No. 61, December 14, noon. Since the Conference today
by unanimous action (except Bolivia which was not present and Paraguay
which refrained from voting) offered its good offices, and since I am
Chairman of the Conference, I do not desire to suggest anything which
might interfere with the action of the Conference or the acceptance of its
good offices which I am exceedingly anxious to have Bolivia give.
KELLOGG
