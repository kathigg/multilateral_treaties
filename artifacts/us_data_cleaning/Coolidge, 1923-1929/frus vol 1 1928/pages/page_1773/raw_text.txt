It would appear that Article 16 gives to the Company an unqualified
monopoly and I am quite aware of the Department’s attitude Regarding
monopolies. But I feel that the benefits accruing to Haiti from the
contemplated services of this Company are such as to permit of the most
liberal action on the part of the Haitian Government.
I would therefore suggest that the Department, while not approving of the
monopolistic features of Article 16, not object to the giving of this Article
as well as Article 8 in the proposed contract, if the Haitian Government so
desires.
