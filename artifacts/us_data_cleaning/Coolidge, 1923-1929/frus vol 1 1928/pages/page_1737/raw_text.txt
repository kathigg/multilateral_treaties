The Minister in Guatemala (Geissler) to the Secretary of State
GUATEMALA, March 26, 1928.
[Received April 4.]
No. 1861
SIR: With reference to the Department’s cablegram No. 26 [21] of March 16,
5 p.m. instructing the Legation to render all possible and proper assistance
to Mr. John D. MacGregor, Vice President of the Pan American Airways
Incorporated, in his effort to obtain a transport concession, I have the honor
to report, that I arranged for the President of Guatemala to receive Mr.
MacGregor today, and that since then he has informed me, that he has had a
very pleasant preliminary talk with President Chacón concerning his
project, and that he will visit the Minister of the Interior (Fomento)
tomorrow, as suggested by the President, for the purpose of discussing
details.
I have [etc.]
ARTHUR H. GEISSLER
