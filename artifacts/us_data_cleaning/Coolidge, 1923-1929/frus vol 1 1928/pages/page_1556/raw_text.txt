717.2114/74: Telegram
[Document 491]
The Minister in Nicaragua (Eberhardt) to the Secretary of State
MANAGUA, September 20, 1928—11 a.m. 
[Received 3:25 p.m.]
349. My telegram of September 19, 2 p.m. Since the newspapers here are
stating that the United States desired an adjustment of the San Andres
question because we wished to acquire the island[s] from Colombia and it is
even being intimated that a portion of the purchase price will be paid
secretly to Conservative officials here, I recommend that I be authorized not
only to furnish the press with a copy of the Department’s statement about
the negotiations but also to state orally at the same time that the United
States has no intention of acquiring the islands for itself.
EBERHARDT
