The Consul at Geneva (Band) to the Secretary of State
GENEVA, December 15, 1928—11 a.m. 
[Received December 15—10:15 a.m.]
The Council of the League has appointed May83 (American) a member of
the Central Board provided for by the Geneva Opium Convention of 1925.
Full report by mail.81
RAND
83 Herbert L. May, of Pittsburgh.
81 Not printed.
