714.1515/763: Telegram
[Document 567]
The Minister in Duatemala (Geissler) to the Secretary of State
GUATEMALA, July 17, 1928—6 p.m. 
[Received July 18—12:10 a.m.]
95. The Legislative Assembly has passed the bill authorizing the Executive
to submit the boundary question to the International Central American
Tribunal without discussion and by unanimous vote. Repeated to Honduras.
GEISSLER
