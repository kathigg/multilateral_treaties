500.A16/70: Telegram [Document 260] The Secretary of State to the Minister in Switzerland (Wilson) WASHINGTON, October 4, 1928—7 p.m. 90. Your 92, October 2, 3 p.m. Department assumes that decision to call meeting on December 5 is based on last paragraph of resolution adopted by Third Committee as quoted on page 236 of Assembly Journal No. 14, September 18. Please report by telegraph any developments which may have taken place in direction of ending deadlock and whether there is greater prospect of an agreement at forthcoming session. Department did not receive your despatch No. 584, September 10, until yesterday and the comments you request will be sent as soon as it has had time to study your report. KELLOGG
