[Document 139] The Ambassador in Brazil (Morgan) to the Secretary of State RIO DE JANEIRO, August 29, 1928. [Received September 12.] No. 3052 SIR: I have the honor to report that the Department’s telegram of August 27, 1 P.M.28 was not delivered by the telegraph company at this office until after 7 o’clock on the evening of that day, and that since the Foreign Office was closed for business before that hour it was impossible to hand to the Minister for Foreign Affairs, Dr. Octavio Mangabeira, until half past eleven on the morning of August 28, the copy of the note the text of which was contained in the Department’s circular telegram of August 16, 12 M.
