The Minister in Switzerland (Wilson) to the Secretary of State
BERNE, December 28, 1928. 
[Received January 30, 1929.]
No. 693
L. of N. No. 1253

honor to transmit herewith eight copies of the communication received
from the League of Nations31 stating that Mr. Loudon had convened the
next session of the Preparatory Commission for April 15, at 11 a.m. A copy
has been forwarded to Mr. Gibson at Brussels.
I have [etc.]
