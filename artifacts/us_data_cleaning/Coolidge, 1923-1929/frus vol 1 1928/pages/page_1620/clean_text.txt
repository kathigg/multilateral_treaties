714.1515/591: Telegram [Document 526] The Minister in Guatemala (Geissler) to the Secretary of State GUATEMALA, March 22, 1928—1 p.m. [Received 4:57 p.m.] 26. I have communicated the contents of the Department’s March 21, 7 p.m., to President Chacon orally and to the Foreign Office by memorandum. The President says that the matter will be considered this afternoon at special meeting of the Cabinet and that the decision will be communicated to me immediately. GEISSLER
