701.2325/27: Telegram
[Document 413]
The Ambassador in Peru (Moore) to the Secretary of State
LIMA, July 28, 1928—11 a.m. 
[Received 1:10 p.m.]
82. When your 62, July 21, 1 p.m., was brought to the attention of President
Leguia yesterday he said that he saw no reason why Chile should not go
ahead with the procedure mentioned therein.
[Paraphrase.] The President was gratified by this indication of good will on
Chile’s part and added hopefully these words: “We will see how far she is
willing to go.” [End paraphrase.]
MOORE
