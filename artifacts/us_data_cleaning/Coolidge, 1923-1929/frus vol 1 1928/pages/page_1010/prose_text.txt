(1) The countries which introduce into their legislation the term of protection of fifty years provided for by Article 7, paragraph 1, of the present Convention, shall make it known to the Government of the Swiss Confederation by a written notification which shall be communicated at once by that Government to all the other countries of the Union. (2) It shall be the same for such countries as shall renounce any reservations made or maintained by them by virtue of Articles 25 and 27. In faith whereof, the respective Plenipotentiaries have signed the present Convention.
