810.79611 Pan American Airways, Inc./169: Telegram
[Document 622]
The Secretary of State to the Minister in Ecuador (Bading)
WASHINGTON, December 8, 1928—10 a.m.
30. Pan American Airways, Incorporated, informs Department Mr. Harold
E. Harris, General Manager Peruvian Airways Corporation will assist Mr.
Otto Berg von Linde, representative of W. E. Grace and Company, to apply
for operating concession in Ecuador similar to concession recently granted
to Scadta.
Please render all possible and proper assistance. Department is much
interested in the development by American interests of commercial aviation
service between the United States and Latin America.
KELLOGG
