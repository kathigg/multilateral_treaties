The Secretary of State to the Consul at Puerto Cortes (Fox)
WASHINGTON, April 9, 1928—10 a.m.
1. For Minister Davis. The Department has received a cordial telegram
signed by the Chairmen of the Commissions of Guatemala and Honduras
expressing appreciation of the Department’s cooperation in bringing about
the present conference. Please inform your colleagues on the Commission
that this telegram has been received and is greatly appreciated.
KELLOGG
