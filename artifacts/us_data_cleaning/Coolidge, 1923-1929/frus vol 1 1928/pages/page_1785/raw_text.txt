The Chargé in Honduras (Johnson) to the Secretary of State
TEGUCIGALPA, December 24, 1928—5 p.m. 
[Received 9:45 p.m.]
147. MacGregor, representative Pan American Airways, informs me he
signed today with Honduranean Government a contract in terms
substantially as proposed by the company.
JOHNSON
