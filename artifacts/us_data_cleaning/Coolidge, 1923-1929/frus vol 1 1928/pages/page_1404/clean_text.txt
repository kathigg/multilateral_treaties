710.1012 Washington/118: Telegram [Document 397] The Minister in Uruguay (Grant-Smith) to the Secretary of State MONTEVIDEO, October 19, 1928—10 a.m. [Received 10:40 a.m.] 32. Your instruction No. 129, April 14 last.19 The Under Secretary for Foreign Affairs informed me yesterday that the attitude of the Uruguayan Government relative to the arbitration of juridical questions would be the same as at the Rio de Janeiro Conference. The delegate[s] would be named shortly and would go from here. GRANT-SMITH 19 See footnote 66, p. 621 [Pg. 621 includes portions of Doc. 369 and Doc. 370].
