717.2114/71: Telegram
[Document 489]
The Minister in Colombia (Piles) to the Secretary of State
BOGOTÁ, September 16, 1928—4 p.m. 
[Received September 17 (?)—10:20 a.m.]
94. Department’s 55, September 15, 1 p.m. Text of treaty has not yet been
made public but the President has publicly stated the general provisions of
the treaty in his recent annual message to Congress. To date there has been
no press comment.
PILES
