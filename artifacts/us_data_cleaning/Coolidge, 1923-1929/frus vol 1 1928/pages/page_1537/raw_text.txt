I wish to inform Your Excellency that my Government has conveyed to the
eminent President of Argentina, Señor Irigoyen, its acquiescence to his
good offices in order to return to the procedure agreed upon in Buenos
Aires for the settlement of the dispute between Bolivia and Paraguay.
Upon accepting the good offices of the Conference on Conciliation and
Arbitration, Bolivia renders homage to the spirit of America and reiterates
her adherence to the principles of justice with which her political conduct is
inspired.
I salute [etc.]
TOMAS M. ELIO
