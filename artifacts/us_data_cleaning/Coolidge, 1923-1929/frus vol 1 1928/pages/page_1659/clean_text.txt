714.1515/685: Telegram [Document 555] The Minister in Guatemala (Geissler) to the Secretary of State GUATEMALA, May 23, 1928—8 p.m. [Received May 24—2:15 p.m.] 70. The President has remarked to me that he believes that Washington would be a suitable place for the next boundary conference but that San José de Costa Rica would also be acceptable. GEISSLER
