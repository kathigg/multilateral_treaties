711.4112Anti-War/23: Telegram
[Document 36]
The Ambassador in France (Herrick) to the Secretary of State
[Paraphrase]
PARIS, May 3, 1928—1 p.m. 
[Received 1:45 p.m.]
112. Your 122, May 2, 6 p.m. I have heard nothing about either a
commission of technical experts or a conference of Foreign Secretaries. If I
do, I shall endeavor to discourage their adoption.
HERRICK
