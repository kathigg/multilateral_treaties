711.1212Anti-War/5 [Document 172] The Ambassador in Mexico (Morrow) to the Secretary of State MEXICO, September 14, 1928. [Received September 20.] No. 970 SIR: Confirming my telegram No. 256, 3 p.m., of today’s date,70 reporting the receipt of a note from the Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs in answer to Ambassador Morrow’s note of August 27th, 1928, inviting the adherence of the Mexican Government to the Multilateral Treaty for the Renunciation of War as an Instrument of National Policy, I have the honor to enclose a copy and translation of the Mexican note accepting this invitation. I have [etc.] For the Ambassador: H. F.
