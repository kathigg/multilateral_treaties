724.3415/253: Telegram
[Document 462]
The Minister in Paraguay (Kreeck) to the Secretary of State
ASUNCIÓN, December 11, 1928—2 p.m. 
[Received December 12—6:13 a.m.]
29. At the meeting of the diplomatic corps today it was the unanimous
opinion that only the American Government could cause Bolivia to
recognize its treaty obligations and that its failure to do so would cause the
Gondra Treaty and the Kellogg Pact to be of no value.
. . . . . . . . . . . . . .
KREECK
