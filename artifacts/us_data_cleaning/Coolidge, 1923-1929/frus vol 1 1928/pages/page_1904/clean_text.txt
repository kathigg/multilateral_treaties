875.001Zog/12: Telegram [Document 704] The Acting Secretary of State to the Minister in Albania (Hart) [Paraphrase] WASHINGTON, August 28, 1928—6 p.m. 28. Your 48, August 4, 10 a.m. The Department presumes you will continue to keep it advised by cable regarding the proposed change of regime in Albania. The Department also wishes information at the appropriate moment regarding the action your colleagues will take concerning recognition and presence at the coronation. CASTLE
