The slate of the other officers of the Conference and its recommendations,
as drawn up in the informal meeting of March 30, was then approved, and
Signor Vivaldi of Italy was elected Chairman of the Drafting Committee.
The organization of the Conference as finally constituted was as follows:
President:
Dr. Fernando Sánchez de Fuentes (Cuba).
Vice Presidents:
Mr. Arata Aoki (Japan).
Mr. Jan Gawronski (Poland).
Mr. Francisco Bernis (Spain).
Mr. Guglielmo de Vivaldi (Italy).
Mr. Luis Rais (France).
Mr. W. W. Husband (United States).
Mr. Amadeo E. Grandi (Argentina).
Mr. Pedro Erasmo Callorda (Uruguay).
Mr. Carlos Trejo y Lerdo de Tejada (Mexico).
(A vice presidency was reserved for Portugal,
but remained vacant as Portugal had no
representative present at the Conference).
