However, it is natural that this endeavour can not lead to satisfactory results unless, war being eliminated, the nations could dispose of some different yet efficacious means for the solution of crises evolved from unjust and unnatural conditions. The Hungarian Government adheres to the proposal of the Government of the United States under the supposition that the Government of the United States as well as the Governments of the other signatory Powers, will seek to find the means (of) rendering (it) possible that in the future injustices may be remedied by peaceful means. The Hungarian Government will in due time take the necessary measures to assure that its adherence attain full legal validity in accordance with the rules of the Hungarian Constitution.
