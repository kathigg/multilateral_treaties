701.2325/19: Telegram [Document 411] The Ambassador in Peru (Moore) to the Secretary of State LIMA, July 17, 1928—noon. [Received 2:30 p.m.] 77. Department’s 59, July 16, 5 p.m. Saw President Leguia. He says that if our country desires it, he could see no objection to appointment of Ambassadors. It is probable that this question would have to go before Congress which meets July 28th. The President said he is surprised and delighted at the way the matter has been received in Peru and the world generally. MOORE
