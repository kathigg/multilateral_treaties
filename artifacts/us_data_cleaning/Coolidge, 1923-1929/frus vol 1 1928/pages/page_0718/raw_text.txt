500.A15/672: Telegram
[Document 211]
The Secretary of State to the Chief of the American
Representation on the Preparatory Commission (Gibson)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, March 20, 1928—noon.
4. With reference to your 10, March 19, 7 p.m., your conclusion that it
would be most desirable to abstain as far as possible from participation in
debate on the Russian proposals is heartily endorsed. The proposals are not
of primary interest to this Government and we should not assume a leading
role in the discussions under any circumstances.
KELLOGG
