724.3415/224: Telegram [Document 445] The Minister in Paraguay (Kreeck) to the Secretary of State ASUNCIÓN, October 16, 1928—2 p.m. [Received 11:28 p.m.] 15. Information in Department’s telegram No. 9 exaggerated. Socalled fortresses only observation outposts, having only 25 to 30 men. No fortifications other than light arms. All Paraguayan fortifications are in indisputable territory of Paraguay. Full report by mail. KREECK
