711.0012Anti-War/336: Telegram
[Document 135]
The Ambassador in France (Herrick) to the Acting Secretary of
State24
PARIS, August 27, 1928—3 p.m. 
[Received August 27—12:12 p.m.]
265. The Secretary asks that the following message from him be sent at
once to all missions to which the circular telegram of August 16, 11 p.m.,
was sent direct by the Department. Missions reached via Paris are being
instructed from here:
“Anti-war treaty signed this afternoon. Please deliver as soon as
possible the note transmitted in the Department’s circular
telegram of August 16, 11 p.m., dating the note today August 27,
1928. Do not fail to transmit French text of the treaty at the same
time. Frank B. Kellogg, Secretary of State.”
HERRICK
24 Repeated by the Department to the missions concerned, Aug. 27, 1 p.m.
