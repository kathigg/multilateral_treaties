If the revision has taken place before the end of the fifth year from the date
of the coming into force of the present Convention, any Member of the
League of Nations or non-Member State who has not accepted the revised
Convention shall have the right to denounce the present Convention,
without regard to the period of five years provided for in paragraph 4 of
Article 18. Such denunciation shall take effect on the date on which the
revised Convention comes into force.
If the revision has taken place in the course of the fifth year from the date of
the coming into force of the present Convention, the period of denunciation
referred to in paragraph 1 of Article 18 will be prolonged by one year.
