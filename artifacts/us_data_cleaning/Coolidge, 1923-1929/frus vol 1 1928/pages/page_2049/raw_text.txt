The Ambassador in Italy (Fletcher) to the Secretary of State
ROME, July 27, 1928.
[Received August 9.]
No. 1798
SIR: Referring to the Department’s confidential instruction No. 1017 of June
21st last regarding the proposed new reconstruction loan to Austria,58 I
have the honor to report that in the course of a conversation at the Foreign
Office to-day with Undersecretary Grandi, he informed me that there had
been no change in the Italian Government’s attitude with regard to this
matter, as expressed to the Department by the Italian Ambassador.
. . . . . . . . . . . . . .
I have [etc.]
HENRY P. FLETCHER
58 Not printed; see similar instruction to Minister in Austria, supra.
