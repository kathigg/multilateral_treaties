In thanking you for your kind communication, I hasten to inform Your
Excellency that the Council of Ministers, at its meeting yesterday,
unanimously approved the adherence of Bulgaria to the above mentioned
Treaty, in accordance with paragraph 2 of Article 3.
The Bulgarian representative at Washington will shortly receive the
necessary instructions on this subject, and all the formalities, prescribed by
the laws in force in the Kingdom for such cases, will be carried out with the
least possible delay.
I avail myself [etc.]
