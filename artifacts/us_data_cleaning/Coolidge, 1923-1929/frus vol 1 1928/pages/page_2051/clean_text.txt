I told the Minister that, having in mind the likelihood of a definite proposal by Austria as to settlement of the debt, it would be desirable that the Austrian Government submit full data in support of any proposal it might make. I recalled my previous requests for such data, and that the data had not been supplied. I further stated that other Governments in proposing debt settlements had furnished very full data. The Minister said that he had written for data, but that his Government had apparently not understood exactly what we wanted. He asked whether I would send him quite informally a statement on the subject. I undertook to consider the matter. A[RTHUR] N. Y[OUNG]
