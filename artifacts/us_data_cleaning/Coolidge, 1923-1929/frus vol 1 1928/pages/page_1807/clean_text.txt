825.796/25 [Document 641] The Secretary of State to the Pan American Airways, Inc. WASHINGTON, October 13, 1928. SIRS: With reference to your project for supplying air mail service from the United States to the southernmost part of Chile, concerning which the Department informed the American Embassy at Santiago some time ago, I have to inform you that the American Ambassador to Chile has now telegraphed that he would be pleased to receive any pertinent information regarding the Chilean section of the proposed air mail service which would facilitate his obtaining from the Chilean Government more precise indication as to the extent of that Government’s probable cooperation and facilities. In order that the Department may reply by telegraph to the Ambassador, I shall be pleased to receive any information you can give me regarding the latest developments with regard to this project. I am [etc.] For the Secretary of State: FRANCIS WHITE Assistant Secretary
