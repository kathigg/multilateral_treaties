The Secretary of State to the Minister in Paraguay (Kreeck)
WASHINGTON, October 12, 1928—6 p.m.
9. The Department has been informally advised through the Bolivian
Legation that Paraguayan troops are building a fortress 30 kilometers from
the Bolivian fortress Sorpresa, located 1 kilometer from Chamar Lagoon.
At the northwest of Arce and 40 kilometers from the said point they are also
building another fortress called Boqueron. The Legation calls attention to
the seriousness of the situation which may arise through construction of
these fortresses so close to those of Sorpresa and Arce. Report by telegraph
any information in your possession regarding matter without discussing it
with any officials.
KELLOGG
