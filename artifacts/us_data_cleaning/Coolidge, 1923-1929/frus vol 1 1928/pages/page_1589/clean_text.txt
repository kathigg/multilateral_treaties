714.1515/549a: Telegram [Document 501] The Secretary of State to the Minister in Honduras (Summerlin) WASHINGTON, February 11, 1928—7 p.m. 13. The Guatemalan Minister informs the Department that his Government is again protesting to the Government of Honduras against violations of the status quo in the disputed territory, and particularly against the construction of a railway line between Cacao and Chachagualillo. Please investigate and report, ascertaining especially whether any railway construction is being carried on in the disputed territory. KELLOGG
