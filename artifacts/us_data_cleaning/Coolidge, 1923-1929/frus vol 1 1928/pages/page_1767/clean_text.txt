810.79611 Pan American Airways, Inc./136: Telegram [Document 615] The Secretary of State to the Vice Consul at Nassau (Points) WASHINGTON, October 1, 1928—6 p.m. Pan American Airways which has been awarded contract by Post Office Department for carrying mail from Key West to Panama Canal Zone and from Key West to Porto Rico is contemplating bidding for contract between Miami and Nassau. Mr. John A. Hambleton, Vice President of the Company, is in Nassau in connection with this matter. Please render him all proper assistance. KELLOGG
