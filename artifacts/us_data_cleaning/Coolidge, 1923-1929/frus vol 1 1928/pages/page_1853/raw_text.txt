The Ambassador in Argentina (Bliss) to the Secretary of State
BUENOS AIRES, September 28, 1928—6 p.m. 
[Received 8 p.m.]
64. Department’s 38, September 26, 3 p.m. Lawyer who holds O’Neil’s
power of attorney saw Director of Posts and Telegraphs this morning and
was advised that no contracts could be consummated in short time
remaining of present administration. He advised waiting till matter could be
taken up with new government. O’Neil’s lawyer counsels deferring further
action until appointment of new Director of Posts and Telegraphs.
BLISS
