711.3412 Anti-War/1: Telegram [Document 441] The Minister in Bolivia (Kaufman) to the Secretary of State LA PAZ, September 10, 1928—4 p.m. [Received 4:05 p.m.] 41. The Bolivian Government through the Minister of Finance has requested me to ask the Department to use its best course to obtain the adhesion of Paraguay to the multilateral treaty for the renunciation of war,53 since the Bolivian Government thinks that such action would facilitate the settlement of Bolivian-Paraguayan boundary question. KAUFMAN 53 See pp. 1 [Pg. 1 is part of Doc. 1] ff.
