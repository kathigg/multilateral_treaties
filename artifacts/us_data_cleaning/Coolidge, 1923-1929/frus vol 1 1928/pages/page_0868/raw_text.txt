The Italian Delegation proposes that this article should be drafted as
follows:
“In time of war the application of the present Convention shall be
suspended until the restoration of peace as regards belligerents
and as regards nonbelligerents upon their giving due notice to the
other High Contracting Parties, in conformity with an undertaking
to this effect.”
