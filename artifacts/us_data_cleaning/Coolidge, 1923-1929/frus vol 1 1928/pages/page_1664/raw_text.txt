The Minister in Honduras (Summerlin) to the Secretary of State
TEGUCIGALPA, June 6, 1928—5 p.m. 
[Received 9:40 p.m.]
71. Your telegram number 44, June 4, 4 p.m.26 President Paz stated to me
this afternoon that he has instructed the Minister for Foreign Affairs to
accept your proposal.
Repeated to Guatemala.
SUMMERLIN
26 See footnote 23, p. 746 [Pg. 746 includes portions of Doc. 556 and Doc. 557].
