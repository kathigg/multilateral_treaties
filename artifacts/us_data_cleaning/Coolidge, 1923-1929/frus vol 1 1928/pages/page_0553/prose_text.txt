[Document 142] The Costa Rican Chargé (González) to the Secretary of State [Translation] WASHINGTON, August 30, 1928. MR. SECRETARY: I have the honor to inform Your Excellency that I have been informed by my Government that, in response to the kind invitation received through the Chargé d’Affaires ad interim of the United States of America at San José, the Government has declared that it adheres to the Kellogg pact which will, in due course, be referred to the Congress for its ratification. GUILLERMO E. GONZÁLEZ
