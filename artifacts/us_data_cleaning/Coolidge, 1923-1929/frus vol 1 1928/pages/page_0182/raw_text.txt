Apr. 30 From the Italian Embassy 
Observation that, while Senate bill provides that U. S.
courts shall be open to seamen for claims concerning wages
earned upon American or foreign vessels, article 11 of the
consular convention between Italy and United States
reserves such questions to Italian consular officials.

From the Danish Minister 
Opinion that extension of jurisdiction contemplated in the
proposed bill would conflict not only with Danish law but
also with generally accepted principles of international law;
desire that this view be presented to the proper Committees,
in anticipation that bill will come up at December session
of Congress.
