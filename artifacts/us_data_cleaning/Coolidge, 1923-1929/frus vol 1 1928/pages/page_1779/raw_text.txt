810.79611 Pan American Airways, Inc./172: Telegram
[Document 624]
The Secretary of State to the Ambassador in Chile (Culbertson)
WASHINGTON, December 11, 1928—2 p.m.
89. The Department is informed that Pan American Airways, Inc. and W. R.
Grace & Company are asking the Chilean Government for a concession for
commercial air mail service from Arica south in the name of the Chilean
Airways Corporation, an American company, and are submitting definite
written proposition.
KELLOGG
