810.79611 Pan American Airways, Inc./85: Telegram
[Document 602]
The Minister in the Dominican Republic (Young) to the Secretary
of State
SANTO DOMINGO, June 29, 1928—10 a.m. 
[Received 1:25 p.m.]
39. Department’s telegram 12, June 28, 1 p.m. Dominican authorities grant
permission for initial flight and are favorably disposed towards
establishment regular service but suggest formal arrangements for latter
since landing facilities apparently necessary.
YOUNG
