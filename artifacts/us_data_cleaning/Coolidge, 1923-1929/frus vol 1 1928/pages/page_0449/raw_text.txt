711.9412Anti-War/48: Telegram
[Document 94]
The Chargé in Japan (Neville) to the Secretary of State
TOKYO, July 18, 1928—6 p.m. 
[Received July 18—8:48 a.m.]
86. My telegram No. 83, July 9, 2 p.m.99 Foreign Minister told me today
that the question of wording is still causing some difficulty in the
Government. He said that this is the only reason for Japanese Parliament’s
delay in answering, but he hoped to be able to reply favorably in a few
days.
NEVILLE
99 Not printed.
