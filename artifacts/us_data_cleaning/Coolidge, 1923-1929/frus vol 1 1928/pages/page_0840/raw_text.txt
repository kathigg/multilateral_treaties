500.A16/70: Telegram
[Document 259]
The Minister in Switzerland (Wilson) to the Secretary of State
BERNE, October 2, 1928—8 p.m. 
[Received October 2—1:45 p.m.]
92. Next session of private manufacture conference called for December
5th. If I am to represent Department again it would be of great help in
preparing myself to receive as soon as possible detailed comment from the
Department on stand taken by delegation during the last session (see
despatch No. 584, September 10).
WILSON
