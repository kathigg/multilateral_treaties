717.2114/70: Telegram
[Document 487]
The Secretary of State to the Minister in Nicaragua (Eberhardt)
WASHINGTON, September 15, 1928—1 p.m.
180. Your 342, September 14, 9 a.m. Has text of treaty been published in
Managua? If so the issuance of a statement by the Department would be
greatly facilitated. It will be difficult for the Department to make a
statement on this subject to the press without giving the general terms of the
treaty.
KELLOGG
