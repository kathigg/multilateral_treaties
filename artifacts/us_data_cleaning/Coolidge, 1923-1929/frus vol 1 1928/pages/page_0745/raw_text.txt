500.A15/834: Telegram

The Secretary of State to the Minister in Switzerland (Wilson)
WASHINGTON, December 17, 1928—7 p.m.
109. Your 123, December 17, noon. You can tell Drummond that this
Government is prepared to send delegates at any time. The dates mentioned
are satisfactory but we should also be satisfied with an earlier date.
KELLOGG
