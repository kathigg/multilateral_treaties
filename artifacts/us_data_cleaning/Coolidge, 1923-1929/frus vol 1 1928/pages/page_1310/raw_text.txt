ARTICLE XXXVII
Any contracting state may denounce this convention at any time by
transmitting notification thereof to the Cuban Government, which shall
communicate it to the other states parties to this convention. Such
denunciation shall not take effect until six months after notification thereof
to the Cuban Government, and shall take effect only with respect to the
state making the denunciation.
