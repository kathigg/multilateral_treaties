On the other side, the Undersecretary of the Treasury, Mr. Ogden S. Mills, has informed me, by letter of December 2nd, 1927, that the seized property, belonging to the Austrian Federal Government and held under Trust No. 6392 and 2056 amounted to $195,000.– and $1, 194,000.– or a total sum of $1,389,000. The Austrian Government are therefore of the opinion that the aforementioned trusts should seem sufficient to cover the maximum possible awards against Austria and that the release of Austrian property could be entered in a short time.
