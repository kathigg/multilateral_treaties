500.A15/691: Telegram
[Document 218]
The Chief of the American Representation on the Preparatory
Commission (Gibson) to the Secretary of State
GENEVA, March 24, 1928—12 p.m. 
[Received March 25—5:30 a.m.]
24. My 20, March 24, 4 p.m. Cushendun’s letter handed me during final
afternoon session of Commission and came as complete surprise.
Cushendun and Admiral Kelly left immediately afterwards for London, so I
had no opportunity to discuss proposals or learn why they were presented
here.
We have declined to discuss matter with press and have taken the stand that
comment must come from Department.
GIBSON
