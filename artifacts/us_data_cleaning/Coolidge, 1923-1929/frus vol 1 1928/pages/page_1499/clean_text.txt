724.3415/243: Telegram [Document 449] The Minister in Paraguay (Kreeck) to the Secretary of State ASUNCIÓN, December 8, 1928—9 p.m. [Received December 9—12:30 p.m.] 27. Bolivia’s answer to Paraguay’s request for convocation of Gondra Treaty was to give passport to Paraguayan Minister at La Paz. Paraguay will give Bolivian Minister his passport tomorrow. KREECK
