The Secretary of State to the Minister in Honduras (Summerlin)
WASHINGTON, July 27, 1928—noon.
67. Your 89, July 25, 12 noon. Paragraph 3 of the Department’s proposal to
the Governments of Guatemala and Honduras that the boundary dispute be
submitted to the International Central American Tribunal contemplated that
the existing Mixed Commission now in recess should reconvene for the
purpose of drawing up and signing the necessary arbitral protocol.
The Department is therefore at a loss to understand the action of the
Government of Honduras in completely dissolving its boundary
commission and will appreciate a report from you as to the motives which
impelled it to take such action.
KELLOGG
