711.5812Anti-War/9: Telegram
[Document 149]
The Minister in Sweden (Harrison) to the Secretary of State
STOCKHOLM, September 1, 1928—5 p.m. 
[Received September 1—3:00 p.m.]
17. Department’s circular of August 16, 11 p.m. In a note dated today
Minister for Foreign Affairs acknowledged the receipt of my note of August
27, containing the text of the Multilateral Anti-War Treaty and replies as
follows:
“Animated by the same desire to ensure the maintenance of peace
which inspired the signatory powers, the Royal Government
proposes to ask the Riksdag as soon as possible to give its consent
to Sweden’s adherence to the [above-mentioned] treaty.”
A statement to this effect will be given out tonight and full text will be
released on September 3.
HARRISON
