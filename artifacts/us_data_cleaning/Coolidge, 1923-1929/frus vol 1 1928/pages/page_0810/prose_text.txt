A.—Arms, Ammunition and Implements exclusively designed and intended for land, sea or aerial warfare which are or shall be comprised in the armament of the armed forces of any State, or which, if they have been but are no longer comprised in such armament, are capable of military to the exclusion of any other use, except such arms, ammunition and implements which, though included in the above definition, are covered by other Categories. Such arms, ammunition and implements are comprised in the following twelve headings: 1. Rifles, muskets, carbines. 2. (a) Machine-guns, automatic rifles and machine pistols of all calibres; (b) Mountings for machine-guns; (c) Interrupter gears. 3. Projectiles and ammunition for the arms enumerated in Nos. 1 and 2 above. 4. Gun-sighting apparatus including aerial gun-sights and bombsights, and fire-control apparatus. 5.
