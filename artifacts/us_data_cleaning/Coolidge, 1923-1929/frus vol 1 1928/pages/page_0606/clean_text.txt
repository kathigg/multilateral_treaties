711.1412Anti-War/1: Telegram [Document 165] The Chargé in Guatemala (Hawks) to the Secretary of State GUATEMALA, September 7, 1928—10 a.m. [Received 1:10 p.m.] 109. Department’s circular of August 16, midnight. Note received from the Foreign Office62 states in part that the Government of Guatemala “accepts in principle all the bases of the Anti-War Treaty to which it will adhere opportunely in conformity with the conditions of the Pact itself.” HAWKS 62 Department of State, Treaty for the Renunciation of War, p. 183.
