811.79620 Boeing Airplane Co./19: Telegram
[Document 666]
The Acting Secretary of State to the Ambassador in Argentina
(Bliss)
WASHINGTON, February 10, 1928—2 p.m.
8. Your 10, February 9, 6 p.m. (1) Yes, if invited to do so. (2) Date of arrival
will be communicated to you by Embassy at Rio de Janeiro. (3) He plans to
fly from Rio de Janeiro to Buenos Aires and thence to Santiago in order to
demonstrate capabilities of machines.
OLDS
