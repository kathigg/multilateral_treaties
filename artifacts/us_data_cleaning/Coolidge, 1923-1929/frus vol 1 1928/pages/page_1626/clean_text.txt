714.1515/596: Telegram [Document 532] The Acting Secretary of State to the Representative of the United States on the Guatemalan-Honduran Boundary Commission (Davis) WASHINGTON, March 29, 1928—11 a.m. 11. The Department is informing Governments of Guatemala and Honduras that you plan to arrive at Puerto Cortes on April 4, and is suggesting that the first meeting of the Commission take place at Cuyamel on April 6.
