820.7961 Dept. of Commerce Flight/22: Telegram [Document 655] The Secretary of State to the Ambassador in Chile (Collier) [Paraphrase] WASHINGTON, April 16, 1928—4 p.m. 28. Your telegram No. 50, April 11, 2 p.m. The Department prefers that you avoid any discussion of this subject because it does not desire to indicate even indirectly what this Government’s policy might be in case of eventualities to which you allude. This should not prevent you, however, from rendering assistance to the representatives of American companies to obtain a fair consideration of their propositions. KELLOGG
