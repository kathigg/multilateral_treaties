810.796/4: Telegram [Document 633] The Secretary of State to the Ambassador in Peru (Poindexter) WASHINGTON, January 25, 1928—7 p.m. 6. Your 4, January 21, 1 p.m. 1. The phrase “foreign interests” is intended to cover all interests in Peru not American or Peruvian. 2. The American interests referred to in the Department’s 4, January 19, 3 p.m., are the Keystone and Huff-Daland Dusters, who are connected with Hayden Stone (Financial) and Pan American Airways, and possibly eventually others. 3. Give all possible and proper support to Woolman. KELLOGG
