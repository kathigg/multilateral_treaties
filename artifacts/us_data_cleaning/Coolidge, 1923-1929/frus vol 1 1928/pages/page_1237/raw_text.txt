ARTICLE II
Topic 1. Consideration of the results of the Commission of Jurists
which assembled at Rio de Janeiro.
Topic 2. In view of the fact that the codification of international
law has been entrusted to the Commission of Jurists which
assembled at Rio de Janeiro, the commission has been
recommended to give preferential attention to the study of
“Methods for the pacific settlement of international disputes”; but
if the commission should not have time to dispatch this part of its
work, this topic will be considered included in the program and
submitted to the consideration of the Sixth Conference.
