863.51 Relief Credits/61: Telegram [Document 747] The Ambassador in Great Britain (Houghton) to the Secretary of State LONDON, March 13, 1928—4 p.m. [Received March 13—12:36 p.m.] 49. My 48, March 13, 1 p.m. Am confidentially informed, by reliable authority speaking unofficially, meeting of March 30 will accept general terms of last Austrian offer for settlement of European relief debts. HOUGHTON
