810.79611 Pan American Airways, Inc./86: Telegram
[Document 603]
The Chargé in Haiti (Gross) to the Secretary of State
PORT AU PRINCE, June 29, 1928—1 p.m. 
[Received 2:45 p.m.]
69. Department’s 33, June 28, 1 p.m.65 Haitian Government authorizes ship
of the Pan American Airways to make contemplated flight. Permission also
granted to use local Marine Corps’ landing field for this flight. The question
of the Government granting permission for establishment of a regular
service by this company will be considered but anticipate no difficulty in
this regard. See my despatch No. 1050, August 8, 1927, High
Commissioner’s Series.66 Please request pilot to telegraph ahead day and
hour of arrival here.
