711.0012Anti-War/281: Telegram
[Document 130]
The Acting Secretary of State to the Ambassador in France
(Herrick)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, August 18, 1928—4 p.m.
283. Your No. 248, August 18, 4 p.m. Department thought that France
would not have any objection to Morocco’s adherence, but instead would
welcome it. As it appears that objection does exist, the Department does not
at this time desire to push matter further. You may so inform the Foreign
Office.
CASTLE
