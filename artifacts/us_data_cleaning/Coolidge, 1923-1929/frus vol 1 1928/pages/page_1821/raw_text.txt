820.7961 Dept. of Commerce Flight/16: Telegram
[Document 652]
The Minister in Uruguay (Grant-Smith) to the Secretary of State
MONTEVIDEO, February 2, 1928—noon.
[Received February 2—11:10 a.m.]
7. Your telegram No. 1 of January 7, 8 p.m.91 The Uruguayan Government
has granted permission flight to visit Uruguay and to pass over its
territories.
It is suggested that the use of the word “pathfinding” might give rise to
misunderstanding.
GRANT-SMITH
91 See footnote 86, p. 811 [Pg. 811 includes portions of Doc. 646 and Doc. 647].
