714.1515/587: Telegram [Document 524] The Minister in Guatemala (Geissler) to the Secretary of State GUATEMALA, March 21, 1928—6 p.m. [Received 11:30 p.m.] 25. The Acting Minister for Foreign Affairs says that Cuyamel would be acceptable and that the Guatemalan Commissioners will meet with the others at such time and place as the Department may suggest. Repeated to Tegucigalpa and Costa Rica. GEISSLER
