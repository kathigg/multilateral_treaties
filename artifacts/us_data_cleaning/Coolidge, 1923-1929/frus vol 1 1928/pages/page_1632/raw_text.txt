The Secretary of State to the Consul at Puerto Cortes (Fox)
WASHINGTON, April 10, 1928—11 a.m.
2. For Minister Davis: Your 1, April 5, 6 p.m. The Department’s telegram
No. 8, of March 23, 6 p.m. modifies its mail instruction No. 1, which was
dispatched before the receipt of the authorization by the two Governments
for you to fix the provisional line in the event the commissioners are unable
to agree.
The Department would view with favor the definitive arbitration of the
dispute by the International Central American Tribunal.
KELLOGG
