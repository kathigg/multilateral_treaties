The Chargé in China (Bell) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, September 6, 1924—noon.
[Received September 6—8:15 a.m.]
325. My 318, September 2, 5 p.m., last paragraph. Naval attaché returned
yesterday from Harbin and Mukden, reports after careful inspection he
could find no evidence of Marshal Chang Tso-lin intending to move in
immediate future. His attitude appeared to be one of watchful waiting.
BELL
