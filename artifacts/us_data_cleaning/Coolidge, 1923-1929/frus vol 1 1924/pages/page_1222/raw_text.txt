The Minister in China (Schurman) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, May 5, 1924.
[Received June 10.]
No. 2242
SIR: I have the honor to transmit herewith enclosed a copy of a note which I
addressed to the Minister for Foreign Affairs on the 3rd instant regarding
the Chinese Eastern Railway pursuant to the instructions contained in your
telegram No. 81 of April 26th, 6 P.M.
I have sent copies of this note to the British, French and Japanese Ministers
for their information.
I have [etc.]
JACOB GOULD SCHURMAN
