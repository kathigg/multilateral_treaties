858.116 M 82/29: Telegram
[Document 194]
The Secretary of State to the Minister in Sweden (Morris)
WASHINGTON, April 7, 1921—6 p.m.
20. Department’s No. 17, April 2, 5 p.m. Your No. 40 April 4, 4 p.m.
Telegraph Swedish Government’s reasons for expelling Mormons. Request
no drastic action be taken until matter can be fully investigated.
HUGHES
