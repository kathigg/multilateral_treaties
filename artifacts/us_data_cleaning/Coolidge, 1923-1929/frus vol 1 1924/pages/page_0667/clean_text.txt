811.2300/48: Telegram [Document 178] The Chargé in Rumania (Dennis) to the Secretary of State BUCHAREST, February 9, 1924—3 p.m. [Received 9:10 p.m.] 7. My telegram no. 5, January 22, 10 a.m.16 Note received today from the Roumanian Government grants all permissions requested but makes no mention of aerial photography.17 Foreign Office desires details of the expedition in due time. DENNIS 16 Not printed. 17 On June 14, 3 p.m., the Minister telegraphed that permission for aerial photography was included in the note (file no. 811.2300/135).
