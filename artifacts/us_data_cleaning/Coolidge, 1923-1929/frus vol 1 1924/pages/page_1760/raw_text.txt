195/609: Telegram
[Document 690]
The Chargé in France (Whitehouse) to the Secretary of State
PARIS, August 23, 1924—noon.
[Received August 23—8:53 a.m.]
379. My 367, August 13, 4 p.m.41 Foreign Office has just informed me
officially that pending recognition by the French Government of the
American Bureau of Shipping, American vessels calling at French ports will
in no way be interfered with.
WHITEHOUSE
41 Not printed.
