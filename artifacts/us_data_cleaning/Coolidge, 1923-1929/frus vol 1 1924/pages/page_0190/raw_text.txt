To the Minister in Ecuador (tel.)
Information received from the Mercantile Bank that
the President, despite his assurances, has definitely
instructed his advisers not to have the bill passed
extending the tax, as he fears the responsibility of
obligating the Government. Instructions to remind the
President of his assurances and to state that the
Department expects he will not delay in having the
matter arranged satisfactorily.
