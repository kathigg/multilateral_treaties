[Document 327]
The Secretary of State to the Chargé in Japan (Caffery)
WASHINGTON, September 12, 1924—2 p.m.
154. Your 227, September 10, 5 p.m. You may orally communicate to Baron
Shidehara the essential portion of the first paragraph of the Department’s
151, September 10, 4 p.m., down to and including “developments in
China.”
You may repeat to Peking for its information the above mentioned first
paragraph of the Department’s 151.
HUGHES
