[Document 355]
The Acting Secretary of State to the Chargé in China (Mayer)
WASHINGTON, November 5, 1924—6 p.m.
269. Tokyo Embassy telegraphs November 518 that Japanese Consul at
Tsingtau advises his Government our Consul there has agreed with Japanese
and British colleagues to advise Wu Pei-fu not to land in the event of his
arrival there.
 
Please advise Consul immediately that the Department could not approve
such intervention in Chinese internal affairs.
Repeat to Tokyo as Department’s No. 180.
GREW
18 Telegram not printed.
