511.3 B 1/184: Telegram
[Document 28]
The Minister in Switzerland (Gibson) to the Secretary of State
[Paraphrase]
BERNE, July 14, 1924—11 a.m.
[Received 2:55 p.m.]
58. On July 12 the Temporary Mixed Commission adjourned. The draft
convention which the Commission drew up will be submitted in September
to the Assembly of the League of Nations. The draft convention as
approved by the Assembly will be submitted later to an international
conference for negotiation. An invitation will be given to the interested
governments to attend this conference.
The draft as approved by the Commission seems to be in harmony with the
Department’s views as indicated by its instructions.
GIBSON
