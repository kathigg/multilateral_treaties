By “present importation” is understood the importation during the 12 months immediately preceding the date the Contracting Party ratifies this Convention* CHAPTER III—MEDICINAL OPIUM, MORPHINE, COCAINE, ETC. Definitions.—By “medicinal opium” is understood: Raw opium which has been heated to 60° Centigrade and contains not less than 10 per cent, of morphine, whether or not it be powdered or granulated or mixed with indifferent materials.

By “morphine” is understood: The principal alkaloid of opium having the chemical formula C17H19NO3. By “cocaine” is understood: The principal alkaloid of the loavos of Erythroxylon Coca, having the formula C 17 H 21 NO 4. The methylbenzoyl derivatives of ecgonine (C9H15O3N) of the chemical formula C17H21O4N, whether occurring naturally or prepared synthetically; and all other derivatives of ecgonine, whether occurring naturally or prepared synthetically, which possess the property of creating addiction by their use.
