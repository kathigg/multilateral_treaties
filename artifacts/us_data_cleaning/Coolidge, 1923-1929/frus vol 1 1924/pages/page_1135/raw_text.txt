the other powers signatory of the 1901 and 1904 protocols must
reserve full liberty of action for their Governments in respect to
the effect of such renunciation upon the rights, privileges and
mutual obligations which accrue to and devolve upon all the
signatories of these collective agreements.

4. I think it is the expectation of all the diplomatic representatives that this

the respective Governments cabled their replies next week the matter could
be settled before I left Peking for America.
