800.51 W 89 Latvia/31: Telegram [Document 79] The Chargé in Estonia, Latvia, and Lithuania (White) to the Secretary of State [Paraphrase] RIGA, November 20, 1924—noon. [Received 1:24 p.m.] 183. Minister of Finance has informed me that the Cabinet of Ministers has decided to inaugurate funding negotiations through the Latvian consul at New York.6 WHITE 6 Minister Coleman informed the Department by despatch of Jan. 28, 1925, that Mr. Ringold Kalning, formerly Latvian Minister of Finance, would proceed to the United States as Commissioner to assist the consul in the funding negotiations (file no. 800.51 W 89 Latvia/36).
