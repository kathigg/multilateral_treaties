839.51/2388: Telegram [Document 598] The Secretary of State to the Commissioner in the Dominican Republic (Welles) WASHINGTON, January 12, 1924—6 p.m. 1. With reference to the telegram number 1, January 7, noon, from American Minister, Santo Domingo, the Department desires your views regarding the possible effect of bond issue on political situation in view of approaching elections. This of course does not refer to obligations of Power Company contract. Reply by cable. HUGHES
