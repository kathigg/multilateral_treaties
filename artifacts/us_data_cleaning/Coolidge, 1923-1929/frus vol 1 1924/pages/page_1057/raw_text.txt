The British Ambassador (Howard) to the Secretary of State
WASHINGTON, October 25, 1924.
No. 1006
SIR: I have the honour to inform you that His Majesty’s Government have
learnt that the Canton Government are again threatening to seize the
Customs House and to declare Canton a free port.
I am directed by my Government to express the earnest hope that you will
authorize the United States Representative at Peking to concert with His
Majesty’s Representative in arranging for a renewal of the naval
demonstration.
I have [etc.]
ESME HOWARD
