358.116 M 82/31: Telegram [Document 198] The Secretary of State to the Minister in Sweden (Morris) WASHINGTON, April 9, 1921—6 p.m. 21. Department’s No. 20, April 7, 6 p.m. Inform Swedish Government Mormon Church invites any investigation Swedish Government may desire to make and will be glad to pay expenses both to and from Utah of any investigator the Swedish Government may desire to send. HUGHES
