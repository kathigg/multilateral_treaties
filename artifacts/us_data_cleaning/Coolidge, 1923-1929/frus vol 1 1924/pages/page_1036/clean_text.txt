893.00/5805: Telegram [Document 371] The Chargé in China (Mayer) to the Secretary of State PEKING, December 2, 1924—noon. [Received December 2—3:33 a.m.] 466. My number 465, December 1, 4 p.m. Chang Tso-lin without warning left Peking December 2, 4 a.m., I am informed, for Tientsin taking headquarters staff and bodyguard. Sun Fo and other Kuomintang leaders in Peking. MAYER
