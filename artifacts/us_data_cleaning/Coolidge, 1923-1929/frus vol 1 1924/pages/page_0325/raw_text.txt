The Minister in Switzerland (Gibson) to the Secretary of State
BERNE, October 14, 1924.
[Received October 28.]
No. 170
L.N. No. 597
SIR: I have the honor to refer to my despatch No. 153, of October 2, 1924
(L.N. No. 593),49 enclosing a document (No. A.115.1924.IX) issued by the
Secretariat of the League of Nations and embodying a resolution adopted by
the Assembly and Council, requesting the interested governments to inform
the Secretary General, before the Council meets in December 1924,
whether they are prepared to take part in a conference to be convened in
April or May 1925 for the purpose of discussing the Draft Convention for
the Control of the International Traffic in Arms, Munitions and Implements
of War.
I have now received a letter, dated October 9, 1924, from the Secretary
General of the League, enclosing a communication (C.L.140.1924.IX)
addressed to the Secretary of State in the sense of the above mentioned
resolution. This communication, as well as a copy of Sir Eric Drummond’s
letter to me, is transmitted herewith.
. . . . . . . . . . . . . .
I have [etc.]
HUGH GIBSON
