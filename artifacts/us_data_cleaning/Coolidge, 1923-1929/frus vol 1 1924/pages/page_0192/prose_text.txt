EGYPT REFUSAL BY THE UNITED STATES TO ASSENT TO THE COLLECTION OF THE GAFFIR TAX FROM AMERICAN NATIONALS Date and Number Subject Page 1923 Nov. (415) From the Minister in Egypt Note on the Gaffir tax prepared by the Egyptian Ministry of Finance (text printed) maintaining that abolition of martial law has no effect upon the legality of the tax which continues to be due from all inhabitants of the territory.
