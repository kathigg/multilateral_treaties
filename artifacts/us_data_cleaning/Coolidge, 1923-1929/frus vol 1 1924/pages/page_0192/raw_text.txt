To the Minister in Egypt
Refusal to admit the application of the Gaffir tax to
American nationals, since U. S. consent to its
collection has never been requested and in view of
the fact that other capitulatory powers in Egypt
have not given their consent to the collection of the
tax from their nationals.

Instructions to inform the Egyptian Government
