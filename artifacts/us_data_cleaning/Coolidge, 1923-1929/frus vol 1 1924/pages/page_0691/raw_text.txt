354.116 M 81/1: Circular telegram
[Document 193]
The Secretary of State to the Chargé in Great Britain (Wright)30
WASHINGTON, April 5, 1921—1 p.m.
Department advised that British diplomatic and consular officers here
refuse visas to American Mormon missionaries going to South Africa.
Investigate and report.
Repeat mutatis mutandis to The Hague, Copenhagen, Christiania, and
Berne.
HUGHES
30 See last paragraph for instructions to repeat to The Hague, Copenhagen,
Christiania, and Berne.
