The Secretary of State to the Minister in China (Schurmcm)
WASHINGTON, March 18, 1924—5 p.m.
55. Your No. 77 March 13, 4 p.m. and Evans’ telegram to Buforcom March
15.32 Pending the receipt of further instructions, you should not take such a
part in conference with your colleagues as to indicate or commit in any way
the position of this Government with respect to the proposal for a
preliminary conference.
HUGHES
32 Telegram from Mr. Arthur H. Evans, assistant commercial attaché at
Peking, to the Bureau of Foreign and Domestic Commerce; not printed.
