The Secretary of State to the Norwegian Minister (Bryn)
WASHINGTON, March 19, 1924.
SIR: I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your note dated February
20, 1924, with respect to certain provisions of the new immigration bill (H.
R. 6540) introduced in the House of Representatives on February 1, 1924.
You may be assured that all questions relating to the appropriate recognition
of the treaty provisions to which you refer are having proper consideration.
Accept [etc.]
CHARLES E. HUGHES
