839.00/2814: Telegram [Document 585] The Secretary of State to the Commissioner in the Dominican Republic (Welles) WASHINGTON, April 7, 1924—3 p.m. 13. Your 23, March 25, 9 a.m. Department informed by Secretary of the Navy that he has decided to keep General Lee in his office as Military Governor until after July 1, and has therefore directed that the order for his relief in April be revoked. HUGHES
