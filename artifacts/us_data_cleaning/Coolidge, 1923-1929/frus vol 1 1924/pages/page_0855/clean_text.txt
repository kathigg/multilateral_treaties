The Department’s feeling about this matter is so strong that it would rather recall the Naval Mission than assume the responsibility for the naval program that the Mission has proposed. Explain the Department’s position to Admiral Vogelgesang and request him to take the first opportunity to revise the naval program on the principle of no new construction or replacement of tonnage in any category that will be greater than the maximum which exists at present in any one of the three states, Argentina, Brazil, or Chile. Ambassador Morgan has been made fully aware of the Department’s position in the matter, and immediately upon the Ambassador’s return to Rio de Janeiro he will discuss it with Admiral Vogelgesang.
