825.01/21 [Document 305] The Secretary of State to the Chilean Ambassador (Mathieu) WASHINGTON, October 9, 1924. EXCELLENCY: While it is not the intention of this Government to discontinue dealing with you as Ambassador of Chile, I desire to state in order to avoid misapprehension that it should be understood that this Government’s action in so doing is not to be construed as a recognition of the régime now functioning in Chile as other than the de facto authorities in control of the administration of Chile. Accept [etc.] CHARLES E. HUGHES
