511.4 A 2/110: Telegram [Document 51] The Secretary General of the League of Nations (Drummond) to the Secretary of State GENEVA [undated]. [Received October 15, 1924—12:36 p.m.] Reference my letter C. L. 5, 14th January, 1924. Would remind you no reply yet received. Answer would facilitate preliminary arrangements conference. DRUMMOND
