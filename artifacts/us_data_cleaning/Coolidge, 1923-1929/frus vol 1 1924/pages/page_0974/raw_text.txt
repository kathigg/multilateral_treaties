The Chargé in China (Bell) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, September 14, 1924—7 p.m.
[Received September 14—1:30 p.m.]
Consul Mukden reports by telegraph heavy troop movements from Mukden
southward in last three days. I have no information that they have gone
further south than Great Wall.
BELL
