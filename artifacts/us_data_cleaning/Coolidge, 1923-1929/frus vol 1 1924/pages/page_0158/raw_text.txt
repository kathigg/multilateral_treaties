From the British Ambassador
Readiness to cooperate with the United States in
approaching the French Government with a view to
concluding a gentleman’s agreement, as suggested by
the U. S. Chargé at Paris, prohibiting the export of all
aircraft to China during the present revolutionary
disturbances there. Suggestion that other governments,
such as Japan and Italy, be included.
