875.00/122: Telegram
[Document 245]
The Minister in Albania (Grant-Smith) to the Secretary of State
TIRANA, May 18, 1924 [—12 noon].
[Received 5:20 p.m.]
43. My telegram number 40 of May 4, noon. …
Arms are being distributed here to reserves and others. Movement on
Scutari planned for this week. A portion of Tirana garrison of doubtful
loyalty. Should south join rebels, which seems probable, telegraph lines
would be cut. I recommend that a destroyer be sent to Durazzo for
communication purposes. Copy to European Information Center.
GRANT-SMITH
