The Swedish Government, at this juncture, permits itself to call your
attention to the fact that an additional conference, summoned for the
purpose of studying the question, is about to meet in Geneva next year. And
it wonders, inasmuch as the prohibition of export of arms and munitions to
China does not seem on the whole to have had the desired efficacy up to the
present, whether it would not be better to submit the question in hand for
study by that conference.
Pray accept [etc.]
ÖSTEN UNDEN
