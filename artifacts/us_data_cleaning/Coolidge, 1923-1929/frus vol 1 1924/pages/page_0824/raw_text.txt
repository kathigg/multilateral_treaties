875.01/243: Telegram
[Document 256]
The Secretary of State to the Minister in Albania (Grant-Smith)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, June 24, 1924—1 p.m.
39. Legation’s 56 of June 19 and 58 of June 21. Unless the head of the State
has been changed, the question of recognition does not seem to arise. There
is no objection to your continuing to carry on with the present Government
the relations which you had with the preceding one if in your opinion it is
properly constituted, stable, and in control of the country. You are
authorized at your discretion to continue such relations. Department desires
telegraphic report of your action.
HUGHES
