The Secretary of State to the Minister in Switzerland (Gibson)
WASHINGTON, October 9, 1924—5 p.m.
75. Your despatch 142, September 24.
You may inform the Secretary General of the League of Nations that the
Department of State will be willing for all practical purposes to consider the
identity certificates for Armenian refugees as appropriate documents in lieu
of passports.
HUGHES
