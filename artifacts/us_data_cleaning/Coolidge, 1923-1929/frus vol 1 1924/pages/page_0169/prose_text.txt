Date and Number Subject Page 1924 Jan. From the Minister in China (tel.) Information that a Presidential mandate, issued December 27, 1923, authorizes the cancelation of the mandate ordering the trial and punishment of Chang Ching-yao for the murder of the Rev. William A. Reimert. Request for authority to lodge a strong protest against it as a violation of the assurance given in the Chinese Foreign Office note of October 7, 1920, and as a discourtesy to the United States.
