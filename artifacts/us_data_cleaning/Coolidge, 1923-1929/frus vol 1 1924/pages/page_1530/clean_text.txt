839.00/2844: Telegram [Document 594] The Minister in the Dominican Republic (Russell) to the Secretary of State SANTO DOMINGO, July 3, 1924—5 p.m. [Received July 4—2:35 p.m.] 30. Electoral College has elected Horatio Vasquez and Doctor Velasquez President and Vice President respectively. Proclamation will be issued July 4th and the inauguration will be set for July 12. RUSSELL
