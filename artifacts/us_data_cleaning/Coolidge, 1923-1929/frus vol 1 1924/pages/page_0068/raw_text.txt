To the Minister in Denmark (tel.)
Instructions to inquire whether Denmark would grant
permission for a U. S. Army officer to investigate
conditions in Iceland in order to secure data for a
projected round-the-world flight of U. S. Army
airplanes in the spring of 1924, the itinerary to include
Iceland.

July 18
To the Chargé in Japan
Instructions to request permission for two U. S. Army
officers, detailed to make a pathfinding expedition, to
investigate conditions and secure data for a projected
round-the-world flight of U. S. Army airplanes in the
spring of 1924.
(Sent also to representatives in France, Great Britain,
and Italy.)
