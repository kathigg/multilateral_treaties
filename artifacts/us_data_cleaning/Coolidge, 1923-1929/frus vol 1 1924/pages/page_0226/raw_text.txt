12. I hope that a resolution will be introduced in Congress authorizing the
use of the appropriation made by the 67th Congress of $6,500,000 for
increasing the elevation of the turret guns of 13 United States capital ships
and I further hope that that resolution will receive the support of the State
Department. I believe that our national interests require a navy second to no
other and that no steps should ever be taken that will bind us either directly
or by implication to any line of conduct that hampers development of such a
Navy.
EDWIN DENBY
