358.116 M 82/38: Telegram
[Document 209]
The Minister in Sweden (Bliss) to the Secretary of State
STOCKHOLM, June 7, 1924—1 p.m.
[Received June 7—11:50 a.m.]
19. With reference to my despatch number 115, January 25,46 Foreign
Office informs me that hereafter visas for Mormons will be subject to the
same regulations as applied to all foreigners.
BLISS
46 Not printed.
