811.2300/32: Telegram [Document 171] The Minister in Hungary (Brentano) to the Secretary of State BUDAPEST, January 23, 1924—6 p.m. [Received January 24—12:07 a.m.] 5. Your December 22, 3 p.m.1 and January 18, 7 p.m.” Foreign Office writes that the Government authorizes the aviators to fly over and land in Hungary and that it will inform me later regarding landing points and restrictions against photography.
