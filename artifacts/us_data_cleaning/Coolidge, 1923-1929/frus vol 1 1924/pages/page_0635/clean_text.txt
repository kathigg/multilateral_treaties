811.2300/3 [Document 153] The Chargé in France (Whitehouse) to the Secretary of State PARIS, September 19, 1923. [Received October 2.] No. 3490 SIR: With reference to the Department’s instruction of July 18, 1923, File No. 811.2300/–,88 I have the honor to report that by a note dated September 15, 1923, the French Foreign Office informs me that the “Central Aeronautical Service” of the Ministry of Marine, will give our officers all possible information and that such of this information as concerns climatology, meteorology, tides, etc., will be given by Major Lefranc, of the Hydrographic Service.
