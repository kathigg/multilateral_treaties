With respect to Section 24, which provides that the Commissioner General shall prescribe rules and regulations for the enforcement of the provisions of the Act, so far as its administration by Consular Officers is concerned, subject to the approval of the Secretary of State, I desire to refer to my comments with respect to Section 8 (e). For the reasons there stated, I am of the opinion that the rules and regulations, so far as they relate to Consular Officers should be prescribed by the Secretary of State upon the recommendation of the Commissioner General.
