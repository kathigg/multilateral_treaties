The Department feels that the above arrangement offers a fair basis for an
adjustment of the difficulty, giving the Government wide latitude of choice
as to the method to be followed out in acquiring the property, protecting the
interest of the Company and permitting the Government to carry on its
other needed public works. The Department hopes that the Government will
be prepared to accept one of the above proposals or to make immediately a
reasonable counter proposal.
