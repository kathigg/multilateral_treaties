The Lithuanian Chargé (Bizauskas) to the Secretary of State
WASHINGTON, May 1, 1924.
SIR: I have the honor to inform you that I have been telegraphically
instructed by my Government to communicate to you that I am authorized
by my Government to enter into negotiations with the Government of the
United States with regard to the settlement of the indebtedness of Lithuania
to the United States.
I should be very grateful if you will cause this to be communicated to the
World War Foreign Debt Commission.
Please accept [etc.]
K. BIZAUSKAS
