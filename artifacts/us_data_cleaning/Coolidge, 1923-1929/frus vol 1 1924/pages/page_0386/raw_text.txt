511.4 A 2/121b: Telegram
[Document 54]
The Acting Secretary of State to the Minister in Switzerland
(Gibson)
WASHINGTON, October 25, 1924—4 p.m.
84. American Delegation to Opium Conference at Geneva will consist of
Honorable Stephen G. Porter, chairman, Eight Reverend Charles H. Brent,
Assistant Surgeon General Rupert Blue, Mrs. Hamilton Wright, Mr. Edwin
L. Neville, delegates, with five assistants and clerks. Please notify Secretary
General of League of Nations and the appropriate Swiss authorities.
GREW
