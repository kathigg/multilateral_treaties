867.77/3654: Telegram [Document 491] The Consul at Harbin (Hanson) to the Secretary of State HARBIN, September 29, 1924—5 p.m. [Received September 29—10:25 a.m.] American, British, French and Japanese consular seals removed from cabinets in the land department Chinese Eastern Railway this afternoon on the initiative of the four consuls. Informed the Embassy. HANSON
