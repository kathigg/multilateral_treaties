CUBA PASSAGE OF AN AMNESTY BILL BY THE CUBAN CONGRESS Date and Number Subject Page 1924 June (730) From the Ambassador in Cuba Passage of an amnesty bill, May 26, 1924, by both Houses of Congress by large majority; Ambassador’s note to President Zayas, May 30, expressing his views on the subject, previous notes of protest having already been sent. (Footnote: Information that President Zayas signed the bill on June 5.)
