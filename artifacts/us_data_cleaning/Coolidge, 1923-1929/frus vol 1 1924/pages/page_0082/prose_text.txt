BOLIVIA AND PARAGUAY Date and Number Subject Page 1924 July (247) To the Chargé in Paraguay Information, in reply to the Chargé’s report concerning Bolivia’s protest to Paraguay against a Paraguayan grant of land in the Chaco to Canadian Mennonites and Bolivia’s inquiry as to possible U. S. intervention, that the United States would be unwilling to intervene in the boundary dispute unless requested to do so by both countries.
