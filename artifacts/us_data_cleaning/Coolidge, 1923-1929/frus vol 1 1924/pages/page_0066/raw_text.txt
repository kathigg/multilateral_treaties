To the Chairman of the Committee on Immigration
and Naturalization of the House of Representatives
Comments and recommendations concerning the new
immigration bill (H. R. 6540): Approval of restricted
immigration; objections to provisions excluding the
Japanese and to quotas based on 1890 census; opinion
that proposed act will conflict with existing treaties;
hope for some nondiscriminatory basis for restriction;
suggestion of certain amendments; endorsement of
proposal to issue immigration certificates.
