800.51 W 89 Lithuania/26: Telegram
[Document 76]
The Minister in Estonia, Latvia, and Lithuania (Coleman) to the
Secretary of State
RIGA, July 30, 1924—3 p.m.
[Received 3:15 p.m.]
118. In re Lithuanian debt to the United States, Lithuanian Government
inquires whether the United States Government would protest the
ratification by the Lithuanian Assembly of the trade agreement with
Germany which provides for the mutual cancelation of all claims arising out
of the war, the German occupation, reparations and post-bellum supplies,
etc. Agreement now before the Assembly. Prompt reply desired.
COLEMAN
