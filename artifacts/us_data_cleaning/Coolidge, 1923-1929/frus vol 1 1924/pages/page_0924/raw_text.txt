The Secretary of State to the Ambassador in Chile (Collier)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, September 13, 1924—1 p.m.
42. Refer to my September 12, 2 p.m. According to press reports military
junta has accepted Alessandri’s resignation. It is reported from Buenos
Aires that Alessandri has made statement that he was banished and that
there is no constitutional government in Chile.
You are instructed to avoid any formal relations with the new regime until
the situation clears up, but do not make your course conspicuous. Should a
situation arise where it would be impossible to remain noncommittal
telegraph Department at once.
HUGHES
