861.01/764: Telegram
[Document 461]
The Minister in China (Schurman) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, March 17, 1924—1 p.m.
[Received March 17—7:40 a.m.]
81. My 76, March 12, 11 a.m., and 78, March 13, 6 p.m. Cabinet has not yet
adopted Karakhan-Wang agreement. Cabinet intimates in [apparent
omission] that the hitch is over Mongolia but political and personal rivalries
in the Government probably more potent. From best available Soviet
sources it is learned Karakhan has placed time limit on negotiations which
is to expire March 19th. Koo confirms this confidentially to the extent of
saying there was a time limit.
SCHURMAN
