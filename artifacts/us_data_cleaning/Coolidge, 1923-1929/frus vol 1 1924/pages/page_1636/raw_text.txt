The Secretary of State to the Minister in Ecuador (Bading)
WASHINGTON, November 6, 1924.
No. 402
SIR: The receipt is acknowledged of your despatch No. 394 dated September
29, 1924, concerning your conversation with the Minister of Hacienda with
a view to bringing about payments on the Guayaquil and Quito Railway
bonds.
 
The Department approves of your action and desires to commend you for
the energy and effectiveness with which you have handled this matter.
I am [etc.]
For the Secretary of State:
JOSEPH C. GREW
