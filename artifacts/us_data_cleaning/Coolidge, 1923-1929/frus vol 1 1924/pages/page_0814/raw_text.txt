875.00/133: Telegram
[Document 248]
The Ambassador in Italy (Fletcher) to the Secretary of State
ROME, June 2, 1924—8 p.m.
[Received 8:45 p.m.]
104. I am just notified by the Foreign Office that as communication with
Durazzo is interrupted an Italian destroyer is being sent immediately to
Durazzo. In accordance with desire of Minister Tirana I have just relayed
his request for a destroyer to the naval attaché at Athens.
FLETCHER
