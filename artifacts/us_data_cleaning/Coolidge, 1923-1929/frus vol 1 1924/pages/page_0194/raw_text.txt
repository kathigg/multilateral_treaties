Feb. 23
(10)
To the Minister in Egypt (tel.)
Instructions to report whether the Egyptian Government
has recently reached any decision which would affect
the right of U. S. archeological institutions under the
law of 1912, in view of recent inquiries from the
Metropolitan Museum regarding further extension of
their right to excavate and retain onehalf of their
discoveries and the Department’s belief that the present
time might not be opportune for such representations
because of the reported difficulties between Howard
Carter and the Egyptian Government with respect to the
tomb of Tutankhamen.
