The Secretary of State to the Minister in Albania (Grant-Smith)
WASHINGTON, May 20, 1924—5 p.m.
31. Your 43, May 18 and 44, May 19, 3 p.m. Department has informed
Navy of substance of your telegrams and in accordance with your
recommendation has expressed desire that the Commander of United States
Naval Forces in European waters be authorized to despatch a destroyer to
Durazzo upon receipt of a request to that effect from you or the Embassy at
Rome. Embassy at Rome has been advised in the premises by telegraph.
HUGHES
