The Secretary of State to the Chinese Minister (Sze)
WASHINGTON, June 14, 1924.
SIR: I have the honor to enclose herewith a copy of an act of Congress,
approved May 21, 1924, whereby the President is authorized, in his
discretion, to remit to China the balance of the Boxer Indemnity, such
remission to begin as from October 1, 1917, and to be at such times and in
such manner as the President shall deem just, the intent of the Congress, as
stated in the preamble to the Act, being further to develop the educational
and other cultural activities of China.
Accept [etc.]
CHARLES E. HUGHES
[Enclosure]
