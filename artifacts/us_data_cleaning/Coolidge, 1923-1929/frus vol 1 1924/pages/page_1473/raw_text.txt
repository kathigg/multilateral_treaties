The Acting Secretary of State to the Minister in Czechoslovakia
(Einstein)
WASHINGTON, January 12, 1925—2 p.m.
1. Your 35, September 3, 4 p.m.4 Your despatch No. 746, December 9. Does
exchange of notes of December 5 require legislative and presidential
approval and publication? Department wishes to publish in Treaty Series as
soon as all Czechoslovak formalities are completed. Your omission of
“indefinitely” approved.
GREW
4 Not printed.
