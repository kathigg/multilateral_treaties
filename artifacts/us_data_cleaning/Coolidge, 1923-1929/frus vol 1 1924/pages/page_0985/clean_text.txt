893.00/5605: Telegram [Document 339] The Chargé in China (Bell) to the Secretary of State PEKING, October 11, 1924—4 p.m. [Received October 11—6:18 a.m.] 383. Marshal Wu Pei-fu left Peking early this morning to direct operations at the northern front. Colonel Barnard and Captain Woodbridge11 accompanied him as observers at his invitation. British, French and Japanese officers also accompanied Wu in like capacity. BELL 11 U. S. Army officers attached to the Legation in China.
