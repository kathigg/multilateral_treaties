811.2300/2: Telegram
[Document 152]
The Ambassador in Italy (Child) to the Secretary of State
ROME, September 18, 1923—noon.
[Received 3:11 p.m.]
118. Department’s unnumbered instructions July 18.88 Foreign Office
advises Italian Government will be glad to furnish American officer with all
information and data he may require for fulfillment of mission in
connection with preparations for round-the-world flight of Army
aeroplanes.
CHILD
88 See footnote 85, p. 227 [Pg. 227 includes portions of Doc. 148 and Doc. 149].
