To the Chargé in Japan (tel.)
Instructions to express gratitude for informal consent,
and to telegraph when written confirmation is
received. Information that Lieutenant Nutt, advance
officer, has left the Philippines for Tokyo and that the
War Department would be glad if a Japanese officer
would accompany him on pathfinding expedition.
(Footnote: Information that, on January 25, the
Chargé reported he had received written confirmation
of the Japanese consent.)
