The Minister in Ecuador (Bading) to the Secretary of State
QUITO, December 2, 1924—11 a.m.
[Received December 3—9:10 a.m.]
19. Department’s telegram November 26, 6 p.m. From sources believed to
be reliable it is learned that all debts to local banks and vale holders have
been paid except one vale in litigation.
BADING
