822.61334/139: Telegram [Document 644] The Minister in Ecuador (Bading) to the Secretary of State QUITO, October 7, 1924—3 p.m. [Received October 8—9:30 a.m.] 15. Department’s telegram number 17, October 1, 4 p.m. Have had interview with the President, and his answer submitted in writing absolutely ignores promises; merely quotes law of 1921 and states he favors strict compliance therewith. Recommend immediate instructions by telegraph calling for the fulfillment of promises without evasion. BADING
