Furthermore, this Department perceived no objection to the printing of a
statement on each of the two-year notes to the effect that the notes are
“issued with the consent of the Government of the United States of
America, as required by Article III of the treaty between the United States
and the Dominican Republic.” It will be understood by you, however, that
while the fact of this consent may appropriately appear, this letter is not to
be published in any circular or statement to be issued in connection with
these securities.

establishment of a Constitutional Government in the Dominican Republic,
