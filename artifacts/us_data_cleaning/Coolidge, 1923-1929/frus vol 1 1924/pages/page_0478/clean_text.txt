463.11 W 891/28: Telegram [Document 93] The Minister in Austria (Washburn) to the Secretary of State VIENNA, September 5, 1924—1 p.m. [Received 4:23 p.m.] 52. Department’s telegram number 25, May 31, 1 p.m. I spoke to Foreign Office about this matter on Monday last and received satisfactory assurances. Prochnik has now been authorized by cable to sign tripartite agreement in form already agreed upon without any reservations whatever. His instructions dated 3d instant should now be in Washington. WASHBURN
