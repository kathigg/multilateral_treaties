The Chief of the Air Service is desirous of sending Lieutenant Clayton
Bissell over the proposed route of the projected flight through British
Columbia from Victoria to Prince Rupert as soon after January 1, 1924 as
the weather will permit, the purpose of his trip being to make all necessary
arrangements in connection with the flight relative to supplies,
transportation facilities, et cetera.
I should greatly appreciate it if you would be so good as to inform me
whether or not Lieutenant Bissell’s trip would be agreeable to the Dominion
Authorities.
