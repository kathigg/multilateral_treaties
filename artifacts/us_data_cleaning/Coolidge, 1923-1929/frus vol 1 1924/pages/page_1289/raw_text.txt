The Secretary of State to the Minister in China (Schurman)
WASHINGTON, May 1, 1924—5 p.m.
84. With reference to the Department’s 80, April 24, 4 p.m., the Embassy at
London has reported36 that the Foreign Office has telegraphed the British
Minister at Peking that he is to regard as definite instructions the views
shared by the American and British Governments as to the reply that should
be returned to the Chinese request for a preliminary conference, and to act
with you in this connection.
HUGHES
36 Telegram not printed.
