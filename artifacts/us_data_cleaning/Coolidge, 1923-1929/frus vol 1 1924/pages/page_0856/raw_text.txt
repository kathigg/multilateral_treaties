832.34/186: Telegram
[Document 272]
The Chargé in Brazil (Crosby) to the Secretary of State
[Paraphrase]
RIO DE JANEIRO, July 1, 1924—noon.
[Received 3 p.m.]
38. Your no. 27, June 26, 4 p.m. Admiral Vogelgesang has requested me to
inform the Secretary of State that in accordance with the Secretary’s wishes
the recommendations for a naval program for Brazil made by the Naval
Mission were withdrawn for revision. In the revised recommendations all
reference to a concrete building program and any suggestions in regard to
tonnage, types, or number of units have been canceled and omitted.
CROSBY
