DOMINICAN REPUBLIC THE ELECTION OF HORACIO VASQUEZ TO THE PRESIDENCY AND THE EVACUATION OF THE FORCES OF THE UNITED STATES Date and Number Subject Page 1924 Jan. From the Commissioner in the Dominican Republic Published statement of General Vasquez and Señor Peynado, the two Presidential candidates, declaring that, if elected, they would maintain the Policia Nacional upon the same basis as it now exists. View that this is a definite guarantee that public order will be maintained after occupation is terminated.
