The Chief of the Division of Western European Affairs,
Department of State (Castle) to the Acting Secretary of State
[Extract]
[WASHINGTON,] August 11, 1924.
MR. GREW: Mr. Pelenyi, Hungarian Chargé, came to see me this morning to
say that he had received authorization from his Government for the signing
of the agreement for a mixed claims commission. He said, however, that
this authorization to sign was made out in the name of the Minister and
Count Szechenyi will not be back in this country before the end of October.
He said, therefore, that instead of sending the papers to us he was at the
moment merely informing us that the authorization had arrived so that, if
the Department so desired, the papers could be ready when the Minister
reaches Washington.
. . . . . . . . . . . . . .
W. E. C[ASTLE]
