To the Ambassador in France (tel.)
Instructions to inform the Foreign Office that benefits
of the extradition treaty will be unilateral unless the
treaty is mentioned in the convention; that the
Department is prepared to proceed to the signature of
the convention omitting reference to extradition if the
Foreign Office still prefers that procedure; that the
Department plans to take up negotiations with Great
Britain for the early conclusion of a convention
concerning Palestine similar to the one regarding Syria
and the Lebanon.
