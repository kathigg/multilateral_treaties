In view of the above circumstances I fear that the naval
authorities of the Powers represented in the Whangpu waters may
be subjecting themselves to the charge of favoring one side as
against the other in the present factional disturbance, and that the
naval authorities, in the absence of any imminent danger or threat
to foreign life and property, were not warranted in forbidding the
mere entrance of Chinese naval vessels into the Whangpu as
distinguished from any activity which might threaten such life
and property.”
