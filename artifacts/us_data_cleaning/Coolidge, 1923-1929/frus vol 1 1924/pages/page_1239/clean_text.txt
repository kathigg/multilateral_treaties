861.77/3482: Telegram [Document 477] The Minister in China (Schurman) to the Secretary of State PEKING, June 17, 1924—5 p.m. [Received June 17—8:15 a.m.] 185. My 176, June 13, 7 p.m. At conference of interested colleagues today it was decided to approve proposal of consuls at Harbin with the understanding that should future developments appear to make it desirable we would order them to remove seals at once. SCHURMAN
