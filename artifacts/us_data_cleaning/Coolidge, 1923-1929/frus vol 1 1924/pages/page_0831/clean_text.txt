875.00/164: Telegram [Document 262] The Minister in Albania (Grant-Smith) to the Secretary of State [Paraphrase] TIRANA, December 12, 1924—2 p.m. [Received 7:55 p.m.] 91. Threats of incursion by refugees from Yugoslav territory increase as the date for the election approaches. Reports in the foreign press that a revolution has started in Albania are premature. GRANT-SMITH
