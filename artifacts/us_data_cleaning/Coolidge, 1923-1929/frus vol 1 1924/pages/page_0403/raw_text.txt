Article 20–F
Each Contracting Party shall make it a penal offense for any person within
its jurisdiction to procure or assist the commission, in any place outside its
jurisdiction, of any offense against the laws in force in such place for
controlling or regulating the manufacture, sale, delivery, distribution, use,
possession, export, or import of any of the substances covered by this
Convention.
