893.00/5611: Telegram
[Document 340]
The Consul General at Shanghai (Cunningham) to the Secretary
of State
SHANGHAI, October 13, 1924—10 a.m.
[Received October 13—2:43 a.m.]
… source of most reliable police information during war states that
Generals Lu and Ho have deserted possibly going about 3 a.m. today. Will
report later whether confirmation obtained.
CUNNINGHAM
