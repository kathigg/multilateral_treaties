[Document 250]
The Minister in Albania (Grant-Smith) to the Secretary of State
TIRANA, June 6, 1924—7 p.m.
[Received June 7—9:50 a.m.]
49. Armistice declared evening 4th at the instance of the Government. Beys
fearful confiscation of estates. The same day the majority of the
Government deputies, a number of officials and families, fled to Durazzo.
Tirana garrison immediately took effective measures to insure public safety.
Force of tribesmen raised by Ahmed Zogu dissolving, carrying off arms.
Nationalists insist upon immediate exile of Ahmed Bey … Destroyer
Bulmer arrived at Durazzo Wednesday afternoon. Copy to European
Information Center.
GRANT-SMITH
