893.00/5691: Telegram [Document 351] The Chargé in China (Mayer) to the Secretary of State PEKING, October 31, 1924—10 a.m. [Received October 31—2:42 a.m.] 418. American consul Tientsin advises despatch of American naval vessel to Tientsin to afford additional protection American life and property if necessary. Ships of other nationalities now present. I have recommended to commander in chief Asiatic Fleet accordingly. MAYER
