12. The status of holders of identification cards shall be inquired into
periodically, preferably every six months from date stamped, where
practicable, but in any event not less frequently than once a year. When
inquiry has been so made, it will be evidenced by a notation to show date of
such inquiry and initials of the officer upon the reverse of the card. When
the holder of a “nonimmigrant” identification card qualifies as an
“immigrant,” a new identification card shall be issued, stamped to show the
correct status.
