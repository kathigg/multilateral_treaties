500.A15 a 1/539b: Telegram
[Document 145]
The Secretary of State to the Chairman of the American
Delegation (Gibson)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, August 3, 1927—2 p.m.
95. This morning the Democratic leader in the Senate (Robinson) called on
me. I discussed with him the entire situation at Geneva. Thoroughly
approving our course, he says he will support it. If speeches are made, he
said we should state our position fully and frankly.
KELLOGG
