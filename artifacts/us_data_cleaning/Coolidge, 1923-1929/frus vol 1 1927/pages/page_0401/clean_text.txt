500.A15 a 1/475: Telegram [Document 125] The Secretary of State to the Chairman of the American Delegation (Gibson) [Paraphrase] WASHINGTON, July 25, 1927—11 a.m. 61. Your No. 115, July 23, 2 p.m. I observe no particular objection to first political clause if necessity arises for it. In view of long distance the Japanese have to travel and time required by both countries to prepare for conference it might be well that 6 months’ notice be required before any meeting called under this clause be held; or, the clause might be made a part of general revision clause of the treaty. . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
