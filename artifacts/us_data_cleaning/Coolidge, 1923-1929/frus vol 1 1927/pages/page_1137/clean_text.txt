711.252/6: Telegram [Document 379] The Secretary of State to the Ambassador in Chile (Collier) [Paraphrase] WASHINGTON, October 27, 1927—6 p.m. 56. Embassy’s telegram number 161, October 24, 4 p.m. Please cable Department whether you think any material prejudice to negotiations would result if we deferred submittal of draft treaty until the middle of November or thereabouts, so as to allow time for further consideration of question whether to include in draft treaty substance of United States Senate reservation regarding national treatment of shipping.
