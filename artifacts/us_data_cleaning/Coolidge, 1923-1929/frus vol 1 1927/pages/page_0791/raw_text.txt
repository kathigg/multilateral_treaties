While it is my impression that the Peruvian Government is taking more
active steps toward ratification at the present time than it has done
heretofore, I believe that things are likely to drag on as they have in the
past, in spite of the assurances which I have reported above, unless it is
made clear to the Peruvian Government that the United States Government
understands that they have given a very definite undertaking to have the
treaty presented at the latest in the early part of October and ratified in the
course of that month. …
