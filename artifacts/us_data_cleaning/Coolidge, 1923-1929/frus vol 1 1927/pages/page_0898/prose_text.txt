They feel, however, that they are unable to conclude their report without referring to the unfailing kindness, courtesy and helpfulness of the American Ambassador to Brazil, the Honorable Edwin V. Morgan. Throughout the entire session of the Commission, he placed himself at their disposal, and gave them the benefit of his large experience, to such an extent that they deem it their duty to put on record a statement of their appreciation and indebtedness, to which they can not give adequate expression. Respectfully submitted, JAMES BROWN SCOTT JESSE S. REEVES HENRY M.
