Contacts have been established with some of the foremost
intellectuals of Latin-America, who are supporting the league and
writing for its monthly organ.

The Fourth Convention listed among the concrete tasks of the Party
“To carry on a systematic and active agitation against American
imperialism, particularly in Latin America. To demand the
withdrawal of American armed forces from foreign lands. …
“To give active support to the activities of the All-America Anti-
Imperialist League.”
