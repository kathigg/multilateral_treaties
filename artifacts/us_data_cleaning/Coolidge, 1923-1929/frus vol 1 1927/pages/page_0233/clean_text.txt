500.A15 a 1/216: Telegram [Document 38] The Ambassador in Great Britain (Houghton) to the Secretary of State LONDON, May 6, 1927—1 p.m. [Received May 6—8:56 a.m.] 105. Your 93, May 5, noon. Chamberlain assures me that British delegation at the forthcoming Three-Power Conference will include fully empowered Dominion representatives. HOUGHTON
