560.M2/92: Telegram [Document 230] The Secretary of State to the Chief of the American Delegation (Wilson) WASHINGTON, November 4, 1927—1 p.m. 20. Your 54, November 4, 1 p.m., last paragraph. You should not sign Saturday night. Draft agreement is extremely complicated and there will not be time for the officials of this Government to give due consideration to the matter. Furthermore, there appears to be no reason for such haste in rushing through a matter of such importance. KELLOGG
