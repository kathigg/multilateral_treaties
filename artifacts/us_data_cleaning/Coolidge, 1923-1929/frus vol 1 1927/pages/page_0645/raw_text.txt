5. It seems to Department that questions of free circulation of capital and of
artificial control of exchange operations are outside scope of the present
Conference. In Department’s view it is preferable that these questions
should not be made subject of seriously considered additional provisions,
but if proposals of this nature are pressed, you will cable comment and
recommendations.
