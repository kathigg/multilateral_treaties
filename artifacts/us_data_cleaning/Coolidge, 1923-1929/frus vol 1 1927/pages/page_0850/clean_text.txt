710.C2/230: Telegram [Document 287] The Ambassador in Brazil (Morgan) to the Secretary of State RIO DE JANEIRO, May 14, 1927—11 a.m. [Received May 14—10:55 a.m.] 24. Messrs. Brown Scott and Reeves have handed me the following text for transmission to the Department: “In compliance with the Department’s telegram of May 13th, we hasten to state that we shall refrain from introducing the plan. We avail ourselves of the opportunity of expressing our appreciation of the courteous language in which the Department’s instruction is couched.” MORGAN
