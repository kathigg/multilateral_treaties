In place of original articles 14 and 15 insert the following Article of two
paragraphs
“Commercial travelers representing manufacturers, merchants
and traders domiciled in the territories of either High Contracting
Party shall on their entry into and sojourn in the territories of the
other Party and on their departure therefrom be accorded the most
favored nation treatment in respect of customs and other
privileges and of all charges and taxes of whatever denomination
applicable to them or to their samples.
If either High Contracting Party require the presentation of an
authentic document establishing the identity and authority of a
commercial traveler, a signed statement by the concern or
concerns represented, certified by a consular officer of the
country of destination shall be accepted as satisfactory.”
