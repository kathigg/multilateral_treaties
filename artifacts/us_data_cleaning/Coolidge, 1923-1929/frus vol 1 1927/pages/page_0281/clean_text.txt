In any event, it seemed to the Navy that it would be best not to open up a revision of the Washington Treaty at this time as no one would know to where it would lead. Of course, the Navy claims that we need larger battleships because of their wider range for cruising, we having but few naval bases throughout the world. I do not know how much there is to this but I cannot see that we would need battleships except for defense and in our Philippine possessions.
