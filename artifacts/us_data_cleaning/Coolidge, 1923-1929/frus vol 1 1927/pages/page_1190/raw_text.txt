825.506/13: Telegram
[Document 408]
The Secretary of State to the Ambassador in Chile (Collier)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, September 14, 1927—6 p.m.
42. Your telegram No. 129, September 12, noon. The Department can
perceive no good basis for a protest against the provisions of the insurance
law. See Department’s telegram number 31, August 8, noon.
KELLOGG
