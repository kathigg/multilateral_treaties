Additional Protocol Between the United States of America and
Other American Republics, Signed at Lima, October 19, 192743

Brazil, Colombia, Costa Rica, Cuba, Ecuador, the United States of America,
Guatemala, Haiti, Honduras, Nicaragua, Panama, Paraguay, Peru, the
Dominican Republic, Uruguay and the United States of Venezuela, desirous
