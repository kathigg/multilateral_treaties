825.85/42: Telegram [Document 384] The Ambassador in Chile (Collier) to the Secretary of State SANTIAGO, August 30, 1927—3 p.m. [Received 6 p.m.] 115. Government has sent to Congress project of law embodying all points of my 111, August 25, 4 p.m., except point 2. Omission of latter will probably greatly diminish opposition to bill by British shipowners and eliminate that of other nations. Grace Line is seriously and almost solely threatened. I renew request for instructions by cable. COLLIER
