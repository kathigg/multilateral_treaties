500.A15 a 1/83: Telegram
[Document 22]
The Ambassador in Great Britain (Houghton) to the Secretary of
State
[Paraphrase]
LONDON, March 9, 1927—4 p.m. 
[Received March 9—2:30 p.m.]
59. Department’s No. 49, March 8, 4 p.m. Chamberlain is in Geneva. I saw
Tyrrell,22 who showed me a copy of cable sent yesterday to British
Embassy at Washington; he said a copy had been forwarded to Tokyo and
would in all probability remove any doubts there in regard to British
acceptance.
HOUGHTON
22 Sir William George Tyrrell, British Permanent Under Secretary of State
for Foreign Affairs.
