President Coolidge to the Secretary of State
RAPID CITY, S. DAK., June 30, 1927.
[Received July 5.]
MY DEAR MR. SECRETARY: I have your letter of the 28th, with the enclosure from the
British Ambassador, both of which I have read with care. Your position
seems to me correct and satisfactory.
Very truly yours,
CALVIN COOLIDGE
