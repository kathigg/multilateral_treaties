825.506/16: Telegram [Document 411] The Acting Secretary of State to the Ambassador in Chile (Collier) WASHINGTON, September 27, 1927—7 p.m. 47. Your 138, September 21, 3 p.m. As there is no discrimination against American concerns there is no apparent ground for a formal protest but in your discretion you may continue to urge informally that the provision in question be eliminated upon the ground that it would interfere with business between the two countries. CARR
