On the same occasion I would appreciate an early notification as to whether

In connection therewith I am in a position to state that my Government,
although not being able—for reasons mentioned before—to enter into an
agreement stipulating for payments to be made prior to 1943, could and will
reserve a right to commence payments on January 1st, 1929.

relief debts on the last mentioned date.
