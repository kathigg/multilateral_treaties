S. views be communicated to the Canadian Government with the suggestion that the United States will appreciate the prohibition of seining in Missisquoi Bay, if such action is found practicable. 514 [Pg. 514 includes portions of Doc. 371 and Doc. 372] Mar. 22 (53) From the Canadian Minister Explanation that the Provincial authorities administering Missisquoi Bay are opposed to the prohibition of a reasonable amount of seine fishing therein, although they prohibit fishing during the spawning season; reiteration of contention that the Missisquoi Bay situation should be dealt with in connection with other outstanding matters, and not by itself. 515 [Pg. 515 includes portions of Doc. 372 and Doc. 373] Mar. 30 To the Canadian Minister Regret that the Canadian Government does not deem it advisable to discontinue the issuance of licenses for seine fishing in Missisquoi Bay at the present time. 516 [Pg. 516 includes portions of Doc. 373 and
