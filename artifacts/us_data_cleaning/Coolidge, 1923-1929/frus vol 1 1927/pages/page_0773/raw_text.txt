717.2114/53: Telegram
[Document 255]
The Secretary of State to the Chargé in Nicaragua (Munro)
WASHINGTON, October 6, 1927—6 p.m.
151. Your 260, October 4, 2 p.m. It has been necessary to consult another
Department in connection with this question and your instructions have
been delayed pending receipt of reply. It is hoped that it may be possible to
send instructions to you before long.
KELLOGG
