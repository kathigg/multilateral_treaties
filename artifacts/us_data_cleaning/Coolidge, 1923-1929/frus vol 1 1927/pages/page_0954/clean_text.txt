835.34/422: Telegram [Document 309] The Acting Secretary of State to the Chargé in Argentina (Cable) [Paraphrase] WASHINGTON, March 9, 1927—7 p.m. 11. Department’s telegram No. 9, dated March 1, 3 p.m. Please inform Department of present status of matter. Representative of Bethlehem interests in Washington states that he has good reason for believing that the board which is supposed to receive and report upon all tenders did not even pass upon his company’s bids. GREW
