At the present moment it has not yet been decided whether the investment
program will provisionally extend for three years or five years. The plan is
drawn to cover a period of five years. It is proposed that the bonds be issued
in annual instalments to cover the year’s need. If the entire loan can
presently be floated at a favorable interest rate for a five year period, this
course will be followed. On the other hand, if the conditions of emission are
not altogether favorable, the program will be curtailed to provide
provisionally for three years only, with the thought of securing later on
better terms for the needs of 1931 and 1932.
