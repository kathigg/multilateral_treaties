551.2 A 2/–: Telegram
[Document 232]
The Chargé in Switzerland (Marriner) to the Secretary of State
BERNE, January 13, 1927—noon.
[Received 2:30 p.m.]
7. My 138, December 28, 3 p.m.30 Letter from League Secretariat states
that on January 12th experts on double taxation decided to hold next
meeting in London on April 4th, 1927, and have expressed the hope that an
expert from the United States will take part in that meeting.31
Secretariat requests to be informed as soon as possible of name of
American expert so that formal invitation may be sent him.
MARRINER
30 Not printed.
31 The United States had not been represented at the meeting of the
Committee of Experts on Double Taxation and Tax Evasion at Geneva on
Jan. 5, 1927.
