500.A15 a 1/206: Telegram
[Document 36]
The Ambassador in Great Britain (Houghton) to the Secretary of
State
[Paraphrase]
LONDON, April 28, 1927—5 p.m. 
[Received April 28—12:47 p.m.]
98. Your No. 84, April 27, noon. Chamberlain tells me that British
Government prefer Geneva for Conference and that they have so notified
Japan. They are also taking the necessary steps in regard to League
facilities.
HOUGHTON
