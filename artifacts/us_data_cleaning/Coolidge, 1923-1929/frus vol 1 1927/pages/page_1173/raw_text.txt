825.6362/25: Telegram
[Document 399]
The Secretary of State to the Ambassador in Chile (Collier)
WASHINGTON, November 1, 1927—7 p.m.
58. Department informed by American interests that Chilean Government is
paying little attention to protests made by their representative, Mr. Seibert,
against the coal bill, which has already been approved by the Joint
Committee of Congress. Department had hoped that no definite action
would be taken pending the receipt of full report which it understands
Chilean Ambassador, following conferences in New York, is making to
President Ibañez. Please investigate and cable briefly report on present
situation.
KELLOGG
