500.A15/519: Telegram [Document 182] The Chief of the American Representation on the Preparatory Commission (Gibson) to the Secretary of State GENEVA, April 26, 1927—9 p.m. [Received April 26—7:40 p.m.] 260. Preparatory Commission adjourned this evening. In closing speech President singled out American delegation alone for warm tribute to our helpful attitude which had promoted good understanding. He alluded to the forthcoming three-power conversations as calculated to facilitate the further approach to the problem of disarmanent. GIBSON
