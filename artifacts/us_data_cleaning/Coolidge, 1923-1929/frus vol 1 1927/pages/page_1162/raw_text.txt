Article 4. Expenses occasioned by the law are to be met by
increasing all consular duties 10 percent.
Article 5. New law to go into effect January 1st, 1928.
Consular fees are frequently based on value of invoice. I assume proposed
increase affects consular invoices of imports from all countries whether
Chilean vessels maintain service with them or not. If so proposed increase
seems to me unobjectionable in principle and possibly beneficial to
American exports. Personally, cannot see ground for objection to new bill
but would appreciate Department’s instructions.
COLLIER
