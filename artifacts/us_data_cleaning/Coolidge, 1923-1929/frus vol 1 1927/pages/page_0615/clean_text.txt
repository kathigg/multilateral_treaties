560.M2/–: Telegram [Document 204] The Consul at Geneva (Tuck) to the Secretary of State GENEVA, June 14, 1927—3 p.m. [Received June 14—1:54 p.m.] Council today adopted Stresemann report fixing date Diplomatic Conference Abolition Export and Import Prohibitions [and] Restrictions for October 17, 1927. This date chosen in order not to conflict with proposed meeting Preparatory Commission for Disarmament in November.89 Report calls attention of states invited to Conference to conclusion[s] formulated by International Economic Conference90 on the subject [of] prohibitions. All countries who sent delegations to Economic Conference will receive invitation to attend. TUCK 89 For correspondence concerning the fourth session of the Preparatory Commission, see pp. 159 [Pg. 159 includes portions of Doc. 150 and Doc. 151] ff. 90 See pp. 238 [Pg. 238 includes portions of Doc. 199, Doc. 200, and Doc. 201] ff.
