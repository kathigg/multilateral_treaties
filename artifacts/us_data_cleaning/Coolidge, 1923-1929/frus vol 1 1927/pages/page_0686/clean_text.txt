551.2 A 2/5: Telegram [Document 233] The Secretary of State to the Chargé in Switzerland (Marriner) WASHINGTON, February 17, 1927—1 p.m. 19. Your despatch No. 1080, January 13, 1927.30 You may advise the Secretary General of the League that this Government takes pleasure in designating Professor Thomas S. Adams, of Yale University, New Haven, Connecticut, as an American expert to attend the forthcoming meeting of the Committee of Experts on Double Taxation and Tax Evasion at London, April 4, 1927.
