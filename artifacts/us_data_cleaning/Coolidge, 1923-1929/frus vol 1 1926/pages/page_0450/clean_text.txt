500.A15/391: Telegram [Document 89] The Minister in Uruguay (Grant-Smith) to the Secretary of State MONTEVIDEO, October 7, 1926—7 p.m. [Received 8:30 p.m.] 53. Your telegram number 23, October 2, 1 p.m. The Minister for Foreign Affairs informed me today that Uruguay will be represented at the next meeting of the Preliminary [sic] Disarmament Commission, possibly by Guani.6 GRANT-SMITH 6 Alberto Guani. On Oct. 9 Mr. Grant-Smith reported Uruguay’s Withdrawal from the Preparatory Commission.
