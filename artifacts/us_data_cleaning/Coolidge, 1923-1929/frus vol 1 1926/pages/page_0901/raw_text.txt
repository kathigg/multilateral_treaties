I want to consult Freyre on this suggested program not later than morning
of March 29, so that action can be taken that afternoon. Haste is necessary, I
can assure you, and only possible way out of present most distressing
dilemma is to terminate the plebiscite at once.
Please notify me of your instructions to Lima, so that I shall be able surely
to arrange for meeting of the Commission on March 29.37
Lassiter
VON TRESCKOW
37 At 8 p.m., Mar. 27, the Secretary telegraphed General Lassiter that the
