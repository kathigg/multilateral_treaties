The Secretary of State to the Chargé in China (Mayer)
WASHINGTON, October 15, 1926—3 p.m.
231. Your telegrams 462, October 8, 8 p.m. and 477, October 14, Noon.
Department considered that its instructions No. 217, October 5, 7 p.m.
sufficiently covered the matter to enable you to join your colleagues in
protest outlined in paragraph 3 of your urgent telegram 462, October 8, 8
p.m. No objection to wording of protest as amended is perceived by
Department.
KELLOGG
