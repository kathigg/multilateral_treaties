The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, March 1, 1926—7 p.m.
[Received March 1—1:52 p.m.]
105. My 103, March 1, 5 p.m. I have telegraphed consul general, Canton,
approving proposal in the concluding paragraph of his telegram on the
assumption that regret and assurances are to be expressed to him.
MACMURRAY
