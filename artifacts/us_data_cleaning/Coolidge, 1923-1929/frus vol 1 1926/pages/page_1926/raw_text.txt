893.512/407: Telegram
[Document 660]
The Secretary of State to the Chargé in China (Mayer)
WASHINGTON, October 13, 1926—noon.
225. Your 462, October 8, 8 p.m. did not reach the Department in time to
get a reply to you before meeting of Legations in Peking. Please wire me
result of the meeting and attitude of different governments as to the
collection of Canton taxes.
KELLOGG
