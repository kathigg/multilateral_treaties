The countries participating in the work of the Commission were the United States, Belgium, the British Empire, China, Denmark, France, Italy, Japan, the Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, Spain, and Sweden. The American Commissioner was Silas H. Strawn; the American technical advisers were Joseph E. Jacobs and Mahlon F. Perkins. [744] The Secretary of State to the Minister in China (MacMurray)
