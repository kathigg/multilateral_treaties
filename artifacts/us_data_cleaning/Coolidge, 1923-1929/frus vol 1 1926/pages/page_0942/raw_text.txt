If no agreement can be reached through the negotiations in Washington and
if the Arbitrator is averse to intervening in matter in any way, then it is
important that plebiscite be terminated without further delay. In that event,
opportunity must surely be given Peruvian Commissioner, before he
withdraws, to participate in passage of resolution to terminate plebiscite for
reasons given.
My purpose herein has been to give you, as you request, the situations to be
faced if no agreement can be reached in Washington. I still have hope in the
negotiations, and shall keep you informed of anything taking place here
which may affect the issue. Lassiter.
