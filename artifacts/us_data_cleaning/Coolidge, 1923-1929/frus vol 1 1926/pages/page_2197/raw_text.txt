500.A4d/120: Telegram
[Document 785]
The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State
[Paraphrase]
PEKING, February 6, 1926—3 p.m.
[Received February 6—7:46 a.m.]
66. Last sentence of your 36. I concur fully in your proposal that you
deliver your intended note to the Chinese Minister without awaiting further
action by him, telegraphing the text of the note to me for use as suggested
in my telegram 50, January 27, noon, first paragraph.
MACMURRAY
