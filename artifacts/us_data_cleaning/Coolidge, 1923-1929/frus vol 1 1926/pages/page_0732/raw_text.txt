723.2515/1807: Telegram
[Document 140]
The Secretary of State to the Ambassador in Chile (Collier)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, January 7, 1926—9 p.m.
4. Embassy’s No. 8, January 6, noon, last sentence. It is not clear to
Department whether you have had an opportunity to make statement to
Argentine Ambassador and Uruguayan Minister authorized in Department’s
No. 85, December 22, 1925, 1 p.m.66 Please inform Department definitely
on this point stating exactly what you said to them so that I may be in better
position to consider what instructions should be sent our representatives in
Argentina and Uruguay.
KELLOGG
66 Not printed; see summary in telegram No. 1, Jan. 4, to the Ambassador in
Peru, p. 260 [Pg. 260 is part of Doc. 138].
