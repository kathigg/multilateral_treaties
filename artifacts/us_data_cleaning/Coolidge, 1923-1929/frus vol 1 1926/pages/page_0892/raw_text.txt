For Lassiter. Your telegram March 25, 3 p.m., received. I wholly approve
position you took in proceedings yesterday and think that the formal record
at this time is satisfactory. I shall send further suggestions, especially with
regard to the resolution for the suspension of proceedings, as soon as replies
have been received from Santiago and Lima, as stated in my telegram of
March 25, 6 p.m.36
KELLOGG
