One means essential to the attainment of this end is the functioning of foreign Assessors in all police and criminal cases arising in the Settlement, and to this the Chinese representatives have interposed, up to the present moment, an absolute veto. There appears to be now no chance that the rendition of the Mixed Court will take place before May 30th next, but it is to be hoped that the Legations will have been able to take some measures before then, to absolve themselves in the eyes of the public from a portion of the blame for this delay that should rightfully be placed upon the Chinese authorities.
