500.A4d/126: Telegram
[Document 789]
The Secretary of State to the Minister in China (MacMurray)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, February 23, 1926—7 p.m.
46. I am temporarily withholding reply of the Department in view of urgent
request of Chinese Minister for delay. He hopes to obtain permission
shortly to withdraw note. I expect to be able, if he succeeds, to authorize
you to return the note from the Foreign Office unanswered.
KELLOGG
