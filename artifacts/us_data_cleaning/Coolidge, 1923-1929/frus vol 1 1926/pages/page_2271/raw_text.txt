Washington for consultation. I would propose at that time to outline to him
the developments which you reported in the telegrams mentioned above,

For your information, the Department would prefer, inasmuch as you have
placed Davis already in direct touch with the Japanese, to leave the decision
as to place and mode of purely business negotiations to arrangement
between the interested parties.
