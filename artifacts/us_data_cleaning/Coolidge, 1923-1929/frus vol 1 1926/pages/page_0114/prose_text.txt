S. proposal, on grounds that controversies of the nature of that of Tacna-Arica are limited in scope to the contending nations; that as plebiscite has not been held, the provinces continue to be Peruvian; that by cession to Bolivia the honor and dignity of Peru would be irretrievably outraged; and that Peru cannot accept a solution which carries with it the forsaking of its citizens in the provinces.
