In this connection your Excellency wants to know whether my Government accepts the conditions, reservations and understandings to the said resolution. The Government of Costa Rica will cease to be a member of the League of Nations on January 1, 1927, and, taking into account that that date is very close, it has instructed me to say to your Excellency that it does not believe it is its duty to decide in regard to the desired acceptance. Be pleased [etc.] RAFAEL OREAMUNO
