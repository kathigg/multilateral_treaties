500.C114/445d [Document 5] The Secretary of State to the Ambassador in Belgium (Phillips)9 WASHINGTON, February 12, 1926. No. 226 SIR: I am enclosing, for transmission to Baron Raymond de Waha, still accredited as Chargé d’Affaires of Luxemburg to the United States, a note10 concerning the conditions, reservations and understandings contained in the Resolution of the Senate, dated January 27, 1926, for the adherence by the United States to the Protocol of Signature and Statute of the Permanent Court of International Justice. I am [etc.] FRANK B. KELLOGG 9 Also Minister to Luxemburg. 10 Text identical with that of the note to the Austrian Minister, February 12, p. 3 [Pg. 3 includes portions of Doc. 2 and Doc. 3].
