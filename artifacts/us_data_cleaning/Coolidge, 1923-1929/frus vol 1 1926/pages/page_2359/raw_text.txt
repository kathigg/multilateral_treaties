701.6193/84: Telegram
[Document 852]
The Acting Secretary of State to the Minister in China
(MacMurray)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, August 19, 1926—6 p.m.
168. Your telegram number 331 of August 18th, noon. The question of the
Soviet Ambassador’s status concerns only China and the Soviet
Government. No doubt as to this fact should be left in the minds of the
Chinese.
HARRISON
