On the other and, the American Government will not direct its war vessels to comply with the above regulations, and more especially with the circular from the commander in chief of the socalled National Revolutionary Army requesting such vessels promptly to sail down river to a comparatively distant place, and not to anchor in the vicinity of the war zone at Wuhan, in order to prevent danger and avoid misunderstanding.
