500.A15/229: Telegram
[Document 74]
The Chargé in Switzerland (Winslow) to the Secretary of State
BERNE, April 24, 1926—noon.
[Received April 24—9:47 a.m.]
95. Buero as head of Uruguayan delegation to the Preparatory Disarmament
Commission having received directly from the Secretariat of the League a
copy of my letter of April 8th to Drummond93 told me at a private interview
in his opinion the attitude of the Department was entirely correct and that he
could be counted on to do all in his power to support our views with regard
to the organization of the work of the Conference when it meets.
WINSLOW
93 See telegram No. 54, Apr. 7, 1926, to the Chargé in Switzerland, p. 70
[Pg. 70 is part of Doc. 59].
