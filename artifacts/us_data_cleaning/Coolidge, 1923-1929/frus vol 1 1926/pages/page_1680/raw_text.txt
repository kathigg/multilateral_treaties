893.5045/376: Telegram
[Document 595]
The Chargé in China (Mayer) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, September 14, 1926—4 p.m.
[Received September 14—9:27 a.m.]
399. Following radiogram received by American naval attaché from U. S. S.
Sacramento at Swatow, September 14th.
“After September 11th the British discontinued campaigns against
the striking pickets of the Canton River. Negotiations concerning
removal of boycott are to be resumed between the British and
Canton Governments.”
MAYER
