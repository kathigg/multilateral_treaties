With the present sinister forces actively at work fomenting discontent and anti-foreign feeling the after effects to be feared from the use of force seem to outweigh the advantage that it might be expected to produce. In view of the accusations that already have been made regarding the length of working hours and under-payment in the American-owned rug factories at Tientsin I should be greatly interested in receiving such information as you could conveniently secure in regard to these points.
