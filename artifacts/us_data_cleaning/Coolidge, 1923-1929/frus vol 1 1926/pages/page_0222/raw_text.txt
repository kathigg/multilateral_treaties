Apr. 19
(202)
To the Minister in China
Instruction to advise the consul general at Canton to
take no further action in the matter of the arrest by
Canton military authorities of Chu Shea-wai, an
American citizen of Chinese race, in view of the fact
that the Department considers it doubtful that he is any
longer entitled to the protection of the U. S.
Government; views of the Department which are to be
followed when similar cases arise in the future.
