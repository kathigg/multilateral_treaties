721.2315/306: Telegram
[Document 370]
The Ambassador in Brazil (Morgan) to the Secretary of State
RIO DE JANEIRO, July 5, 1926—7 p.m.
[Received 8:30 p.m.]
54. Brazilian Minister for Foreign Affairs considers the moment favorable
for renewing representations to the Peruvian Government regarding
ratification of Colombian-Peruvian treaty. Colombian Minister here
entertains same belief.
MORGAN
