The passengers shall remain in the Camaran establishment five or six days according as whether the case is plague or cholera. If a new case should occur after disembarkation, the period of observation shall be extended by five days for cholera and six days for plague, to date from the isolation of the last case. For plague, the measures prescribed by Article 25 shall be applied with regard to the rats which may be found on board the vessels. When these operations have been completed, the ship, having reembarked its pilgrims, shall be sent on to Jeddah. ARTICLE 131
