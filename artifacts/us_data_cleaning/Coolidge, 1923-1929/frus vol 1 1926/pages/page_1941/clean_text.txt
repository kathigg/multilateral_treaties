893.512/421: Telegram [Document 673] The Chargé in China (Mayer) to the Secretary of State PEKING, October 20, 1926—11 a.m. [Received October 20—4:25 a.m.] 495. Your 234, October 16, 4 p.m. I am deferring action contemplated in third paragraph my 485, October 16, 1 p.m., in view of diplomatic body meeting called for this morning further to discuss Canton tax situation in particular relation to certain instructions from his Government which the British Minister, I understand, intends then to bring to our attention. MAYER
