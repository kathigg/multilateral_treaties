The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, March 13, 1926—4 p.m.
[Received March 13—9:12 a.m.]
125. My number 123, March 12, 7 p.m. Naval commanders at Tientsin have
found it necessary because of practical difficulties to request delay of one
day in making the contemplated notification to Chinese military and naval
authorities. I have approved.
MACMURRAY
