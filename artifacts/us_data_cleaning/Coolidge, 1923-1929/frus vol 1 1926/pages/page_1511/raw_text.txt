393.1163/65: Telegram
[Document 508]
The Chargé in China (Mayer) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, October 13, 1926—4 p.m.
[Received October 13—9:35 a.m.]
476. My 453, October 5, 10 a.m. British Legation have received a telegram
from British missionary near Sianfu, dated October 12th, indicating that all
British and American missionaries who desired to leave are safely out of
Sianfu. American military officer will not therefore proceed to Sianfu
unless report proves incorrect.
MAYER
