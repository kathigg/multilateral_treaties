After consulting by telegraph with commander in chief and acting upon his suggestion, I telegraphed American consul at Swatow as follow: “September 14, noon. Regarding 24 hours’ advance notice regulation I consider after consultation with commander in chief that notice upon arrival channel entrance should be sufficient. You should likewise reserve the right for our vessels to leave at night in an emergency after due notice to the authorities.” 3.
