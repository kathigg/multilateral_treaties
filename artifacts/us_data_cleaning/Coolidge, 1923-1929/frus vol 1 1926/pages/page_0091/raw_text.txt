Apr. 18 From the Consul at Arica (tel.)
From Lassiter: Statement that Chile’s real default lies
not in technical nonfulfillment, although that can be
shown, but in deportations of Peruvians and constant
campaign of open violence and secret terrorism, and that
she is counting on difficulty of securing satisfactory
evidence against her.
