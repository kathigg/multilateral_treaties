S. adherence to protocol of December 16, 1920, and the adjoined statute for the Permanent Court, but not the optional clause for compulsory jurisdiction, subject to certain reservations and understandings, with further stipulation that signature shall not be affixed until signatory powers shall have indicated through exchange of notes their acceptance of the reservations and understandings as a part and condition of U.
