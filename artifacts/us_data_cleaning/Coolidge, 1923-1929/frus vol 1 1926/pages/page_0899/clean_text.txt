723.2515/2067: Telegram [Document 209] The Consul at Arica (Von Tresckow) to the Secretary of State [Extract—Paraphrase] ARICA, March 27, 1926—noon. [Received 3:23 p.m.] From Lassiter. As Peru is not participating in the registration and another impasse has been reached, I think that, even in light of stand I have taken in last three days, I should be justified in saying that I do not believe that there is any hope of acceptable solution of this problem through a plebiscite and that, with a view to terminating plebiscite and to facilitating good offices, I am willing to vote for resolution suggested in my telegram of March 21, 6 p.m. … . . . . . . . . . . . . . . Lassiter VON TRESCKOW
