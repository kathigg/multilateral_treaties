From this provision it is clear that the Hukuang Loan Service is entitled to
an automatic priority of charge on the available revenues of the Chinese
Government over loans contracted at a later date, such as the internal loans,
the services of which are now being met out of Customs revenues, and the
Undersigned have therefore the honour to request that the necessary funds
shall immediately be furnished from the Maritime Customs revenues.
