Two notes from the Acting Foreign Minister at Canton
(texts printed): (1) To the Portuguese consul general,
November 8, returning the joint protest with the
statement that relations of the powers with the
Cantonese regime are not regulated on a basis which
can entitle them to raise questions of treaty violations;

referring him to the reply made November 8 to the
Portuguese consul general.
