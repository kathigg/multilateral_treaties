723.2515/2428: Telegram
[Document 325]
The Secretary of State to the Consul at Arica (Von Tresckow)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, June 11, 1926—9 a.m.
For Lassiter. Resolution which you have offered has aroused very bitter
feeling in Chilean Embassy here. They feel that it is unwarranted public
indictment of Chile and that honor will require her to break off negotiations
and possibly to break off relations with the United States. If Chilean
Commissioner proposes any possible solution or any compromise
arrangement by which passage of resolution would be avoided, please try to
adjourn action and cable us immediately.
KELLOGG
