ARTICLE 17
The aircraft of a contracting State which establishes reservations and
restrictions in accordance with Article 16, may be subjected to the same
reservations and restrictions in any other contracting State, even though the
latter State does not itself impose the reservations and restrictions on other
foreign aircraft.
ARTICLE 18
Every aircraft passing through the territory of a contracting State, including
landing and stoppages reasonably necessary for the purpose of such transit,
shall be exempt from any seizure on the ground of infringement of patent,
design or model, subject to the deposit of security the amount of which in
default of amicable agreement shall be fixed with the least possible delay
by the competent authority of the place of seizure.

RULES TO BE OBSERVED ON DEPARTURE WHEN UNDER WAY AND ON LANDING
ARTICLE 19
