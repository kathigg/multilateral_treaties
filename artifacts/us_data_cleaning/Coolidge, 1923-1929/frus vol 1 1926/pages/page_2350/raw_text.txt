893.6363/60: Telegram
[Document 846]
The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, January 30, 1926—3 p.m.
[Received January 30—5:42 a.m.]
59. American consul at Swatow telegraphs that Swatow authorities have
instituted oil monopoly similar to that in operation at Canton—see Canton’s
despatch 366, October 27th, to Department.92
MACMURRAY
92 Not printed.
