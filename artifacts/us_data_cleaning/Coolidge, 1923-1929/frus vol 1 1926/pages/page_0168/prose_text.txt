Date and number Subject Page 1926 Jan. 30 (24) From the American Delegation (tel.) Probability that the powers will be asked to permit the 2½ percent surtax provided by the Washington treaty to become effective immediately, with the provision that the revenue therefrom be made available for the unrestricted use of the Central Government; belief that the powers will not recede from their position that these funds should be impounded for future disposition as agreed by the Conference.
