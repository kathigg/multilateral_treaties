ANNEXIV.—PROPOSAL CONCERNING THE RENDITION OF THE MIXED COURT AND THE REORGANIZATION OF THE JUDICIAL SYSTEM IN THE INTERNATIONAL SETTLEMENT OF SHANGHAI 1. The Shanghai Mixed Court, together with the Registrar’s Office, the prison and Detention House attached thereto, shall be restored to the Chinese Government. 2. The Chinese Government will, in place of the said Mixed Court, establish a Court of Justice of the Shanghai International Settlement, which will be organized upon the model of a regular court of law of the Republic, to deal with all civil and criminal cases, as well as cases involving violations of police regulations arising within the Settlement, in accordance with the laws and ordinances in force in China. 3.
