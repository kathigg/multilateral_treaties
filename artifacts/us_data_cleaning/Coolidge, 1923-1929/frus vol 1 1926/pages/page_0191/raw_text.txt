From the American, British, French, and Japanese
Ministers to the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs
Protest against the issuance of a further internal loan
secured upon surplus customs revenues; statement
that, since every new lien upon these revenues
necessarily postpones the payment of the foreign debt,
the Ministers regard as unacceptable the Chinese
assertion that foreign creditors are not adversely
affected thereby.
