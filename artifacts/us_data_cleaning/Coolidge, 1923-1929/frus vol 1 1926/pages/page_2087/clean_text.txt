893.51/4974: Telegram [Document 739] The Secretary of State to the Minister in China (MacMurray) WASHINGTON, October 30, 1926—11 p.m. 254. John Jay Abbott39 telegraphs Department that Chinese Minister for Finance plans new domestic loan 30,000,000 Mex. secured on Customs to be released from service 9th year internal loan 1927. If this report is correct you are authorized to make protest similar to that reported in your 346, August 24, 4 p.m. KELLOGG 39 Vice President, Continental & Commercial Trust & Savings Bank of Chicago.
