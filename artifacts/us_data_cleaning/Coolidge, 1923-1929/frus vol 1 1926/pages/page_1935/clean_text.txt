893.512/417: Telegram [Document 668] The Secretary of State to the Chargé in China (Mayer) WASHINGTON, October 16, 1926—4 p.m. 234. Your 485, October 16, 1 p.m. Action proposed by you has approval of Department which is of the opinion that the record should be clear on the subject of taxes such as those proposed at Canton and in Shantung. KELLOGG
