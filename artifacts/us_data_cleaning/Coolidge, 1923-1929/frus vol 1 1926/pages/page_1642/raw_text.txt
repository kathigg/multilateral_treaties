893.00/7411: Telegram
[Document 574]
The Secretary of State to the Minister in China (MacMurray)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, May 26, 1926—5 p.m.
107. Your telegram No. 226, May 23, 2 p.m. Instructions suggested in
second paragraph of your telegram are approved.
The Department considers it preferable to have Mayer confer with Tredwell
only at Hongkong. However, in instructions sent to Tredwell10 he is
authorized to go to Canton if Mayer so requests. If you consider it
necessary you may authorize Chamberlain to go to Canton for conference
with Mayer.
The Department prefers to have Mayer use commercial transportation. If
commercial transportation is available, no request will be made of the
Navy.11
KELLOGG
10 Not printed.
11 Mayer left Peking May 31, and his arrival at Canton was reported June
12.
