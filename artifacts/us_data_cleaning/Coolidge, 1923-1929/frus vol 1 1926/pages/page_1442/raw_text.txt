[Document 459]
The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, August 17, 1926—10 a.m.
[Received August 17—6:14 a.m.]
328. My 317, August 5, noon. Following from American consul at
Changsha:
“Chiang Kai-shih arrived this morning. There is no serious
fighting. Earty renewal of drive toward Hankow is not unlikely.
Department has not been informed.”
MACMURRAY
