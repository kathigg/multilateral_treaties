“No. 90. In order to further concrete the ideas set forth in my
telegram No. 89 I wish to add:
a) We accept the division of the territory following the
present inter-departmental boundaries, Chile thus
remaining in possession of the Railroad’s route.
b) If it were insisted upon, we would give Bolivia a
corridor with the aforementioned boundary line as an
axis.
c) In order to avoid the possibility of the corridor
covering the route of the Railroad, its width would be
restricted or else it would be completed by Peru, the
latter being compensated by Chile in other sections
along the length of the same corridor.”
