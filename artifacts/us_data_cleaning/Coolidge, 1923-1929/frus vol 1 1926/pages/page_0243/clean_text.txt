500.C114/445aa [Document 8] The Cuban Chargé (Barón) to the Secretary of State [Translation] WASHINGTON, March 17, 1926. EXCELLENCY: I have the honor to inform you that my Government, upon reading the resolution of the Senate of the United States of America of January 27, 1926, by which it gave its advice and consent to the adhesion of the United States to the protocol of signature of the statute for the Permanent Court of International Justice dated December 16, 1920, and the appended statute for the Permanent Court of International Justice, authorizes me to accept, as I hereby do, in its name, the conditions, reservations and understandings contained in the said resolution as part and condition of the adhesion of the United States to the said protocol and statutes. I avail myself [etc.] JOSÉ T. BARÓN
