From the Minister in China
Observation that the situation at Tientsin, in which
American forces find themselves in a militarily
untenable position, must ultimately be resolved by
their withdrawal, if possible in conjunction with
forces of other foreign powers; but that such action
must be timed with greatest care to avoid further
incitement of Chinese nationalistic sentiment against
foreign rights and interests.
