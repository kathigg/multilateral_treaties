Commission reconvenes on March 24. I am doing this in the hope that door can be kept open for diplomatic adjustment and especially with view to affording opportunity for Chile to consider fully and to present, if she is so advised, any new suggestion such as enlargement of Arbitrator’s powers. If Chile were to come forward on her own initiative with proposal either to invest Arbitrator with unlimited powers or otherwise, we should all regard it as distinct step forward in direction of a settlement, and it would be our hope that it would lead to actual negotiations. I do not know how long General Lassiter can or will maintain status quo in Plebiscitary Commission; for that reason it becomes exceedingly important that you explore all possibilities with Chilean Government at once.
