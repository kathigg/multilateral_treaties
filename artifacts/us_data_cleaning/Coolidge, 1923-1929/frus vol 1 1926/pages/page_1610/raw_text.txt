The Secretary of State to the Minister in China (MacMurray)
WASHINGTON, February 10, 1926—7 p.m.
39. Central News despatch from Hongkong via London published today
reports attack by students and Bolshevist sympathizers on American
Presbyterian mission in Kachek, Hainan Island, Kwangtung. Please cable
brief report.
KELLOGG
