500.C114/445j [Document 24] The Dominican Chargé (Vasquez) to the Secretary of State [Translation26 ] WASHINGTON, August 30, 1926. MR. SECRETARY OF STATE: In reply to your note of February 12, last, I have the honor to inform Your Excellency that by a telegram received on this date I am instructed to advise your Department that the Dominican Government will vote for the adhesion of the United States to the Protocol of Signature and Bylaws of the Court of International Justice, with the five reservations adopted in the Senate resolution, and that instructions to that effect have been sent to the Dominican delegate in the Assembly of the League of Nations. I avail myself [etc.] M. L. VASQUEZ 26 File translation revised.
