893.05/84: Telegram [Document 808] The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State PEKING, January 4, 1927—4 p.m. [Received January 4—10:17 a.m.] 5. American consul general at Shanghai reported January 3d that rendition of Mixed Court was effected by exchange of notes dated December 31st31a and that first session of Provisional Court would be held January 4th. MACMURRAY 31a For the English text of the exchange of notes on Dec. 31, 1926, see The Municipal Gazette (official organ of the Council for the Foreign Settlement of Shanghai), vol. XX, No. 1064 (Feb. 18, 1927), p. 47; cf. The China Year Book, 1928, p. 468.
