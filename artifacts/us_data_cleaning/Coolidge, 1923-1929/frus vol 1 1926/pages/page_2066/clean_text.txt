The contention of our Government has been that it was solely with a view to meeting this limited purpose that the diplomatic body is entrusted with a degree of supervision over the funds which is obviously of a purely fiduciary character and on the principle originally laid down by the Department’s instruction above quoted and now accepted by all other nationalities.
