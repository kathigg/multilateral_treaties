It was agreed that the official language of the Conference should be Chinese and English, but that any Delegate who so preferred could speak in French. The Rules of Procedure were then taken up and, after discussion, were adopted with slight amendments. A copy of the Rules of Procedure as adopted may be found on p. 23 of the Minutes of the Committee on Programme and Procedure, October 27, 1925.
