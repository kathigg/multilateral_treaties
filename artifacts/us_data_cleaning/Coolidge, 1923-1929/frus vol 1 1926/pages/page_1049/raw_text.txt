723.2515/2293: Telegram
[Document 289]
The Secretary of State to the Ambassador in Peru (Poindexter)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, May 15, 1926—noon.
50. Your No. 58, May 14, 9 a.m. This morning I called in Ambassador
Velarde, who said he had not yet received instructions. In your interview
with President Leguía did he confirm my understanding of his proposal as I
set it forth in my No. 49, May 12, 2 p.m.? Did President explain what
disposition is thought of for city of Arica? I do not understand what he
means by formula of negotiation: “Peru ratifies promise made to Bolivia to
grant her in the plebiscitary territory a corridor to the sea.” Please cable
reply as soon as possible.
KELLOGG
