5. There is no protest from the Chinese delegates against the participation of
foreign powers in evolving plan for a debt consolidation. Principal
arguments have arisen among the powers themselves as to the rates of
interest and as to what debts should be included.
The conferences respecting debt consolidation were continued until the
British delegates refused longer to sit, and the two members representing
them in this phase of conference work left for home (see our telegram
number 35, May 4, 6 p.m.).75
