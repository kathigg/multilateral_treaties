393.1162/21: Telegram
[Document 577]
The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, July 19, 1926—1 p.m.
[Received July 19—6:47 a.m.]
290. My 186, April 20, 11 a.m.13 Following from American consul general
at Canton:
“July 18, noon. Reliable information just received that Chinese
have taken over American Baptist hospital at Wuchow which is
now full of sick and wounded soldiers. Further details later.”
For the Minister:
MAYER
13 Not printed.
