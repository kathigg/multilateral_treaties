The Albanian Minister (Konitza) to the Secretary of State
WASHINGTON, August 20, 1926.
SIR: I have the honor to confirm that my Government has approved the
United States reservations for adherence to the Permanent Court of
International Justice. The Albanian delegate to the League of Nations has
received instructions in accordance with this decision.
I beg [etc.]
FAIK KONITZA
