ARTICLE 81 A health officer and at least two guards of the sanitary service shall board the vessel and accompany her to Port Said. Their duty shall be to prevent communications and see to the execution of the prescribed measures during the passage through the canal. ARTICLE 82 All embarkations, landings, and transshipments of passengers or cargo are forbidden during the passage through the Suez Canal. However, passengers may embark at Suez or Port Said in quarantine. ARTICLE 83 Vessels passing through in quarantine shall make the trip from Suez to Port Said or vice versa, without lying up. In case of stranding or of being compelled to lie up, the necessary operations shall be performed by the personnel on board, all communications with the employees of the Suez Canal Company being avoided. ARTICLE 84 When troops are conveyed through the canal on suspicious or infected vessels passing through in quarantine, the trip shall be made in the daytime only.
