The Minister in Honduras (Summerlin) to the Secretary of State
TEGUCIGALPA, June 25, 1926—11 a.m.
[Received 2:30 p.m.]
43. The Foreign Office telegraphed to the Chargé d’Affaires of Honduras at
Panama June 22, prior to the receipt of your telegram No. 21, June 22, 6
p.m., requesting a report regarding Trejo’s reported actions.
The Minister for Foreign Affairs has expressed regrets. He states that Trejo
was not acting under instructions, that disavowal will be published this
afternoon and that Trejo has ceased to be the [delegate of Honduras].
SUMMERLIN
