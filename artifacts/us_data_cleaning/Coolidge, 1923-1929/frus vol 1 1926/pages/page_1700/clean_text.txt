893.113 Airplanes/1: Telegram [Document 608] The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State PEKING, July 30, 1926—5 p.m. [Received July 30—10 a.m.] 309. My telegram 162, April 3, 2 p.m. I am reliably informed that the British Government has recently removed commercial aeroplanes from the list of articles procured under the arms embargo and that Avro machines are now being offered for sale in China by representatives of British interests. MACMURRAY
