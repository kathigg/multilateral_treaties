(5) Program which is being pursued and which I outline below for your
information is as follows:
(a) Decision on pending cross appeals will not be rendered until
further notice.
(b) Simultaneously with this message to you I am sending
instructions to Ambassadors Collier and Poindexter to discuss
present situation confidentially and personally with Foreign
Ministers of Chile and Peru respectively, and to leave with them
an identic memorandum.99
(6) Foregoing has approval of both General Pershing and Mr. Hughes.1
