500.A15/219: Telegram [Document 68] The Ambassador in Belgium (Phillips) to the Secretary of State [Paraphrase] BRUSSELS, April 17, 1926—noon. [Received April 17—11:04 a.m.] 33. Your 93, April 12, via Paris. Belgian Foreign Office indicates a disposition not to adopt any procedure which would cause American Government difficulties at forthcoming meeting of the Preparatory Commission in Geneva. Belgian Government does not desire postponement of meeting. PHILLIPS
