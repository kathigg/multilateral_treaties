9. Inasmuch as startlingly rapid developments have taken place in
the situation since my telegram 566, November 20,91 seeming to
rule out every alternative from consideration with the exception
of the powers granting the Washington surtaxes of their own
volition immediately and unconditionally, I am not making any
other reply to your 278, November 23.
