(vi) A Court of appeal shall be established in connection
with the Provisional Court to deal with criminal cases
which directly affect the peace and order of the
Settlement and with mixed criminal cases. The
President of the Provisional Court shall act concurrently
as President of the Court of Appeal. No appeal shall be
allowed in cases in which the penalty is below the
maximum imprisonment of the fifth degree nor in cases
under the Land Regulations and Bye Laws of the
International Settlement.
