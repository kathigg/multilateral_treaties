rather difficult task of preparing the ground for the negotiations I have the

Trusting that the United States Government fully realizing Austria’s actual
situation and need will readily support steps leading towards an early return
of Austrian property by an Act of Congress, I have the honor to request
Your Excellency to kindly have me advised at Your earliest convenience as
to the person or persons, with whom I may take up the matter in question.
Accept [etc.]
EDGAR PROCHNIK
