500.A15/242a [Document 72] The Secretary of State to the Minister in Switzerland (Gibson) WASHINGTON, April 23, 1926. SIR: The President has instructed me to inform you of his desire that you should be in charge of the American representation on the Preparatory Commission which is to meet in Geneva on May 18, 1926 to consider questions relating to the limitation of armaments. You will be assisted by: From the Department of State: Mr. Allen W. Dulles, Mr. Dorsey Richardson; From the War Department: Major General Dennis E. Nolan, Brigadier General H. A. Smith, Major George V. Strong; From the Navy Department: Rear Admiral Hilary P. Jones, Rear Admiral Andrew T. Long, Captain Adolphus Andrews. Mr. Alan F. Winslow, Secretary of the American Legation at Berne, will act as Secretary to the American Representation.
