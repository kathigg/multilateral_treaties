723.2515/2434: Telegram
[Document 326]
The Consul at Arica (Von Tresckow) to the Secretary of State
[Paraphrase]
ARICA, June 11, 1926—2 p.m.
[Received 3:40 p.m.]
From Lassiter. Your telegram June 11, 9 a.m. It might not be amiss to
intimate to Ambassador Cruchaga that if Chilean Commissioner will
propose suspension of all plebiscitary activities during period of good
offices and will likewise propose postponement of Commission’s action on
motions to consummate as well as on motions to terminate the plebiscite,
the resolution which embodies such proposals will receive my support.
Lassiter.
VON TRESCKOW
