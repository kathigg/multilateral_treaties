Dairen, Mukden, Harbin, Tientsin, and back to Peking. South China has
been eliminated from the itinerary because of the hostile attitude of the
Canton Government. The Commission accordingly left Peking last night for
Hankow and is expected to return by June 10. I was unable to go on account
of the Tariff Conference and sent Mr. Jacobs in my place.
I do not anticipate that there will be any great delay in the preparation of the
report and recommendations and believe that the Commission will complete
its labors and adjourn before July 1.
