4. This morning I received the following from Mr. Freyre.
“In compliance with instructions from my Government to the
effect that according to the understanding with the Government of
the United States I should take steps at once looking to the
suspension of the plebiscitary proceedings, I have today given
instructions to the Peruvian members of the registration boards
not to concur thereto.”
We now have reached the impasse in which one party refuses to go ahead
on account of its acceptance of good offices and the other insists on
proceeding despite the acceptance of good offices.
VON TRESCKOW
