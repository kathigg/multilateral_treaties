893.512/460: Telegram
[Document 703]
The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State
[Paraphrase]
PEKING, December 9, 1926—noon.
[Received December 9—7:54 a.m.]
606. My 598 of December 4th, 4 p.m.
1. I am informed by the British Chargé that the British
Government 
has 
authorized 
action 
toward 
immediate
implementing of Washington Treaty and instructed him that it
considers that action as suggested in my 598 is an obvious and
necessary next step.
2. He further gave me to understand that his Government while
gladly approving this way out of the dilemma is resolved
nevertheless in case that that course of action should prove
unsuccessful, to compound with the Cantonese in the matter of
the illegal surtaxes.
MACMURRAY
