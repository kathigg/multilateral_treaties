The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, July 30, 1926—7 p.m.
[Received July 31—12:56 a.m.]
311. Legation’s 304, July 26, noon. Following telegram has been received
from the American consul general at Canton:
“July 29, 2 p.m. Referring to telegram of July 23, noon.
Cantonese authorities now predict capture of Hankow and
Wuchang in 10 days. General Chiang of Whampoa Cadets has left
Canton for the Hunan front.”
MACMURRAY
