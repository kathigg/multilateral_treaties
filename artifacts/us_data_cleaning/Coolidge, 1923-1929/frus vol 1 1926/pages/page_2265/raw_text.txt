893.74/653: Telegram
[Document 812]
The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, January 16, 1926—noon.
[Received January 16—6:09 a.m.]
28. My telegram number 25, January 14, 10 a.m. I am reliably informed
that Japanese Minister addressed to the Chinese Government, January 14th,
a note protesting against transaction of commercial business with France by
French wireless station at Yünnanfu, and with Germany by station at
Mukden, reasserting claim to 30-year monopoly.
MACMURRAY
