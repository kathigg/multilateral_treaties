893.512/423: Telegram
[Document 676]
The Chargé in China (Mayer) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, October 23, 1926—7 p.m.
[Received October 23—9:45 a.m.]
507. Department’s 240, October 22nd. In expressing preference for draft
formula set forth in my 462, October 8th and 477, October 14th, am I to
understand that Department is unwilling to join in draft formula presented
in paragraph 2 of my 497, October 20th?
MAYER
