819.415C/–: Telegram [Document 127] The Secretary of State to the Minister in Panama (South) WASHINGTON, April 24, 1926—2 p.m. 33. Your despatch No. 678, April 2, 1925. Department assumes congress to which invitation refers will be of a purely ceremonial and commemorative nature and that no discussion of international matters is contemplated. Discreetly verify and report by cable. KELLOGG
