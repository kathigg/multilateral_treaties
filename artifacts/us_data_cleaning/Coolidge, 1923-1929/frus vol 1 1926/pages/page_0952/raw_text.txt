I am asking these questions so that I may know as exactly as possible the
position in which Arbitrator would be placed in predicating action upon
alleged defaults. It is of gravest importance that any action taken by
Arbitrator should have impregnable foundation in verifiable data that
cannot be assailed successfully. As far as possible I wish to know present
state of the record on all cardinal points before deciding on definite
instructions, should good offices fail.
