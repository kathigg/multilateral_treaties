Treaty Series No. 762 [Document 118] Procès-Verbal of the Deposit of Ratifications of the International Sanitary Contention by Czechoslovakia [Translation] In accordance with the provisions of Article 170 of the International Sanitary Convention signed at Paris June 21, 1926, the Minister of the Czechoslovak Republic this day called at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the French Republic and deposited the instrument of ratifications of that International Act by His Excellency the President of the Czechoslovak Republic. The instrument being, upon examination, found to be in good and due form, has been entrusted to the Government of the French Republic to be kept in its archives. A certified copy of this procès-verbal shall be sent to the contracting Powers. Done at Paris March 28, 1928. [SEAL] A. BRIAND [SEAL] STEFAN OSUSKY
