Doc. and Doc. 482] Sept. 15 Memorandum by the Chief of the Division of Far Eastern Affairs Conversation between the Italian Ambassador and the Secretary, in which the Ambassador inquired as to U. S. attitude toward cooperation with Great Britain in a firmer policy in China; the Secretary’s statement that, while naval forces in Far East have standing instructions to cooperate with friendly powers in protecting life in emergencies, this Government not only did not contemplate joint action, but that there did not seem to be any occasion for it at present.
