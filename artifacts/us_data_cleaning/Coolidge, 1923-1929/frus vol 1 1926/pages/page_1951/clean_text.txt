893.512/424: Telegram [Document 680] The Secretary of State to the Chargé in China (Mayer) WASHINGTON, October 25, 1926—6 p.m. 245. Your 510, October 25, 9 a.m. Department assumes that protest against taxes instituted by Canton authorities at Peking and at Canton will of course cover situation at Swatow which it is understood is within the zone mentioned in the mandate of the Canton authorities instituting the tax. KELLOGG
