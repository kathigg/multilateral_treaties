3. British Minister asked me to inform you of this and to inquire whether
our Government would be disposed to join in such action even though
American interests are concerned with the salt revenues only to the extent
of partial security for their share of Hukuang loan. I consented to do so,
although informing him that I did not anticipate my Government would be
prepared to use its forces at Tientsin for any purposes other than those
incidental to its mission of keeping open the railroad or in case of
emergency for the protection of life and property.
