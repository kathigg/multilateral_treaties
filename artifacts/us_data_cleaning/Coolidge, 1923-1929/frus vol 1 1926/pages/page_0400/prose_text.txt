In the foregoing definition the term “organized military forces” should be construed to mean persons equipped and formed into groups under the direction and control of a central authority to maintain and protect national rights through force of arms. The term “materiel” should be construed to embrace generally the arms of various calibers, ammunition, means of transportation and communication, supplies, accessories, et cetera, necessary to the operation of the forces.
