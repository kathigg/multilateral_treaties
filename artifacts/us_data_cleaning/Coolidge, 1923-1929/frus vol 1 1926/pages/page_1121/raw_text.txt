As I pointed out in my earlier message to you today, your resolution should
not be introduced until and unless issue is crowded to that point by Edwards
and no other course is left open to you. I am counting upon you to exercise
utmost discretion, introducing your resolution only through absolute
necessity. A great deal may depend upon a few days’ delay.
KELLOGG
