January 27, 1926, gave its advice and consent to the adherence on the part

Powers signatory to the Protocol of the conditions, reservations and
understandings contained in the Senate Resolution, a certified true copy of
which is attached.
The Government of the United States of America therefore desires to
ascertain whether the Imperial Ethiopian Government will accept the
conditions, reservations and understandings contained in the Resolution as a
part and a condition of the adherence of the United States to the said
Protocol and Statute.
WASHINGTON, November 12, 1926.
