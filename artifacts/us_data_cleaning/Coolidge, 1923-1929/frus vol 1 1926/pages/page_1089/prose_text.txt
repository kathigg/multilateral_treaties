I am inclined, however, to approve your suggestion with reference to Secretary Hoover, and I shall take up matter with him at once. 3. There is only one way whereby Chile can avoid disastrous consequences otherwise inevitable, and that is (1) by instructing Edwards to arrange with General Lassiter to hold up issue on plebiscite, and then (2) by modifying her intransigent attitude in the negotiations here in Washington. The Government of Chile will have to take both these measures quickly or it will have to accept full responsibility for consequences. 4.
