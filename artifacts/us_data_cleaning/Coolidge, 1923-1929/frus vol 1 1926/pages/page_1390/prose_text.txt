The Ministers in reply wish to state that they did not contemplate either the use of the foreign garrison at Tientsin or the employment of naval landing forces to ensure compliance with the five demands embodied in their notification. The five Ministers, who are hopeful that the exclusion of the Tsingtau flotilla from entering the channel will make easy the compliance of the Kuominchun with the demands made upon them, consider it highly undesirable to make use of land forces and would not be prepared to seek from their Governments, except as a last resort, the necessary authorization to that end.”
