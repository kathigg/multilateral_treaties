It is my desire, therefore, that at earliest possible moment you speak with President Leguía; without suggesting any definite settlement, set forth the above considerations, but do not give him any note or memorandum, in endeavor to have him accept in principle division of the territory. Time is running against Peru, for extension of period of registration will soon expire; it is imperative, therefore, that you immediately urge the President to accept this solution.
