7. We now have Sacramento and two destroyers at Shanghai. Barker, Bone and
Whipple with 2 officers and 100 marines left Cavite January 17th and are due at
Shanghai January 20th. Admiral McVay now at Shanghai stated at 1 o’clock today:
“Six destroyers sufficient for present needs. Subject to agreement Tsingtau
authorities 10,000 interned soldiers will be sent there today and tomorrow on
board foreign vessels. Conditions Nanking, Cliinkiang, Shanghai quiet but
with general feeling of unrest.”
British have two large cruisers and also river gunboats at Shanghai.
 
8. There are large concentrations of Chinese troops in and near Peking, Tientsin,

9. I intend sending an assistant military attaché to Shanghai by the first available
steamer. Train service very irregular.
