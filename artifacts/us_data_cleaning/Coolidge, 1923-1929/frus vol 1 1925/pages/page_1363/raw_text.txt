[Document 586]
The Chargé in China (Mayer) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, June 15, 1925—2 p.m.
[Received June 15—9:50 a.m.]
229. My 226, June 14, 4 p.m. Consul general at Hankow reports British and Japanese
consulates and British municipal building at Kiukiang looted and bank of Taiwan
burned. State of affairs in Chungking is serious and situation at Hankow is becoming
more tense.
MAYER
