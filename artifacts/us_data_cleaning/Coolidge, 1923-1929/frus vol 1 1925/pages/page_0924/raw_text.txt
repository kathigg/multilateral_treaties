875.6363/157: Telegram
[Document 333]
The Secretary of State to the Minister in Albania (Grant-Smith)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, February 4, 1925—6 p.m.
6. The Standard Oil Company of New York informs the Department that it has received
word of renewed activity on the part of Anglo-Persian in Albania. The company is
disposed to send a representative to Albania should the situation warrant. Please make a
brief telegraphic report.
HUGHES
