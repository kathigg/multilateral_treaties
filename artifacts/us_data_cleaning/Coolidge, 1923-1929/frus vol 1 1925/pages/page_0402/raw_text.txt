800.51 W 89France/132: Telegram
[Document 96]
The Secretary of State to the Ambassador in France (Herrick)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, June 23, 1925—6 p.m.
235. Your 336, June 18, 6 p.m. Reference your first paragraph. I would be glad if you
can find it possible to suggest that in speech on July 3 intention be publicly announced
of sending commission to Washington to enter upon debt-funding negotiations, and that
an early date be set for commission’s departure.
KELLOGG
