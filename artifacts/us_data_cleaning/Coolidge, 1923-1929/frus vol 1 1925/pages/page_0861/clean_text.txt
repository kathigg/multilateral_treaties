721.2315/180: Telegram [Document 298] The Ambassador in Peru (Poindexter) to the Secretary of State LIMA, March 3, 1925—noon. [Received 12:48 p.m.] 12. Your last cable. Peruvian Government promises to forward immediately following modifications procès-verbal as substitute for Dr. Velarde’s proposed statement: “Dr. Velarde then stated that he was also authorized by his Government to accept the friendly suggestion which the Secretary of State had just made in the sense that his Government would immediately advise the Peruvian Congress thereof—repeating at the same time its recommendation that it approve the boundary treaty with Colombia.[”] Instructions in other respects to remain as previously given. POINDEXTER
