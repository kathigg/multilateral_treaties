893.00/6876: Telegram
[Document 461]
The Consul at Mukden (Sokobin) to the Secretary of State
MUKDEN, December 24, 1925—5 p.m.
[Received 8:42 p.m.]
It is reported that Marshal Chang has completely defeated Kuo.44 Most of the forces of
Kuo have surrendered. Kuo fled.
SOKOBIN
44 Kuo Sung-ling, commander of Manchurian forces attempting to overthrow Chang
Tso-lin.
