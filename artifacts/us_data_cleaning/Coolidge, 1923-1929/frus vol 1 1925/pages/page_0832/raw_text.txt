721.2315/154: Telegram
[Document 275]
The Ambassador in Brazil (Morgan) to the Secretary of State
[Paraphrase]
RIO DE JANEIRO, February 6, 1925—noon.
[Received 2:47 p.m.]
10. The Foreign Office has accepted the Apaporis-Tabatinga line as establishing the
boundary between Brazil and Colombia, and will agree to the free navigation of the
Amazon and the other rivers common to both countries. This information will be
officially transmitted to the Department as soon as the President gives his approval. It
is requested that no announcement be made at present.
MORGAN
