875.01/265: Telegram
[Document 331]
The Secretary of State to the Minister in Albania (Grant-Smith)
WASHINGTON, January 31, 1925—6 p.m.
5. 1. Your No. 8, January 29, 5 p.m. and No. 6, January 25, 9 a.m. You are authorized to
extend recognition to the new régime in Albania and to acknowledge the receipt of
official notification of establishment of the republic.
2. Department’s No. 1, January 9, 7 p.m. and your despatch No. 362, December 12.5
When above action has been taken you are authorized leave of absence as requested.
3. Telegraph action taken.
HUGHES
5 Neither printed.
