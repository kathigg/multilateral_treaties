500.A12/41: Telegram [Document 10] The Ambassador in Japan (Bancroft) to the Secretary of State TOKYO, February 20, 1925—6 p.m. [Received 10 p.m.] 34. My 33, February 20, 4 p.m.13 Have just seen United Press cablegram that President Coolidge has received consent of England, Japan and Italy and is waiting for consent of France before calling disarmament conference. BANCROFT 13 Not printed.
