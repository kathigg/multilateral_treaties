Undated Memorandum by the Secretary of State of a Conversation With
the Colombian Minister, October 31, 1925
Ecuadoran protest to United States that Colombian- Peruvian
treaty would make it more difficult for Ecuador to agree with
Peru as to the boundary between those two countries. Secretary’s
inability to see how treaty could affect Ecuadoran interests or to
offer U. S. good offices unless requested by all countries
involved.
