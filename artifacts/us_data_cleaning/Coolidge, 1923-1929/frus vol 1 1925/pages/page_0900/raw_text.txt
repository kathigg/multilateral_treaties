718.1915/758: Telegram
[Document 322]
The Minister in Costa Rica (Davis) to the Secretary of State
[Paraphrase]
SAN JOSÉ, January 7, 1926—10 a.m.
[Received 1:53 p.m.]
2. Department’s telegram number 29 dated December 29, 1925, 7 p.m.
Costa Rica has rejected Fabrega’s counterproposal for rectification of the boundary and
his proposal to postpone the negotiations until May.
I shall forward on January 9 a memorandum on the final conference between President
Jiménez and Fabrega.46
DAVIS
46 Not printed.
