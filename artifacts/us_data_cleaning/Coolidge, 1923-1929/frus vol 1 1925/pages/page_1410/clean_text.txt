893.00/6292: Telegram [Document 620] The Chargé in China (Mayer) to the Secretary of State PEKING, June 19, 1925—5 p.m. [Received June 19—8:50 a.m.] 236. Following from consul general at Canton: “June 18, 1 p.m. General strike against foreigners imminent. I am warning American women and children to leave and the men who remain to be prepared for immediate departure in event of danger.” MAYER
