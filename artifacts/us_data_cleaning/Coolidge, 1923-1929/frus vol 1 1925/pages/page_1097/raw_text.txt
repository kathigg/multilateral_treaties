The Minister in China (Schurman) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, January 13, 1925—8 p.m.
[Received January 13—9:35 a.m.]
22. My telegram number 14, January 12, 4 p.m.10 Announcing his departure from
Tientsin for Mukden, Chang Tso-lin made following significant statement: “All my
troops will also be instructed to return to Mukden successively.”
SCHURMAN
10 Not printed.
