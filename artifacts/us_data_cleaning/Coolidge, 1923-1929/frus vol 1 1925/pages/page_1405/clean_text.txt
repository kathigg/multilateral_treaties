893.51/4861: Telegram [Document 618] The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State PEKING, November 23, 1925—10 a.m. [Received November 23—1:01 a.m.] 493. American consul, Swatow, states that it is reported Canton Government contemplates taking over Maritime Customs, Swatow. He asks whether action similar to that of the consul general, Canton, on the same question in 1923 should be taken.58 Please instruct. MACMURRAY 58 See Foreign Relations, 1923, vol. I, pp. 551 ff.
