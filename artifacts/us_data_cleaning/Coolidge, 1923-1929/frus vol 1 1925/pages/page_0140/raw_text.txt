From the British Chargé
Opinion of the British Government that the powers should
indicate an unwillingness to discuss particular reforms or to
review treaty relations until order has been restored and the
Chinese Government has evidenced its determination to repress
antiforeign agitations and enforce protection of foreigners;
unfavorable attitude toward a promise to expedite the meeting of
the Commission on Extraterritoriality.
