723.2515/1679: Telegram
[Document 247]
The Secretary of State to the Ambassador in Chile (Collier)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, November 19, 1925—7 p.m.
74. Embassy’s No. 118, November 12, 3 p.m. I should be glad to have you discuss
matter in friendly informal way with Chilean Minister for Foreign Affairs. This will
give him opportunity of his own volition to correct the existing abuses. Continue to
keep Department informed.
KELLOGG
