875.6363/160: Telegram
[Document 337]
The Secretary of State to the Minister in Albania (Grant-Smith)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, February 10, 1925—3 p.m.
7. The Department has information that Mr. Sheffield10 is shortly to leave the United
States and will proceed at once to Tirana, where he should arrive about February 28th.
HUGHES
10 E. S. Sheffield, representative of the Standard Oil Co. of New York.
