The Chargé in France (Whitehouse) to the Secretary of State
PARIS, February 6, 1925—1 p.m.
[Received February 6—11:40 a.m.]
108. My 75, January 21, 3 p.m. British memorandum was adopted by the Conference
this morning, subject to my reservation that I had as yet received no instructions.
WHITEHOUSE
