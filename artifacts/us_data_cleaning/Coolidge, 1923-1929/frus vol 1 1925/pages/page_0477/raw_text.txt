463.00 R 29/171: Telegram
[Document 141]
The Ambassador in France (Herrick) to the Secretary of State
PARIS, March 3, 1925—noon.
[Received March 3—11:02 a.m.]
150. Your 99, February 24, 4 p.m. Memorandum was presented to the Conference of
Ambassadors at its 275th meeting this morning. No comment was made but it was
reserved for future discussion.
HERRICK
