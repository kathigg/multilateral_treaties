The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, November 9, 1925—1 p.m.
[Received November 9—5:45 a.m.]
478. My 477, November 6, 4 p.m. Following from Swatow:
“November 7, 12 noon. Chiang Kai-shek arrived yesterday with several
thousand troops, quiet.”
MACMURRAY
