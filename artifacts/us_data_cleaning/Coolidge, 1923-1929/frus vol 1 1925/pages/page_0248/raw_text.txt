500.A14/242: Telegram
[Document 37]
The Secretary of State to the Chairman of the American Delegation
(Burton)
WASHINGTON, May 16, 1925—3 p.m.
21. Your 31, May 15, 3 p.m. The laws of the United States applicable to American
nationals in extraterritorial countries under Section 4086 of the Revised Statutes would
without provision to that effect in the Convention include laws which may be passed to
carry out terms of Convention primarily in this country. However, Department would
not object to provision substantially as suggested by you. The meaning would be
clearer if words “under legislation” were changed to “under their respective laws.”
KELLOGG
