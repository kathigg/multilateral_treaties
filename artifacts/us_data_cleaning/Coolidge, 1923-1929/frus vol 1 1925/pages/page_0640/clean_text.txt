Among the Peruvian governmental publications relating to the award and plebiscite are: Arbitration between Peru and Chile: The Case of Peru, etc. (Washington, 1923); Appendix to the Case of Peru, etc. (Washington, 1923); The Counter Case of Peru, etc. (Washington, 1924); and Appendix to the Counter Case of Peru, etc. (Washington, 1924).
