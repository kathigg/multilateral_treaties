800.51 W 89Rumania/55: Telegram
[Document 113]
The Minister in Rumania (Jay) to the Secretary of State
[Extract—Paraphrase]
BUCHAREST, April 6, 1925—noon.
[Received 11:49 p.m.]
29.
. . . . . . . . . . . . . .
It is my personal impression, which I should like to have officially confirmed as far as
possible through the Rumanian Legation in Washington, that the Rumanian
Government, in despair at failures in the European markets, is making a sincere bid for
American financial support, with corresponding advantages to us. For this reason I do
not recommend too vigorous action at present, as at last the Government seems to
realize absolute necessity of obtaining our good will.
JAY
