121.5793/3 [Document 183] The Consul General at Hongkong (Tredwell) to the Secretary of State HONGKONG, September 22, 1925. [Received October 26.] No. 24 SIR: I have the honor to transmit herewith copy of a communication received to-day from the Colonial Secretary of the Colonial Government of Hongkong,78 in which I am informed that His Excellency the Governor has withdrawn the provisional recognition which was accorded to Mr. H. C. Anderson, Assistant Customs Attaché, at this port.79 I have [etc.] ROGER CULVER TREDWELL 78 Not printed. 79 The consul in charge at Hongkong had been instructed on May 5 to notify the local authorities of the appointment of Mr. Anderson as assistant customs attaché at Hongkong (file No. 121.5793/a).
