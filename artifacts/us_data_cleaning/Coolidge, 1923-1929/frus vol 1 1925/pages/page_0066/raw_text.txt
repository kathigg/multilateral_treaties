To the French Ambassador
Views of U. S. Government that subjects to be discussed at the
International Telegraph Conference in Paris should not include
questions relating to radio, as such questions will be considered

Washington. U. S. willingness, in response to French inquiry, to
postpone the Washington conference till 1926.
