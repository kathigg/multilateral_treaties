893.00/6825: Telegram [Document 452] The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State PEKING, December 7, 1925—10 a.m. [Received December 7—3:04 a.m.] 517. My 514, December 4, 1 p.m.38 Following from American consul, Mukden: “December 6, 11 a.m. Chang has suffered complete debacle. Young Chang retreated to a place very near to Mukden; position of Chang absolutely hopeless. There is a general exodus from the city. I do not expect any danger to any foreigners. Have heard Marshal will leave today.” MACMURRAY 38 Not printed.
