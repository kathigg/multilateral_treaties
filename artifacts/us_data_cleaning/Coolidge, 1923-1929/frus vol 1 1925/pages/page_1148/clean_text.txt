893.00/6838: Telegram [Document 453] The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State PEKING, December 9, 1925—9 a.m. [Received December 9—2:24 a.m.] 521. My 517, December 7, 10 a.m. Following from American consul, Mukden: “December 8, 1 p.m. Chang and his son are determined to make hard fight. Young Chang has a strong position on the Liao. There is still a small possibility that the Marshal can hold on. The city quiet.” MACMURRAY
