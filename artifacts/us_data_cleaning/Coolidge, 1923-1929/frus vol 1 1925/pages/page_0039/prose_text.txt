Undated Memorandum by the Secretary of State of a Conversation With the German Ambassador, March 16, 1925 Substance of proposition made by Germany to France and Great Britain for a joint security pact to secure France, including proposal that France’s security be guaranteed to United States. Secretary’s statement that United States is not authorized to join in any guarantee, nor is question of Germany’s joining the League of Nations a matter for U. S. comment or suggestion.
