800.51 W 89Czechoslovakia/90: Telegram [Document 68] The Minister in Czechoslovakia (Einstein) to the Secretary of State PRAGUE, May 2, 1925—1 p.m. [Received 4:55 p.m.] 12. Your instruction number 252, April 4th. Presented note today.9 Dr. Beneš denies any intention of discriminating against the United States by arranging payment of socalled Nansen relief bonds and believes such impression is based on misunderstanding. Will write me in explanation. EINSTEIN 9 Note dated April 30.
