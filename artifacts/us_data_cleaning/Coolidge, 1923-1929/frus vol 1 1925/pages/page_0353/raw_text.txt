800.51 W 89Czeclioslovakia/92: Telegram
[Document 70]
The Minister in Czechoslovakia (Einstein) to the Secretary of State
PRAGUE, May 16, 1925—2 p.m.
[Received 3:30 p.m.]
16. Your number 11, May 5, 4 p.m.
1. Doctor Beneš admitted that he had forgotten his letter of June 25 [26],
1919; if he had remembered this he would have negotiated differently with
Great Britain.
2. He reaffirms the intention of Czechoslovakia to honor all her engagements.
3. On his return from Geneva where he goes tomorrow he will take up the
debt question with the Minister of Finance and give me early answer. My
impression is that he will settle relief bonds on the same terms as extended to
other governments.
EINSTEIN
