793.003 C 73/228: Telegram [Document 731] The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State PEKING, December 17, 1925—3 p.m. [Received December 17—9:09 a.m.] 532. Opening of Extraterritoriality Commission which should take place tomorrow will be delayed until arrival of Belgian, British, French and Italian Commissioners who are detained at Tientsin by the interruption of all means of communication in consequence of factional fighting now in progress. In spite of Chief Executive’s orders to both parties to facilitate arrangements for the operation of an international train it has not yet been possible to effectuate such an arrangement with Marshall Feng’s commander here. Repeated to Tokyo. MACMURRAY
