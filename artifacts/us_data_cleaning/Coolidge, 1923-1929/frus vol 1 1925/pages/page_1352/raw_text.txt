The Chargé in China (Mayer) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, June 4, 1925—noon.
[Received June 4—5:57 a.m.]
201. Following from consul, Changsha:
“June 3, 1 p.m. Students of all the schools in Changsha striking today
because of the May 30th affray in Shanghai. Many private rikishas
demolished last evening on the main streets. Boycott against British and
Japanese declared. The situation is serious but is under control.”
MAYER
