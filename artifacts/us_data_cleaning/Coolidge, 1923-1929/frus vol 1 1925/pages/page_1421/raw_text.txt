The Secretary of State to the Consul General at Canton (Jenkins)
WASHINGTON, July 1, 1925—6 p.m.
Your June 26, 11 a.m. You should tactfully decline to sit on proposed commission of
inquiry unless officially requested to do so by the British and French Consuls General
and unless common foreign interests would be furthered by your participation in which
case you should telegraph your views stating also the composition and defined objects
of the commission and await further instructions.
KELLOGG
