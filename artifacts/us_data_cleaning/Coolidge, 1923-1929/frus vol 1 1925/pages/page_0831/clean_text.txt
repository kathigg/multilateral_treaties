There would then be drawn up a procès-verbal of the meeting in four copies which would be signed by the Secretary of State, the Brazilian Chargé, the Colombian Minister, and the Peruvian Ambassador. This procedure would follow the method used to bring about the establishment of diplomatic relations between Colombia and Panama and the settlement of the boundary between those countries.3 As this method appears to offer an appropriate way of disposing of this difficulty, the Department trusts that it will be satisfactory to the Brazilian Government and hopes that you will be able to obtain the prompt agreement of that Government.
