875.6363/199: Telegram
[Document 352]
The Chargé in Albania (Kodding) to the Secretary of State
[Extract]
TIRANA, March 22, 1925—2 p.m.
[Received March 23—3:15 p.m.]
32. Department’s 15, March 17.
1. According to interpretation of Sheffield and Legation, Italian concession renders
50,000 hectares indefinitely unavailable for exploitation. Decree provides for choice
within 2 months of 50,000 hectares, from which 30,000 for exploitation may be chosen
within research period. Research period of 3 years, which may be extended by mutual
accord before deciding the time of official publication of ratification by Parliament of
concession. Decree valid for 20 years without ratification and for 60 years if ratified.
