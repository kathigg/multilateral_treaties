500.A 4 e/210: Telegram
[Document 685]
The Secretary of State to the Chargé in China (Mayer)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, April 27, 1925—1 p.m.
85. This Government believes that after the customs treaty comes into force following
its ratification by the French Government, it would be appropriate for the Chinese
Government to issue a call convening the Special Tariff Conference. This Government
would be willing to accept the tentative date of September 1, provided that date is also
convenient for the other governments which signed or have adhered to the treaty.
KELLOGG
