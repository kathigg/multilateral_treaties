121.5764/1 [Document 175] The Minister in Hungary (Brentano) to the Secretary of State BUDAPEST, July 18, 1925. [Received August 3.] No. 680 SIR: With reference to the Department’s instruction No. 940 of April 21, 1925,65 concerning agents of the Customs Division of the United States Treasury stationed abroad being regularly and officially attached to American Diplomatic Missions, I have the honor to transmit a copy of a note from the Foreign Office dated July 8, 1925.66 received yesterday, stating that Mr. James F. O’Neill, Customs Attaché, is placed on the Hungarian diplomatic list. Mr. O’Neill has been informed of this action. I have [etc.] THEODORE BRENTANO 65 See footnote 41, p. 227 [Pg. 227 is part of Doc. 162]. 66 Not printed.
