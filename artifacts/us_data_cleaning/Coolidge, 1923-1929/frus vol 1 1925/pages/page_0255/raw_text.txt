500.A14/294: Telegram
[Document 43]
The Secretary of State to the Chairman of the American Delegation
(Burton)
[Paraphrase]
WASHINGTON, June 4, 1925—[(?) p.m.]
51. Department’s No. 50, June 3, 7 p.m., last paragraph. As result of my consultation
with the President you are authorized, if in your opinion it is wise, to indicate that if
powers represented at present Conference, as well as other interested powers, feel that
moment is opportune to take further steps in this matter, the President will be glad to
extend invitation as you suggest. As I indicated in telegram referred to above, if the
President should call a further naval disarmament conference, he would probably make
the other a part of it as was done last time.
KELLOGG
