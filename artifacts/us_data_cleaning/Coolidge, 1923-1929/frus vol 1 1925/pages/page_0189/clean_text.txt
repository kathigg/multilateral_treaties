500.A12/68: Telegram [Document 17] The Chargé in France (Whitehouse) to the Secretary of State [Extract—Paraphrase] PARIS, October 23, 1925—6 p.m. [Received 11:55 p.m.] 510. Your 335 [385], October 22. Press comment is uniformly opposed to any conference on disarmament under the President’s auspices, whether held at Washington or at The Hague. . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
