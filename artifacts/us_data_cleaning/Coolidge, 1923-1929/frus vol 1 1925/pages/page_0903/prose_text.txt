The British Embassy at Washington under date of January 30, 1918, informed the Department that His Majesty’s Government understood that Swain’s Island was American territory and, accordingly, transmitted to the Department for consideration a copy of the evidence presented in support of the charges. The evidence was referred to the Secretary of the Navy, who caused an investigation to be made respecting the conditions obtaining on the island.
