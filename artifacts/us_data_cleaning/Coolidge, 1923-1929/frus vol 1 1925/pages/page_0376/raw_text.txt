800.51 W 89France/50: Telegram
[Document 82]
The Ambassador in France (Herrick) to the Secretary of State
[Paraphrase]
PARIS, January 1, 1925—5 p.m.
[Received 6:20 p.m.]
1. My 614, December 30, 7 p.m. I have requested both Herriot and Clémentel to say
nothing about the latter’s note, and believe it may be advisable, considering nature of
proposal, for Department to regard it as confidential.
Detailed newspaper rumor has already appeared in regard to the note, but I believe that
this is attributable to source which has since been stopped. I am informing press
correspondents that there is no new development.
HERRICK
