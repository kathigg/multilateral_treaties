893.00/6691: Telegram [Document 614] The Minister in China (MacMurray) to the Secretary of State PEKING, October 24, 1925—noon. [Received October 24—7:42 a.m.] 463. Following received from Canton: “Oct. 20, 4 p.m. Local government announces capture of Waichow by the Whampoa Cadets, but rumors persist city has been retaken by Chen Chiungming forces. Fighting in eastern Kwangtung unusually severe, local authorities admitting heavy losses in killed and wounded. Several hundred wounded have arrived in Canton.” MACMURRAY
