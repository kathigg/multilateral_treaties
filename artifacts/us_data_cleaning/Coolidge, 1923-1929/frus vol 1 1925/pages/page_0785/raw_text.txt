Tuesday I shall call up for final action motion presented yesterday calling for departure
from the province of officials who have been removed from public office unless they
have Commission’s permission to remain. There has been merely formal compliance
with demands for removal of officials, who have been uniformly honored and in some
instances have been promoted to higher civil positions and in others have been retained
for service in the province. Presence of officials guilty of acts inimical to fair plebiscite
cannot be permitted if purpose of their removal is not to be completely frustrated.
Pershing
VON TRESCKOW
