The Secretary of State to the Ambassador in Peru (Poindexter)
WASHINGTON, October 30, 1925—6 p.m.
62. Department advised by Colombian Minister that Colombian Government has
ratified Colombian-Peruvian Boundary Treaty of 1922, thus complying with its
obligations under Section 2 of the Procès Verbal of March 4, last.19
In the course of conversation today on other matters I informed the Peruvian
Ambassador of the above intimating that information that the Peruvian Congress had
done likewise would be received with pleasure. You may in your discretion say to
President Leguía that the definite termination of this matter would cause gratification to
this Government.
KELLOGG
19 This paragraph was telegraphed to Brazil, Oct. 30, 6 p.m., as Department’s telegram
No. 57 (file No. 721.2315/243b).
