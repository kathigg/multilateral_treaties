893.00/6248: Telegram [Document 601] The Chargé in China (Mayer) to the Secretary of State PEKING, June 9, 1925—9 a.m. [Received June 9—3:46 a.m.] 212. My 205, June 6, 4 p.m. The following telegram, 29 hours in transmission, has been received from the consul general at Canton: “June 7, 5 p.m. Referring to my telegram of June 5, 3 p.m. Fighting began about 4:30 p.m. on Saturday and still continues. All Americans safe so far.” MAYER
