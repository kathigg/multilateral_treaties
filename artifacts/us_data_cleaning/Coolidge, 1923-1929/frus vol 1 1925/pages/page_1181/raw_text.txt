The Chargé in China (Mayer) to the Secretary of State
PEKING, July 7, 1925—3 p.m.
[Received July 7—8:37 a.m.]
266. From MacMurray: Arrived today. Mayer will remain in charge pending
consultations with colleagues as to proposed presentation of letters of credence to Tuan
as Provisional Chief Executive.
MAYER
