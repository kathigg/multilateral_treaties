1062 Foreign Relations, 1977–1980, Volume IX Minister Weizman asked about Bob Strauss’ being in the re-election fight. The President said that he is good at it, and this was what he wanted to do. Minister Weizman said that maybe he should give Bob Strauss some ideas, since he ran the Likud election campaign two years ago. The President said that this was one of the great surprises in the world. Perhaps Begin will give the Minister a vacation so that he can come here to help Bob and him (the President). Minister Weizman said anytime. He has bet some cases of whiskey on the President, and did so when the polls were down. He would correct something the President said—“if” he wins. He would say “when” the President wins. The President said that he is pretty confident. Minister Weizman said that he appreciates that he cannot expect a substantial increase in aid. It is important to Israeli morale that the President took time to see him. The President said he will let Eppy know no later than Monday. Minister Weizman said he will be on Face the Nation Sunday.12 The President said he would watch it. He said that we are making good progress, in part because of what they are doing together. (The meeting ended at 12:51 p.m.) 12 December 30.
