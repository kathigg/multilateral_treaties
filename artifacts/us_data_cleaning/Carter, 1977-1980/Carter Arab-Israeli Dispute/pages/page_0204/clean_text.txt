August 8–September 17, 1978 169 Tamir, at Vance’s request, then unveiled several detailed maps5 of the West Bank and Gaza showing exactly what force dispositions Israel would require during the five-year transition period, and in most cases beyond. He also explained in detail the roles of the various Sinai air bases, force dispositions near Gaza (it would be unnecessary to retain forces within Gaza itself except for the military nahals), and the areas along the Jordan River required as permanent installations to defend against attacks from the east. Secretary Vance studied the maps carefully and listened to the briefing. He remarked that it would be very helpful if Weizman and Tamir would repeat this briefing for President Carter.
