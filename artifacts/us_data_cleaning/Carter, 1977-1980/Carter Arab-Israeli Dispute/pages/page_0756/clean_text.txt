December 17, 1978–March 26, 1979 721 with Dayan this evening.4 President is making major effort to persuade Begin to agree to positions on remaining issues as discussed with Sadat and Khalil at end of our visit to Egypt. Begin has called Cabinet meeting for tonight5 (after state dinner for President) which will probably last into early morning hours, and we expect to hear Israeli positions Monday morning. Until then, we will not be able to judge whether our efforts have been successful. If Cabinet approves reasonable positions, Pres. will ask Sec. to go to Cairo Monday.
