Letter From President Carter to Egyptian President Sadat and Israeli Prime Minister Begin1 Camp David, September 15, 1978 To Pres. Sadat & P.M. Begin: We are approaching the final stage of our negotiations. With your approval, I propose that today we receive your most constructive recommendations, that tomorrow (Saturday) be devoted to drafting efforts, and that we conclude the meeting at Camp David at some time during the following day. We will, at that time, issue a common statement to the press, drafted together. Additionally, we should agree not to make any further public statements prior to noon on Monday. Please let me know if you object to any of these proposals. J.C. 1 Source: Carter Library, National Security Affairs, Brzezinski Material, Country
