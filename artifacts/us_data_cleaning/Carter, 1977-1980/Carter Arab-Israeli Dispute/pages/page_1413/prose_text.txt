President Sadat and Prime Minister Begin also affirmed3 their strong conviction that the process which you had developed with them at Camp David is, in their words, “the only viable path toward comprehensive peace in the Middle East today.” I share that conviction and deeply believe that it is in the highest interest of the United States to press forward in the weeks and months ahead in order to conclude the negotiations at the earliest possible date. I have been honored to serve as your representative in these negotiations. Respectfully submitted, Sol M. Linowitz4 Personal Representative of the President for the Middle East Peace Negotiations
