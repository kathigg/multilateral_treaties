The President commented that he understood Linowitz had spent five hours with Begin. Ambassador Linowitz said Sadat did not want to take the draft, but he would give it to Ali tomorrow. After their meeting, Sadat had agreed with his proposal and took Linowitz out directly to talk to the press and announce agreement. The President said he was glad both leaders were constructive.
