Telegram From the Department of State to the Embassy in Israel1 Washington, September 27, 1978, 1710Z Tosec 110017/245637. Subject: Oral Message From the President on Settlements Issue. 1. Following is text of an oral message from the President to Begin for you to give to Begin in the course of your discussion2 with him on Camp David Agreement on question of settlements in the West Bank and Gaza.
