Memorandum From Robert Hunter, Gary Sick, William Odom, and Fritz Ermarth of the National Security Council Staff to the President’s Assistant for National Security Affairs (Brzezinski)1 Washington, July 16, 1980 SUBJECT Syria (U) We met, today, per your request, on CIA’s Alert Memorandum (Tab I). We focussed not on the prospects for Assad’s removal from power2—that is beyond our ability to predict—but on likely consequences and steps we can take now.
