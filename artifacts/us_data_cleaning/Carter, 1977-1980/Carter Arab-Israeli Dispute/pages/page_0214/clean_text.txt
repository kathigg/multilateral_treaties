Eilts, U.S. Ambassador to Egypt Denis Clift, Vice President’s Staff EGYPTIAN SIDE Mohamed Ibrahim Kamel, Minister of Foreign Affairs Boutros Ghali, Minister of State for Foreign Affairs Osama el-Baz, Under Secretary for Foreign Affairs Ahmed Maher, Director of the Cabinet of the Foreign Minister Ashraf Ghorbal, Egyptian Ambassador to the United States Nabil el-Araby, Director of Legal Department, Foreign Ministry Abdul Rauf el-Reedy, Director of Policy Planning, Foreign Ministry Abou el Gheite, Office of the Foreign Minister SUBJECT Egyptian Comments on U.S. Paper Kamel first expressed appreciation for the efforts of President Carter, the Secretary and the U.S. Delegation.
