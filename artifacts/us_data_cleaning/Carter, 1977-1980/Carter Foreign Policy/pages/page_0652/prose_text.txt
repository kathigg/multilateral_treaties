And I hope that every American will join in with me, not in a quiescent way, just observing what’s going on, but in an active way. And I particularly hope the news media will assess the details of the SALT agreement, the consequences of either passage or rejection, and let your voices be heard in the strongest possible way. “It transcends partisanship; it transcends the necessary objectivity of the news media toward politicians.
