Memorandum of Conversation1 Washington, March 11, 1978 SUBJECT SALT; South African Nuclear Test; Middle East; Yugoslavia; China; Environmental Modification (Part 2 of 2) PARTICIPANTS US USSR The Secretary Amb. Anatoliy Dobrynin Deputy Secretary Marshall D. Shulman Following a discussion of the Horn of Africa, Dobrynin raised several other topics. 1. SALT. Dobrynin asked if there had been any developments in the U.S. position on SALT issues. The Secretary said these matters had been worked on, and were still in the decision process. Dobrynin said that he had tried to be of help at a recent dinner given by the British Ambassador which was attended by many prominent Senators and Congressmen, most of whom were opponents of the SALT treaty.
