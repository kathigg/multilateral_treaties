SUMMARY: At a dinner meeting in New York, Giorgi A. Arbatov,
Director of the Institute on USA and Canada, filled in some back-
ground on Soviet reactions during and following our Moscow
meetings and discussed some possible terms for future SALT
negotiations.
I had dinner with Giorgi A. Arbatov, Director of the Institute on
USA and Canada, on April 10. His account of the reaction in Moscow to
our meetings largely confirmed information received from other
sources, but in some respects went further.
