End text. Vance 83. Memorandum of Conversation1 Washington, February 21, 1978 SUBJECT Horn, Arms Control, Middle East, Misc. PARTICIPANTS US USSR The Secretary Amb. Anatoly F. Dobrynin Marshall D. Shulman, S/MS Ambassador Dobrynin came in at our request. We discussed the following matters: 1. Horn. The Secretary reviewed the results of Aaron’s meetings with Mengistu. He indicated that Mengistu had repeated his assurance that Ethiopia had no territorial ambitions beyond its own border, but was determined to have the Somalis withdrawn from the Ogaden. Mengistu also indicated that any initiative at the present time would have to come through the OAU, and that emissaries had been sent to see Garba in Lagos.2 The Secretary reported that Mengistu had agreed to receive an Ambassador from the U.S. The supply of non-lethal equipment was also discussed. On the whole, the conversations had been successful in keeping the channels of communication open. 1 Source: Department of State, Office of the Secretariat Staff, Cyrus R.
