Washington, March 11, 1978
SUBJECT
Horn of Africa, Rhodesia (Part 1 of 2)
PARTICIPANTS
US
USSR
The Secretary
Amb. Anatoliy Dobrynin
Deputy Secretary
Marshall D. Shulman
Summary: Dobrynin came in at the Secretary’s request, primarily to
discuss the Horn. In response to the Secretary, he indicated some Soviet
willingness to influence Ethiopia against reprisals, but he maintained
that outside observers and Soviet/Cuban withdrawal were matters for
internal Ethiopian jurisdiction.
