([classification marking and handling restriction not declassified]) Summary: This report discusses political and military aspects of the Afghan situation as perceived by the Afghan guerrillas. It is divided into six general areas: (A) Soviet pacification/rapproachment efforts, (B) preparations for a protracted conflict, (C) counter-insurgency tactics, (D) feelings toward the Karmal government, (E) tacit support provided by the Government of Pakistan, and (F) relationships between guerrilla tribes and leaders. 8A. (U) Details: (1) ([classification marking and handling restriction not declassified]) Soviet pacification/rapproachment efforts. The guerrillas all expressed a strong hatred for Russians/Soviets.
