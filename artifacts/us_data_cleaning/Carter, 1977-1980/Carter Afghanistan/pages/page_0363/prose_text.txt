Hotline Message From President Carter to General Secretary Brezhnev1 Washington, December 28, 1979 Dear President Brezhnev: I want to insure that you have fully weighed the ramifications of the Soviet actions in Afghanistan, which we regard as a clear threat to the peace. You should understand that these actions could mark a fundamental and long-lasting turning point in our relations.
