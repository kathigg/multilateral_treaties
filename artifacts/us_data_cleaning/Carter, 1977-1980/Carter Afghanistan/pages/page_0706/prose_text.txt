Currently we have no firm evidence to confirm or deny support from either Pakistan or China. 6. (C) The tenor of the insurgency during the winter months far exceeded expectations. A lull was expected in both the insurgency and Soviet/Afghan Army operations in order to refit, retrain and plan operations for the upcoming summer months. However, this was not the case and as the weeks progressed the insurgency gained momentum, most recently manifested in the general strike and demonstrations in Kabul. Historically insurgent operations increase in the warmer months. This, in combination with the increasing support of urban populations, continued defections of Afghan Army personnel, innate hatred of the Russians and the relative successes enjoyed to date portend increased insurgent activity during the summer months. 7.
