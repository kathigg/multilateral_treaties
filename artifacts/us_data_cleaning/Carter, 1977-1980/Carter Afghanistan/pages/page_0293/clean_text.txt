Alert Memorandum Prepared in the Central Intelligence Agency1 [memorandum number not declassified] Washington, December 19, 1979 USSR-AFGHANISTAN2 The Soviets [2 lines not declassified] are building up other forces [1 line not declassified] near the Soviet-Afghan border. This indicates the USSR has significantly changed the nature of its military commitment in Afghanistan [1½ lines not declassified]. The buildup [2 lines not declassified] suggests that further augmentation there is likely soon, and that preparations for a much more substantial reinforcement may also be under way.3 ([classification marking and codeword not declassified]). [1 paragraph (8 lines) not declassified] Concurrent with the upgrading of Soviet forces at Bagram the Soviets began building up forces near the Afghan border.
