Washington, March 29, 1980
Moscow’s Terms for Withdrawal from Afghanistan
The Soviets have not rejected the notion of negotiation about
Afghanistan. But, to the extent that Moscow has developed and elabo-
rated its views, the stated Soviet terms for withdrawal from Afghani-
stan appear to be even stiffer now than they were on December 27.
