The Soviet Military Position in Afghanistan:
Problems and Prospects
([classification marking not declassified])
Key Judgments
After six months of fighting, the Soviets have not achieved any
lasting military success against the Afghan insurgency. A review of
operations to date shows that the 80,000 Soviet troops in Afghanistan
are no more able to control the countryside than the Afghan Army
was in 1979. ([classification marking not declassified])
