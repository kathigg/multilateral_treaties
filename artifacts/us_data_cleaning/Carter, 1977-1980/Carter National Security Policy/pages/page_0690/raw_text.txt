Memorandum From the President’s Assistant for National 
Security Affairs (Brzezinski) to Secretary of Defense Brown4
Washington, undated
SUBJECT
DOD’s Study of the US–USSR Military Investment Balance (S)
I have reviewed the subject paper you sent to the President on 
5 July. Although it’s an impressive piece of work and it presents an 
important message, the paper may characterize the defense investment 
imbalance too unfavorably and even the executive summary is much 
too long to ask the President to read. (S)
Because the message of this study is very important and disturb-
ing, I urge you to prepare a short 3–5 page memo for the President that:
—­crisply summarizes the study;
—­states the appropriate caveats (our staffs have discussed the 
more important ones); and
—­states your own view of the implications for our defense spend-
ing and policies. (S)
Zbigniew Brzezinski5
