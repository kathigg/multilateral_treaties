Comprehensive Net Assessment and Military  
Force Posture Review
I. PURPOSE AND STRUCTURE
A. The purpose of this undertaking is to provide a comprehensive 
examination of overall U.S. national strategy and capabilities, partic-
ularly in relation to potential adversaries. This examination will start 
from U.S. foreign policy as it exists at the present time. While recogniz-
ing that U.S. relations with potential adversaries involve elements of 
cooperation, this study will concentrate on overall U.S. national strat-
egies for achieving U.S. objectives in the face of foreign competition, 
opposition, and hostility. The study will identify alternative military 
and non-­military strategies as well as military force postures for imple-
menting current national objectives. The analysis will also identify
