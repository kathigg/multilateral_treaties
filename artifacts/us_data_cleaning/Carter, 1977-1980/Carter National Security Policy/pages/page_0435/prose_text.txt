Paper Prepared in the National Security Council2 Washington, undated COMPREHENSIVE NET ASSESSMENT 1978 Overview I. Introduction The principal purposes of Comprehensive Net Assessment 1978 (CNA 78) are to assess: 1. The changes that have occurred since January 1977 in: (a) the military, political, economic, diplomatic, and technological capabilities of the U.S. and the USSR; (b) the projections of future trends in those capabilities; and (c) our perceptions of those capabilities and trends; 2. The causes of these changes; 3. The implications of these changes for U.S. strategy vis-­a-­vis the Soviet Union.
