Paper Prepared in the National Security Council4 Washington, undated PROPOSED STATEMENT OF US CIVIL DEFENSE POLICY Enhanced Survivability. The U.S. civil defense program will seek to enhance the survivability of the American people in the event that a major nuclear war occurs by providing some increase in numbers of surviving population and leadership, thereby improving the basis for eventual national recovery. In addition, the U.S. civil defense program will contribute in part to the overall U.S.-Soviet strategic balance and reduce the possibility that the Soviets could coerce us in time of crisis. Perceptions of the overall balance should not be marked by major asymmetries in population of leadership fatalities. “Enhanced survivability” as a policy does not require similar or equivalent programs.
