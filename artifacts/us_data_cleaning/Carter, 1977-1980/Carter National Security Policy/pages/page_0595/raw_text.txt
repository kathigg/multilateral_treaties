Summary of Conclusions
An SCC meeting was held for the purpose of hearing some brief-
ings by the JCS that DOD sees as important background for the SCC’s 
continuing discussion of strategic forces employment policy. (S)
The first brief described the latest major revision of the SIOP. It 
described the targeting guidance under which the SIOP is prepared, 
the numbers and types of targets that are included in the national tar-
get base and are thus candidates for attack, the numbers of weapons 
applied to the targets under each attack option, and the consequences 
of execution under different conditions. (TS)
