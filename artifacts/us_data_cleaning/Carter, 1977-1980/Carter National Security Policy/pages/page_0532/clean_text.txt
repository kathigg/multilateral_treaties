Table Prepared in the National Security Council8 Washington, undated MAP AIR MOBILE OFF-ROAD MOBILE Concept Rotate missiles among simplified silos when maintenance needed; approximately 20 silos per missile. Carry 2 missiles each in C5A aircraft or 1 each in stretched AMST aircraft; strip alert at military bases—­disperse to civil airfields in crisis. Large transporters carrying 1 missile each; normally in garrison but scatter, across wide area on alert. Interaction with Missile Decision No advantage to small missiles; larger missiles imply lower total costs to achieve objective of 800 surviving EMT. Optimal missile smaller than M–X (TRIDENT II size missile could throw 8 RVs with air drop and northern launch areas). Same as MAP. Warning Requirement Completely independent of warning.
