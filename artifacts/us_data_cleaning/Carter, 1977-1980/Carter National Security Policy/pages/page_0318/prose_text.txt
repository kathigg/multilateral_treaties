Memorandum From Secretary of Defense Brown to President Carter2 Washington, June 17, 1978 SUBJECT M–X/TRIDENT II Commonality Study (U) We are conducting a detailed study to determine the extent of commonality we should require in the M–X missile (currently in the advanced development phase) and the TRIDENT II missile (currently still in the design phase). There is a significant difference in the performance and physical characteristics of these missiles as they are now envisaged.
