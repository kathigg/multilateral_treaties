SUBJECT
A National Integrated Telecommunications Protection Policy2

lations in Washington, New York and San Francisco, and Cuba to con-
duct an extensive and growing microwave intercept program targeted 
against US government agencies, government defense contractors, and 
US business and industry. The information collected by this intercept 
program is exploited by the Soviets to gain insight into critical US
