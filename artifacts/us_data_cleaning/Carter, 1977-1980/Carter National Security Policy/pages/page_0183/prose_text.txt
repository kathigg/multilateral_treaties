Memorandum From Secretary of Defense Brown to President Carter6 Washington, August 19, 1977 SUBJECT US Nuclear Weapons Employment Policy (U) (TS) Several months ago, you directed Zbigniew Brzezinski to ask me7 several questions concerning our current nuclear weapons employment policy (NSDM–242), and the appropriateness of retaining that policy. My response to your questions was initially forwarded to the White House on June 3.8 At that time, however, it was anticipated that PRM–10 would likely provide additional insight with respect to nuclear weapons employment questions. As a consequence, the report was held within the NSC awaiting completion of PRM–10. The report was returned to me on July 29 to permit whatever modifications necessary to accommodate my views on the results of PRM–10. (TS) I have reviewed my previously developed responses to your questions, and am enclosing the report, slightly altered to the above end, for your information and consideration.
