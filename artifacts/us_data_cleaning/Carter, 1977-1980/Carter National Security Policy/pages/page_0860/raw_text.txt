A task force of highly competent and respected computer experts 
from the private sector, headed by Bob Evans (Vice President of IBM 
for Engineering, Programming, and Technology), has been assembled 
to conduct a thorough review of the computer and communications 
problems at NORAD. The task force began meeting on Tuesday2 and a 
preliminary report is due this weekend.
I will continue to keep you advised of our progress.
