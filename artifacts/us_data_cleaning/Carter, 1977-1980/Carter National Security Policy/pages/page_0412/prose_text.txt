Purpose (S) [2 lines not declassified] The impact of this threat and potential US responses have been studied this year under the sponsorship of a number of organizations: Air Force Scientific Advisory Board (AFSAB), Defense Science Board (DSB), JASON and OSTP. This seminar provided a forum for interchange and discussion among representatives of these groups in an attempt to: 1) examine the convergence of the technical community on the basic facts and issues, 2) better focus and understand the issues, and 3) separate the issues from the underlying technical facts.
