All groups recommended accelerated R&D for both concepts. 5. Missile (C) All groups agreed on the need and importance of developing and deploying a new missile, survivably based. However, there was disagreement on the relative priority of initiating a new missile development as opposed to proceeding first with a survivable basing approach. Some panels believed that the TRIDENT II SLBM (D–5) was needed for TRIDENT and therefore recommended starting now with the development of a common missile. Others felt that a D–5 was not needed in the medium term, and recommended proceeding now to obtain the flexibility offered by a large MX. 89.
