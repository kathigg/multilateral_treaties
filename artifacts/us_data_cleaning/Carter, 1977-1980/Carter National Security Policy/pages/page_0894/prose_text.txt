Memorandum From the Director of the Office of Management and Budget (McIntyre) to President Carter3 Washington, July 24, 1980 SUBJECT Defense Program for 1982 Introduction Upward pressures for even higher Defense spending will continue. This memo provides you an update of the Defense program status and the major fiscal and programmatic questions which you will have to address in the FY 1982 budget. In March you approved 1981–85 Defense funding levels that provide cumulative real growth of $116B in this five year period. This is the largest peacetime commitment to Defense real growth since 1945.
