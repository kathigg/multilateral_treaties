Memorandum From Secretary of Defense Brown to President 
Carter1
Washington, February 18, 1977
SUBJECT
FY 1978 Budget Revisions
Attached for your approval are my recommended adjustments 
to the FY 1978 Department of Defense budget request. I believe they 
accurately reflect the conversations we have had, but they should be 
formalized for the record.2
Harold Brown
	
Enclosure
	
Paper Prepared in the Department of Defense3
