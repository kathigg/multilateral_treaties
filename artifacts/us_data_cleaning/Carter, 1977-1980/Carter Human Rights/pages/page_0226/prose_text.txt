We therefore need to have systematic discussions with other like-minded countries to explain our human rights position, not only on IFI loans but also concerning our approach on other forms of assistance, particularly when there appear to be inconsistencies in our position. By consulting with other countries through our Executive Directors in the IFIs and directly with their governments, we will avoid confrontations and stand a chance of building a consensus in favor of our position on the human rights issue. I recommend that we discuss these matters in the next meeting of the Inter-Agency Group on Human Rights and Foreign Assistance. C. Fred Bergsten5 5 Bergsten signed “Fred” above his typed signature.
