That you approve an increase of approximately 450,000 tons, $100 million, in the FY 1980 PL–480 request for such uses as those indicated in the State–IDCA–USDA memorandum, with priority for programs likely to increase US grain exports. (This is my recommendation.)8 Alternative: That you approve the State–USDA–IDCA proposal to increase the FY 1980 program from 6 million tons to 6.8 million tons. (This is my recommendation.)9 Alternative: That you increase the FY 1981 program by 800,000 tons, $200 million, as proposed by State–USDA–IDCA. c.
