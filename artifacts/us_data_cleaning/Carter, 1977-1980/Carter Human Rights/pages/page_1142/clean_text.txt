While funding does not appear to be a problem for the women’s programs, as we have noted in the case of the human rights money, there is a real reluctance on the part of AID field missions and overworked planners in the regional bureaus to develop good projects in a field which many of them believe is peripheral to development, or, at least, not of a high priority.
