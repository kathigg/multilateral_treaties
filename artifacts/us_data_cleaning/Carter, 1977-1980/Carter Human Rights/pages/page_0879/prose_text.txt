The Fund’s contribution to agricultural investment, while not impressive in terms of the amounts already being spent, is expected to act as a catalyst, spurring other financial flows. Another objective of the Fund is to help the poor and landless by fostering the use of appropriate technologies and generating rural employment. So far, however, the Fund’s executive board has approved only two projects, and it is expected that less than $100 million will be disbursed in the first year of operation.
