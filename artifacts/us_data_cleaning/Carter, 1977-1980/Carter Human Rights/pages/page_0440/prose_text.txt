The inclusion of OPIC’s authorization within the chapter of the FAA entitled “Development Assistance” and the statement of its purpose in section 231 of the FAA are indications that the Corporation is intended to serve, among other objectives, the purpose expressed in section 102(a) of the FAA “That the United States, through private as well as public efforts, assist the people of less developed countries in their (development) efforts . . .” These indications are reinforced by the relevant legislative history.
