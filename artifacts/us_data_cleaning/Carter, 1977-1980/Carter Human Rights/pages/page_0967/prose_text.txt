Memorandum From the Special Representative for Economic Summits (Owen) to President Carter1 Washington, April 24, 1980 SUBJECT Your Response to the Report of the Presidential Commission on World Hunger You received with my memorandum of March 26 an advance copy of the Hunger Commission’s final report.2 It was not possible to schedule the meeting with you that I mentioned in that memorandum, so Sol has decided to release the report on Saturday, April 26, for publication Sunday.3 This release should be accompanied by a presidential statement of appreciation, coupled with your issuance of instructions for Executive Branch review of the Commission’s proposals and submission of recommendations and other views for your decision. Attached, for your approval, are:4 (1) A draft presidential statement, which has been cleared by the speechwriters.
