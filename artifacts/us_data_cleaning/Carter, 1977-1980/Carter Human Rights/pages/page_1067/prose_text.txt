Memorandum From the President’s Special Assistant for Health Issues (Bourne) to President Carter1 Washington, January 9, 1978 SUBJECT International Health On July 30th, 1977 you sent me a note referring to the issue of International Health directing me to prepare from you to key members of the Cabinet a memo “directing them to cooperate with you in doing the analysis—I can call them in later for a meeting when I understand from you what we need to do.”2 In collaboration with the involved agencies we have completed that analysis producing a 500 page report which we have circulated for comment both within the government, and in the private sector. It will be published as an administration document.3 This memo summarizes the recommendations of that report, and following your trip4 I would like to request that we have the meeting you proposed. 1 Source: Carter Library, Staff Office Files, Special Assistant for Health Issues—
