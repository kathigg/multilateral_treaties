While I was working on this final version, I received a copy of Sam’s memo.5 I went ahead with this anyway because it includes some additional new ideas (not all my own), and because it specifically addresses the President and Vice President’s previous reservations about this proposal. This memorandum is designed to provide the President everything he needs to make a final decision—we need to get this idea out of limbo. Regarding other human rights initiatives, I have consulted with State (at the Vice President’s direction) on who should be included in the Congressional sessions. A shortened version of State’s list has been submitted to Moore, and I have received comments on the Senators but not yet on the House members. I expect final word from them shortly, and will then arrange the first meeting with Clift.
