Fulltime human rights officers in each of the Department’s regional bureaus and full or near fulltime human rights officers in each of the Department’s functional bureaus have been designated. They are responsible for monitoring human rights concerns within their bureaus, coordinating with the Department’s Office of Human Rights and Humanitarian Affairs, and relating bureau actions on human rights to actions taken elsewhere in the foreign affairs agencies. Next steps could include expansion of the mandate of the Interagency Group on Human Rights and Foreign Assistance to include all aspects of our human rights policy, not simply foreign assistance. That expanded purview would promote greater coordination of our human rights policy within the Executive Branch. VI.
