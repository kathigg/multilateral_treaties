That would keep the pressure on Tehran while not losing what momentum we have left. I do not think the Soviet veto is totally negative for us. We can— and should—attack it as an indication of Soviet interest in creating a series of compliant states on their southern border even if that requires gross violations of international law. In the Afghan case they used aggression. In the case of Iran, they are willing to ignore the rules governing immunity of diplomats, counter to the will of a clear majority of the nations on the Security Council. 144.
