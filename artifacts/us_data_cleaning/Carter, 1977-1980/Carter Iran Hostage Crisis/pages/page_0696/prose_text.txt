Diplomatic Relations/Rescue Mission The Attorney General said that a situation deemed beyond the control of local forces could justify a declaration of national emergency and the sending of U.S. troops. The President asked if the Attorney General anticipated problems in the event of an announcement this afternoon. The Attorney General said that he thought 3:00 p.m. was about right to move. We should begin during normal business hours. Afterwards we would seal the Embassy. We would let people out, but let no one in, and we would hope the numbers inside would be reduced by attrition. The Secretary of State wondered what would be the interaction between this action and the Iranian students who planned to march against the Shah.
