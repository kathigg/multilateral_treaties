Diplomatic Relations/Rescue Mission 705 Ghotbzadeh said that anything we could do on our side to improve the atmosphere would be welcome. He mentioned Senator Church’s idea of a White Paper on Iran2 in that connection. Finally, Ghotbzadeh said that the hostage issue was not having any impact at all on the campaign for parliamentary elections. 2 See footnote 5, Document 227. 263.
