Sanctions Against Iran. Dr. Brzezinski opened the meeting by noting that the President wishes to move expeditiously to implement the sanctions agreed at Camp David over the weekend. Consequently, it is necessary to inform the allies as quickly as possible. The three areas to be discussed are: (1) imposing formal economic sanctions; (2) expulsion of Iranian diplomats; and (3) conducting a census of claims against Iran.
