International Monetary and Trade Policy, 1977 Status The Japanese are prepared to sign this agreement on May 20 following authorization by their cabinet and by you to proceed. I am convinced that this is a fair and balanced agreement which can be accepted by the Japanese and our domestic interests. Attached is a description of the elements of the draft agreement which has been initialed on an ad referendum basis (Attachment A).3 Your decision is required by May 21, 1977 and I would need your authorization to sign the agreement by the 19th of May.
