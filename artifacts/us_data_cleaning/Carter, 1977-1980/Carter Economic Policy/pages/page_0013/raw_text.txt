Preface
The editor collected and selected the documentation and edited
the volume under the supervision of Erin Mahan, then Chief of the Di-
vision of Arms Control, Asia, and Africa, and Edward C. Keefer, then
General Editor of the Foreign Relations series. The volume was reviewed
by Adam Howard, then Chief of the Division of Middle East and Af-
rica. Dean Weatherhead coordinated the declassification review under
the supervision of Susan C. Weetman and Carl Ashley, successive
Chiefs of the Declassification and Publishing Division. Rita Baker and
Stephanie Eckroth did the copy and technical editing. Do Mi Stauber
prepared the index.
Kathleen B. Rasmussen, Ph.D.
Historian
