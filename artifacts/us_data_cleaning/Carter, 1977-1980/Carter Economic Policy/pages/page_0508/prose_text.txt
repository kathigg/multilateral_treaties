Giscard: We have not said this in the past. We do not want to institutionalize this. There are two advantages of announcing our intention to come together in the spring of ’79. I would like to institutionalize this body so that it meets every twelve months. It also reduces pressures from others to get in. Giscard: Let’s not be bureaucratic.
