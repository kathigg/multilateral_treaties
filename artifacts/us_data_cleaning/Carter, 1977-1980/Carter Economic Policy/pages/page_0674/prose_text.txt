Minutes of the Tokyo Economic Summit Meeting1 Tokyo, June 28, 1979 Tokyo Summit First Session2 OHIRA: Now that the press has left let’s get to business. I extend greetings to all of you. I know many of you have come from afar. I am happy to welcome the new members of our group—Mrs. Thatcher and Prime Minister Clark.
