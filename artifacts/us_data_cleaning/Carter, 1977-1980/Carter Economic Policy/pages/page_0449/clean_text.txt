Discussion Paper Prepared for the Economic Policy Group1 Washington, undated MTN AND THE BONN SUMMIT I. Overview We, the EC and Japan are committed to reaching political agreement on a comprehensive MTN package by July 15. We are also working to have Canada join such a consensus.
