Memorandum From Acting Secretary of State Christopher to Vice President Mondale1 Washington, January 5, 1978 [Omitted here is discussion unrelated to P.L. 480.] PL 480 Agreements. FY–1978 Title I agreements have now been negotiated and signed with nine governments, covering 2.7 million metric tons of commodities (approximately 50 percent of the total Title I program for FY–1978). Purchase and shipment of most of these commodities are expected to begin in the next several weeks. Negotiations have been authorized or are underway with another nine countries, covering nearly 750,000 tons of commodities.
