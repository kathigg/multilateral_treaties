Memorandum of Conversation1 Washington, November 25, 1977, 11:30 a.m. SUBJECT Meeting with Foreign Minister PARTICIPANTS US The Secretary Philip Habib Under Secretary for Political Affairs Alfred Atherton, NEA Assistant Secretary William Luers, EUR Deputy Assistant Secretary (notetaker) ROMANIA Foreign Minister George Macovescu Ambassador Nicolae Nicolae Summary of Actions The Secretary told Macovescu he would work on setting a date for the Ceausescu visit and report back to the Foreign Minister soon. The Secretary asked that the Romanians keep us up-to-date on the PLO thinking over the next few weeks. Macovescu renewed his invitation to the Secretary and to Mr. Habib to visit Romania. No commitments made on these. 1 Source: Department of State, Office of the Secretariat Staff, Cyrus R. Vance, Secretary of State—1977–1980, Lot 84D241, Box 10, unlabeled folder. Secret; Nodis. Drafted by Luers; approved in S/S on November 30. The meeting took place in the Secretary’s office.
