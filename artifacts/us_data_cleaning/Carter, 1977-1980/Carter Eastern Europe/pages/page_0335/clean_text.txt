He reiterated his view that because the Czechs seized our property we have every right to seize theirs. He was not convinced by any of our standard arguments, to wit, that legal custody of the gold is shared jointly by us, the UK, and France, that many of the claimants are elderly and would be happy with partial settlements now while they are still alive, and that the time is favorable for obtaining a reasonable settlement with the Czechs. I have asked L to examine the legal restraints on our disposing of the gold unilaterally.
