LIMITS ON COPRODUCTION Issue: The development of indigenous military industries is a priority objective of an increasing number of countries. Responding to requests for assistance in establishing production capabilities is a growing policy issue for the United States. There are two interrelated concerns: (1) Should the United States limit the number of significant coproduction agreements; and (2) How might we restrict the proliferation of arms we do approve for coproduction.
