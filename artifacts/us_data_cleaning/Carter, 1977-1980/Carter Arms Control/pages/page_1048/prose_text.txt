Unless you perceive objection, please deliver the following message, and make the following points, to Foreign Minister Silveira: Begin quote: Dear Mr. Minister: I have asked Ambassador Crimmins to make himself available to you and want to convey to you, by means of this letter and his presence, the importance we attach to our relationship with Brazil. We wish to affirm our desire to continue the consultative processes which have served both countries so well over the years. My colleagues in this administration would welcome your views on the various issues—bilateral, regional and global—with which our two governments will be confronted in coming months. For my own part, I am looking forward to the early opportunity of meeting you. Please accept my best wishes. Sincerely, Cyrus Vance. End quote. 2.
