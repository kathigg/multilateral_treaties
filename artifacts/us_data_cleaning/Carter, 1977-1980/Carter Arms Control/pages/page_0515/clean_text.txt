Vance Secretary of Defense Harold Brown Dr. Zbigniew Brzezinski Ambassador Paul C. Warnke Ambassador Malcolm Toon Mr. David Aaron Mr. Reginald Bartholomew Mr. Hamilton Jordan Mr. Jody Powell Mr. Wm. Sukhodrev, Interpreter [Omitted here is a discussion about SALT.] CTB As for CTB, the President did not see any need for detailed discussion here of verification and other matters. He believed that one primary issue he and Gromyko might discuss was the duration of the agreement being negotiated. We would like to see it have a five-year term, and then renegotiate the agreement if both sides considered it advisable to enter into a new agreement.
