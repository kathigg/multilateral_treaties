They are particularly interested in stopping possible tests of high energy laser ASAT systems. (TS) NASA is against such broad coverage because it could be interpreted as including testing of systems for displacing satellites, and thus would cause trouble for our plans to use the Space Shuttle to retrieve our own satellites from orbit. [2 lines not declassified] (TS) DOD and I believe that the test suspension should only cover ASAT interceptors because of the verification and shuttle problems.
