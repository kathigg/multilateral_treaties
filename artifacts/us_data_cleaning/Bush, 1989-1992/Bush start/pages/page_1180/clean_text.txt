On August 1, Bush participated in several morning meetings with Soviet officials before flying from Sheremetyevo Airport to Boryspol Airport in Kiev, where he was greeted by Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic Leonid Kravchuk. After delivering remarks, Bush traveled to Mariinsky Palace for meetings with Kravchuk, Scowcroft, and other U.S. and Ukrainian officials, before addressing the Supreme Soviet of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic. Following a tour of the St. Sophia Cathedral Museum, the President and First Lady participated in a ceremony and laid wreath at the Babi Yar Holocaust Memorial. At 7:07 p.m., they flew by Air Force One from Boryspol to Andrews Air Force Base. 246.
