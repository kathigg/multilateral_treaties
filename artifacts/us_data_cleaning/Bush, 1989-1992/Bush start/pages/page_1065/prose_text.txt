USSR Amb. L.E. Brooks Min. A. A. Obukhov 4. Summary: Brooks reviewed the results of his recent trip to Washington, stressing U.S. interest in prompt START completion once CFE was resolved. Obukhov said these political reassurances were welcome but should be matched with practical steps. Obukhov was negative on either using throw-­weight plus volume in a new types definition or attempting to deal with specific missiles separately. Obukhov claimed U.S. attempts to get dates on which the maximum throw-­ weight of existing types was demonstrated were an attempt to “calibrate U.S. NTM.” End summary. 5. Subject summary: U.S. attitude toward START; Stage dimensions; new types; PPCM/SSI; throw-­weight information; CFE meetings in Washington; final complaints; and follow-­up phone call.
