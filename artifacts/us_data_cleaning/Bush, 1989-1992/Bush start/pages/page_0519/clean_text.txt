492 Foreign Relations, 1989–1992, Volume XXXI The Secretary asked Shevardnadze whether he would object to his saying at his press conference that we had made significant progress on SLCMs through a declaratory approach. Shevardnadze said this was fine. The Secretary thanked Shevardnadze for his time and effort. [Omitted here is a discussion of Karelian Bear Dogs.] 75.
