Nazarkin said that Burt seemed to be closing the meeting on a negative note. Burt, wrapping up, said that the two sides had made progress. He was encouraged by work on the umbrella VSM agreement. It should be possible to resolve language difficulties on the strategic exercises agreement. Perhaps it was also possible to reach a formulation meeting both sides’ concerns on telemetry. The sides were close to resolving the unit of account issue. And there was the possibility of progress on mobile ICBM verification. He noted that the entire Working Group would meet the next day at 10:00, after which the NST sub-­group would reassemble. 47.
