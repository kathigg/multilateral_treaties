SUBJECT
Second Expanded Bilateral Session
PARTICIPANTS
The President
James A. Baker, Secretary of State

Interpreter
Mikhail Gorbachev, Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR

Aleksandr Yakovlev, Member, Politburo; Chairman, International Policy Commis-

Sergey Akhromeyev, Advisor to the Chairman, USSR Supreme Soviet
