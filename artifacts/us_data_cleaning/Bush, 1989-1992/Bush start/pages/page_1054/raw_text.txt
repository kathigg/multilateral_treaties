21. Comment: Nazarkin seemed unusually well prepared and up 
to speed on the details of all issues, including those for which he had no 
indication that Brooks would raise. Nazarkin appeared fully informed 
of discussions between Brooks and Obukhov earlier that day.4 This fact, 
coupled with the apparently large amount of time being devoted to 
internal Soviet meetings, may suggest some improvement in internal 
coordination within the Soviet delegation. End comment.
