U.S.-Proposed Joint Statement Language
The Soviet side expressed its preference for a formal agreement 
that either side could withdraw from the START in case of a violation 
of the ABM treaty, but said this is not a precondition for signing and 
implementing the START. The U.S. side welcomed the Soviet statement.
