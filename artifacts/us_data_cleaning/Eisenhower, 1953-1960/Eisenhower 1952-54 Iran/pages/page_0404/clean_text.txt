Special Estimate1 SE–33 Washington, October 14, 1952. PROSPECTS FOR SURVIVAL OF MOSSADEQ REGIME IN IRAN2 Conclusions 1. On the basis of available evidence we believe that the Mossadeq Government can survive at least for the next six months unless illhealth or death removes Mossadeq from the Iranian political scene. 2. If Kashani should come to power, the most probable result would be the progressive deterioration of Iran, possibly leading to the eventual assumption of power by the Tudeh. Estimate The Oil Issue 3. An early settlement of the oil dispute with the UK is unlikely.
