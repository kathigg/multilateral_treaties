Telegram From the Station in Iran to the Central Intelligence Agency1 Tehran, October 12, 1951. SUBJECT Analysis of Iranian Political Situation (It is specifically requested that no distribution of this report be made outside of the Agency.) 1. Background. A. Xenophobia.
