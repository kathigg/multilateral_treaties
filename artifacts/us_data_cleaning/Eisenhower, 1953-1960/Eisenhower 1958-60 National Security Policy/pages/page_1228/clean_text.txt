The terms of reference, operational concepts and ground rules reflected herein were approved by JSTPS Policy Committee on 14 September 1960. INTEGRATION AND UTILIZATION OF SIOP FORCES5 1. The objectives to be kept in mind in development of the SIOP are: A. MUTUAL SUPPORT. Timing of available forces to take max­ imum advantage of the principles of mass, crossing tracks, ECM and enemy defensive degradation. (S) B. ROUTE COORDINATION. Strike routes will take cognizance of all preplanned bomb impact points to enhance the probability of successful arrival of a weapon carrier at the bomb release line by reducing the exposure of the weapon carrier to blast, flash and radi­ ation.
