May we respectfully present the fact that you have said many times we should not underestimate a possible enemy. Based on the information outlined, however, we believe our national intelligence system is underestimating the enemy’s current and future ballistic missile capability. As a result, we also believe that our national defense plans and programs are not being effectively related to sound estimates of Soviet capability. Sincerely yours, Stuart Symington 92.
