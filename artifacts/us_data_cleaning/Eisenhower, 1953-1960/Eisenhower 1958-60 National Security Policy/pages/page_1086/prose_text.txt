National Security Policy 981 233. Memorandum From Lay to the NSC1 Washington, January 19, 1960 SUBJECT The Role of the Military Air Transport Service in Peace and War REFERENCES A. NSC 5919 B. NSC Action No. 2167 The enclosed draft recommendations on the subject, prepared by the Deputy Secretary of Defense, the Acting Secretary of Commerce, the Director, Bureau of the Budget, the Chairman, Civil Aeronautics Board, and the Administrator, Federal Aviation Administration, as a revi­ sion of the recommendations contained in the Department of Defense report on the subject, are transmitted herewith for consideration by the National Security Council at its meeting on Thursday, January 21, 1960. It is contemplated that the President’s decision following such NSC consideration will be subsequently transmitted to the Secretary of Defense and will not be circulated as an NSC paper.
