Comments on the subject paragraph provided by certain mem­ bers of the National Aeronautics and Space Council were forwarded to you with reference memorandum. 2. Following is a summary of the discussion on the subject para­ graph that took place at the June 29, 1959 informal meeting of the NASC: The explanation given as the basis for the Budget and Treasury proposal represented by the bracketed phrase was that it presents a position previously stated by the Department of Defense as noted in the last paragraph, item 6 of the NASC minutes for the April 27, 1959 meeting. There was discussion to the effect that, although this is an accurate statement of Defense position, including it in the policy seems inappropriate. Alternate wording for the phrase was proposed as follows: (2) A military space program designed to exploit the application of advancing space technology whenever that exploitation will sensibly extend U.S. military capabilities;
