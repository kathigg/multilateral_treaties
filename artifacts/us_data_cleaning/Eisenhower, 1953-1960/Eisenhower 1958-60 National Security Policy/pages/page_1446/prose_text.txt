Arms Control and Disarmament 1341 General Gruenther commented that the recent United Nations Security Council meeting was a defeat for the Soviet Union. Khrushchev has referred since to the danger of American bombers in his latest letter. Now the satellite gov­ ernments are sending protest notes.
