In furtherance of our recent conversations concerning the flexibility of the B–70 as a weapon system, the following informa­ tion might be of assistance to you in your future discussions on this subject: a. The B–­70 provides the flexibility inherent in other aircraft to include: (1) The ability to deliver high yield weapons against pinpoint hardened targets. (2) The ability to deliver ALBM’s.
