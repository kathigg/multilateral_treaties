Arms Control and Disarmament 1253 301. Memorandum From Washburn (USIA) to Cutler1 Washington, March 25, 1958 USIA feels very strongly that unless the President can state that the open shot at Eniwetok will be 90% fallout-­free or better—and unless AEC is sure it will be at least that clean—it would be better for the United States not to hold the shot. The President has already publicly used the figure of 96%.
