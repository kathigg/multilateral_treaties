The following two earlier points should be raised by the Chair­ man at the meeting with the principals: (a) The advisability of taking this matter out of the staff level and settling between the principals to prevent leakage. (b) To alleviate the growing concern that U.K. policies in several areas differ from those of the U.S., take positive action with the British to secure an agreed position on the following: (1) Link to the control system. (2) Duration (or right to withdraw). (3) Link (if any) to disarmament.
