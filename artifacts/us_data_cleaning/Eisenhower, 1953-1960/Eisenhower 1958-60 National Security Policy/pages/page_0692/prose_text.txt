Preliminary analysis indicates BOUNDER to be powered by four turbojet engines. The use of con­ ventional fuels would give it a range marginal for intercontinental bombing. The possibility for development of BOUNDER with a more advanced propulsion system exists, and the design intent for a nuclear-­ powered vehicle cannot be excluded at this time. However, present information is inadequate to permit an estimate of BOUNDER’s prob­ able development. 147. Our evidence also continues to support the existence of one or more other prototypes of new or improved long-­range bombers. Past experience cautions that existing prototypes may represent competitive designs. The Soviets may not yet have evaluated such prototypes in relation to each other or to their missile programs.
