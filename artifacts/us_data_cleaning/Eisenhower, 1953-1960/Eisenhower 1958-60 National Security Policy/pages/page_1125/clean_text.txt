1020 Foreign Relations, 1958–1960, Volume III, Supplement 247. Memorandum From Twining to Gates1 JCSM–149–60 Washington, April 11, 1960 SUBJECT U.S. Policy in the Event of War (NSC 5904/1) (C) 1. The Joint Chiefs of Staff have reviewed the proposed amendment to NSC 5904/1, prepared by the NSC Planning Board pursuant to NSC Action No. 2057, and have been unable to reach agreement thereon. 2. Accordingly, forwarded herewith, as Appendix “A”, are the views of the Chief of Staff, U.S. Army, Chief of Naval Operations and Commandant of the Marine Corps; and as Appendix “B”, the views of the Chief of Staff, U.S. Air Force. 3. It is requested that the Joint Chiefs of Staff be advised of your decision in this matter. For the Joint Chiefs of Staff: N.F.
