Memorandum From Smith (S/P) to John Foster Dulles1 Washington, July 15, 1958 SUBJECT Basic National Security Policy (NSC 5810/1): NSC Review of Military Paragraphs 13 and 14 Scheduled on July 24, 1958 I Your concept of main reliance on a capability for massive retalia­ tion to deter Communist aggression has served the free world well. You have, however, recently expressed doubts as to the continuing validity of this concept. These doubts are shared by W, C, ARA, FE, NEA and IO which believe that the time has come to adopt a new concept placing greater emphasis on varied and flexible capabilities. (See Tabs A–F.)
