U.S. Relations With the Lemus Government U.S. relations with El Salvador were without serious difficulties during most of the 1958–1960 period. Johns Hopkins University President Milton Eisenhower visited El Salvador in July 1958 during his 3-­week trip to several countries in Central America as President Eisenhower’s personal representative. He met with President Jose Maria Lemus and other government officials, businessmen, and labor leaders.
