Editorial Note On July 11, 1960, during his news conference at Newport, Rhode Island, at which Secretary Herter was present, President Eisenhower commented on the basic ideas that would guide United States participation in the forthcoming meetings of the Committee of 21 at Bogotá, Colombia.
