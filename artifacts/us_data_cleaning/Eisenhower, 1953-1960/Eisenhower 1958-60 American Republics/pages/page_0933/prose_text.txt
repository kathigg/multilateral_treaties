Nicaragua 845 NI–6. Editorial Note On April 25, 1958, a group of Nicaraguan exiles, led by Manuel Gómez Flores, launched a plot to invade Nicaragua. After stealing an aircraft in Miami, two Nicaraguan air force officers, already implicated in an earlier invasion attempt, flew to Honduras and rendezvoused with Flores and his followers.
