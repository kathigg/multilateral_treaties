SUBJECT
Publication in Arabic of the Recently Enacted Income Tax Law in Venezuela
In an effort to win friends and influence people, Mr. Bernardo 
FLORES, Venezuelan Minister to Lebanon, has announced the publi-
cation in Arabic of the new “Income Tax Law”, which has had such a 
serious effect on the foreign oil companies doing business in Venezuela.
