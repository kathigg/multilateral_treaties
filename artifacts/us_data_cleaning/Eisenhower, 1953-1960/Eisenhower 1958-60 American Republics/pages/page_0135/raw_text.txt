The DLF Board has so far approved loans to Latin American appli-
cants totaling $18,940,000. I recommend prompt and favorable action 
on an additional seven applications in the amount of $27,840,000 as a 
minimum. Even including the $21.75 million for Argentina, which is 
a very special case, the combined total for Latin America would thus 
be only $68,530,000, or less than 10% of the current $700 million DLF 
appropriation.
