Guatemala 717 The Minister of Finance explained that it had been necessary for him to call today instead of on August 22, as had been scheduled, due to the necessity of his immediate return to Guatemala. He stated that he and Mr. Fernandes had just completed negotiation of a $10 million loan from the Bank of America in association with the Chase Manhattan Bank and other unspecified banks. Terms for repayment were five years at 5 percent annual interest. The loan agreement included a provision for the deposit of certain Bank of Guatemala dollar reserves in the loaning banks on a 6-­months call basis.
