The Ambassador’s call might be a good opportunity to remind him 
of our disappointment over the Guatemalan position that the George B. 
Soto claim is a closed matter. You might say we are preparing a reply to 
the latest Guatemalan note on this subject. It is suggested that you ask 
the Ambassador to convey to his government, during his forthcoming 
trip, reiteration of our hope that the case may be reopened and a set-
tlement negotiated between the GOG and Mr. Soto’s representatives.9
