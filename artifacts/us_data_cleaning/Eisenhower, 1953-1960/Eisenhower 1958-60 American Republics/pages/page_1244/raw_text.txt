to extend to Uruguay in the foreseeable future the kind of economic 
concessions which the Uruguayan Government more ardently desires, 
such as liberal credits, since it will be extremely difficult for the United 
States Government to make this kind of concession until the Uruguayan 
Government either releases or purchases the Swift packing [illegible in

important that the United States Government give at least a substan-
