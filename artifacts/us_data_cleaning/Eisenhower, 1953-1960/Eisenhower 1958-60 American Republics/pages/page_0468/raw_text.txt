CO–37.	
Memorandum from the Office in Charge of Colombian 
Affairs (Gerberich) to the Deputy Assistant Secretary of 
State for Inter-American Affairs (Barall)1
 
Washington, October 23, 1960.
SUBJECT
Colombian Excess Sugar.2
Mr. Rourk, Mrs. Mulliken and I have about exhausted all possibil-
ities for helping the Colombians to dispose of the 40,000 tons of excess 
sugar in the United States. We have investigated the following:
1. Purchase under the Sugar Act.3 (Colombia has never joined the 
International Sugar Agreement.)4
2. DLF financing.
3. ICA purchase.
1 Source: Department of State, ARA/WST Files, Lot 64 D 1, “Colombia 1960.”

2 In a letter to Gerberich, October 28, 1960, Milton K. Wells, Counselor of Embassy
