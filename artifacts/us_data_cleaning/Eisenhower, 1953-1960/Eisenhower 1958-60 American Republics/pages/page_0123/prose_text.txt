He added that any international agreement on a charter for the Bank should be reached outside the forthcoming meeting of foreign ministers in Washington, but within the OAS.7 With respect to a suggestion that the OAS ask the Inter-American Economic and Social Council (IAECOSOC) to draft the charter, the Chairman said that the drafting was a task for specially qualified financial experts rather than the usual delegates to IAECOSOC. Mr. Dillon agreed the drafting of a charter should not be done by the staff of IAECOSOC, although it could be done under the aegis of that organization. The drafting should be done by specially designated representatives of governments, including ad hoc financial experts.
