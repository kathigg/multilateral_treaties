Editorial Note
President Alberto Lleras Camargo of Colombia made an official 
visit to the United States in April 1940. He spent the period April 5–8 
in Washington participating in official discussions with President 
Eisenhower and other United States officials. Thereafter, he traveled 
through parts of the United States, ending the official phase of his 
visit on April 14. After spending three days in Miami Beach, Florida, 
he departed for Colombia on April 17. Documentation relating to 
preparations for President Lleras Camargo’s visit, including brief-
ing materials and position papers, are in Department of State deci-
mal file 721.11, and the following Department of State lot files: ARA/
WST Files, Lot 63 D 20; Conference Files, Lot 64 D 559, CF 1621–1622. 
For additional documentation, see Department of State Bulletin, 
November 16, 1959, p. 718.
