The results of these investigations have resulted in the conclusion 
that there is no legal way in which we can buy this sugar in 1960.
There remain, apparently, only two alternatives:
1. Colombia to finance its excess stocks until next spring, mean-
while taking steps to join the ISA. Although there is a strong possibil-
ity that we can buy Colombian sugar under the Sugar Act, when it is 
renewed on March 31, it is impossible to guarantee this.
2. Colombia to sell the sugar at world market prices.
It is recommended that Mr. Mann tell this to Ambassador Santamaria, if 
he has not already done so.6
