ES–11.	
Editorial Note
Representatives of the United States and El Salvador exchanged 
notes dated January 15 and 22, 1960, at San Salvador indefinitely 
extending the Agreement of November 21, 1957, for a United States 
Air Force Mission in El Salvador. The notes were transmitted to the 
Department in despatch 310 from San Salvador, January 29, 1960. 
(611.167/1–2960) For the text of the notes, see 11 UST 76.
