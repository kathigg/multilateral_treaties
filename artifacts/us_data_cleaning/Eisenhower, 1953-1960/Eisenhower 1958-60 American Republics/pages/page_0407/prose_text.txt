Colombia 319 The Secretary apologized for being late since he had to confer with the President with respect to the latest Soviet note on a summit meeting and on Tunisia. Foreign Minister Santamaria stated that Colombia had made tremendous efforts to restore a democratic regime and to restore financial stability. It had ran into grave difficulties because of the fall in coffee prices, and although it had followed the advice of the International Monetary Fund and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development, it needed help. To underscore that need, the Colombian Government had sent two ex-Presidents, two ex-Ministers of Finance and the Director of the Bank of Colombia.
