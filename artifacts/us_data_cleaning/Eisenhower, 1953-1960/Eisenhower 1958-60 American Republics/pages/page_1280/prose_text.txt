1192 Venezuela: U.S. Relations with Venezuela VE–1. Memorandum from the Assistant Secretary of State for Inter– American Affairs (Rubottom) to the Acting Secretary of State1 Washington, January 28, 1958 SUBJECT Recognition of the new Venezuelan Government.2 Discussion: Venezuela has undergone a revolutionary change of government. The former President, General Marcos Pérez Jiménez, was forced by the Armed Forces to resign on January 23. His downfall was preceded by an unsuccessful uprising on January 1 and 2, in which the Air Force and units of the Army sought to overthrow his government.
