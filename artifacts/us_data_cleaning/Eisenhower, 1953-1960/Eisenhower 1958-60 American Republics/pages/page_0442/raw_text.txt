Editorial Note
By an exchange of notes signed at Bogotá, April 5 and 7, 1960, and 
entered into force on the latter date, the United States agreed to transfer 
to Colombia on a five year loan basis, renewable for an additional five 
year period, one destroyer pursuant to the provisions of Public Law 
85–532, an act authorizing the transfer of naval vessels to friendly for-
eign countries, enacted July 18, 1958. For text of the notes, see 11 UST 
(pt. 2) 1315; for text of the act, see 72 Stat. 376.
The destroyer loan agreement between the United States and 
Colombia was amended by an exchange of notes signed at Bogotá, July 
25, 1960, and entered into force on the same date. For text of the notes 
amending the agreement, see 11 UST (pt. 2) 2102.
