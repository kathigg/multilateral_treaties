Objectives in Paraguay The objectives of the United States Embassy, as pursued during the past two years in Paraguay, have been: to stabilize the economy and to lay a sound base for development; to establish a mutuality of understanding and confidence so that economic assistance could be programmed with the common purpose of supporting both U.S. policy and Paraguay’s national self-­interest; to educate the political thinking of the Government to cooperate with United States objectives in regional and world organizations; to nurture in the people of Paraguay an attitude of friendship and respect for the people of the United States and for our Government; to avoid all appearances of condoning the dictatorship of General Alfredo Stroessner and letting it be clearly known that similar cooperation would be extended to any other non-Communist regime which might come to power in Paraguay; and to initiate and foster steps toward democracy in a way that would give no honest pretext for charges of interference.
