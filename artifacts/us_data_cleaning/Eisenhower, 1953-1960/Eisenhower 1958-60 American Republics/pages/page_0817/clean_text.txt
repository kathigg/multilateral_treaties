Telegram from the Ambassador in Guatemala (Muccio) to the Department of State1 Guatemala City, November 15, 1960, 10 a.m. 228. Said Alejos would communicate with Department as to exact wording GOG request.2 Foreign Minister ecstatic about U.S. willingness make this commitment, stating it was what GOG had long wanted.
