Sr. Eleta then said that the six Delegations expect to hold further

Sr. Fuentes Mohr of Guatemala then said the six republics fully 
agreed on the revolutionary new program prepared by the U.S. but 
hope Mr. Dillon will do something concrete to help launch the integra-
tion movement in Central America and these republics will sacrifice 
as much as their economies will permit. In planning, financing of the 
Central American Bank the U.S. should think of Central America as 
an economic unit. So far; the movement is proceeding with differing 
degrees if intensity down there and the movement needs acceleration.
