Washington, December 11, 1959.
SUBJECT
COSTA RICA: Request for Radio-Equipped Jeeps
Ambassador Willauer has relayed to the Department an urgent 
request by President Echandi for United States assistance in the acqui-
sition of 15 new radio-­equipped jeeps. This equipment is urgently 
needed by Costa Rica, primarily to implement its capability of polic-
ing its border to prevent incursions into Nicaragua by revolutionary 
groups emanating from Costa Rica.
