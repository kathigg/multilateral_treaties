(811F.061/7–2558) PM–5. Editorial Note In an exchange of notes between Ambassador Julian F. Harrington and Foreign Minister Moreno on August 5 and 16, 1958, respectively, the United States Government and the Republic of Panama concluded an agreement that entered into force on August 16, 1958, to extend the agreement of August 5, 1957, authorizing the inspection by Panamanian nautical inspectors of vessels of Panamanian registry in the Canal Zone (8 UST 1413). For the texts of the notes exchanged, see 9 UST 1146. Documentation concerning the negotiation of this agreement is in Department of State file 611.19. PM–6.
