Chile 307 Heretofore practically all assistance had been given on a project basis and often did not reach a large part of the people. While the project approach would continue to be a useful device, it was most gratifying to know that assistance could now be given in such important sectors as housing, agrarian reform, public health, schools, etc. The President said he thought this type of program was the best answer we could make to the aggressive tactics of international Communism, and the Ambassador agreed. Editorial Note October 28, 1960 By an exchange of notes at Santiago, October 28, 1960, entered into force on the same date, representatives of the United States and Chile signed an agreement providing for a grant of funds to Chile for purpose of disaster rehabilitation.
