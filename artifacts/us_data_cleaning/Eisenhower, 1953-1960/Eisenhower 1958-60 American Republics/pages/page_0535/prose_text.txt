Thirdly, the Echandi Administration is attempting to implement, through local bond issues which it can ill afford, a plan to improve its own road network, as distinguished from the Pan American Highway. This is known locally as the Plan Vial. Since every member of the Assembly has a direct interest in a piece of this project, help on this plan ought to gain us a lot of good will with the Congress, which is where we most badly will need it to forestall criticism, if the Executive acts on Cuba the way we want it to do.
