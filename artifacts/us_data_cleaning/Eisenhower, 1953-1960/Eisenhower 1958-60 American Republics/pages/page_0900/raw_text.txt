Editorial Note
July 12, 1959.
On July 12, 1959, Armando Velásquez Cerrato, the former Chief 
of Staff of the Honduran Armed Forces, attempted once again to over-
throw the government of President Villeda Morales, launching a full 
scale revolt in Tegucigalpa with the assistance of disloyal police and 
military units. The bulk of the Honduran Armed Forces remained 
loyal to President Villeda and acted successfully to crush the revolt 
and to force Col. Velásquez to seek asylum in the Costa Rican Embassy. 
Documentation on this incident is in Department of State file 715.00.
