Washington, December 16, 1959.
SUBJECT
Ship Loan Program for Peru
I understand that—­following the recent interagency correspondence 
described in Appendix A2 and word of a second cruiser purchase by 
Peru—­a draft proposed action under Circular 175,3 in implementation of 
the Ship Loan Program as a whole, has been sent to the Defense Department 
that for Peru includes two MSFs (converted minesweepers) but omits the 
originally programmed destroyer. (See Appendix, para 4, re MSFs) The 
omission of the destroyer was in reaction to Peru’s second cruiser purchase 
and was made pending further consideration of the matter.
You are, of course, aware of our recent unsuccessful efforts to 
induce Peru to forego the purchase of the British cruisers, which we 
consider militarily unnecessary, economically wasteful and likely to 
contribute to a Latin American arms race.

the two cruisers and the cynical attitude of certain Peruvian officials in
