Sullivan, Special Assistant to the Under Secretary SUBJECT Oil, the OEPC and Venezuela Lord Home raised the subject of oil, the OEPC, and Venezuela. Mr. Dillon said that several U.S. oil companies had got the same idea at the same time, and it was apparent that thinking among the oil companies was much the same, except for the French oil companies. He said that in his opinion the situation is bad from the viewpoint of over-­all security. He pointed out that the OEPC put control of prices in the hands of Middle East companies and Venezuela.
