Editorial Note
Airgram 151 to Lima, February 14, 1958, authorized the 
Embassy to negotiate with the Peruvian Government for the sale of 
­$7.8 ­million (including transportation costs on 50 percent of the com-
modities) worth of United States surplus wheat, rice, and dairy 
products under Title I of Public Law 480, the Agricultural Trade and 
Development and Assistance Act of 1954. Enclosed was a draft sales 
agreement. (411.2341/2–1458) The draft agreement with amendments 
was effected by notes exchanged at Lima on April 9, and it entered 
into force on the same day. The text of the agreement was sent to 
the Department of State with despatch 769 from Lima, April 15. 
(411.2341/4–l558) For text, see 9 UST 693. For extensions and amend-
ments of this agreement by notes exchanged at Lima September 10 
and 12, 1958, and September 11 and 25, 1959, see 9 UST 1299 and 10 
UST 1762, respectively.
