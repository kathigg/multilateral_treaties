Panama 989 PM–62. Editorial Note In an exchange of notes on October 31, 1960, between Joseph S. Farland, the American Ambassador in Panama, and Galileo Solís, the Panamanian Minister of Foreign Relations, the United States Government and the Republic of Panama concluded an agreement that entered into force on October 31, 1960, whereby the Canal Zone Government and the Republic of Panama established procedures for the reciprocal recognition of motor vehicle operator’s licenses. For the texts of the notes exchanged, see 12 UST 301. Documentation concerning the negotiation of this agreement is in decimal file 611.1913. PM–63. Memorandum from the Director of the Executive Secretariat (Stoessel) to the President’s Staff Secretary (Goodpaster)1 Washington, November 2, 1960. SUBJECT Recent Developments in U.S. Economic Assistance to Panama Under Secretary Dillon has suggested that the President might be interested in recent developments concerning United States economic assistance to Panama. At his request, the enclosed brief summary has been prepared which, if convenient, you may wish to bring to the attention of the President. Walter J.
