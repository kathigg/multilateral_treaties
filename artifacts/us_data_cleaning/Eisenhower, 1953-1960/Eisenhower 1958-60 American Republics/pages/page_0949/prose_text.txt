Nicaragua 861 On June 4, the OAS approved sending a fact-­finding mission to report on the situation. Neither the Nicaraguan Ambassador nor President Somoza formally charged the Costa Rican Government with complicity in the invasion, but instead levelled charges against the Government of Cuba. By June 12, after several engagements with the Nicaraguan National Guard, the invaders either surrendered or were isolated and driven into the jungle.
