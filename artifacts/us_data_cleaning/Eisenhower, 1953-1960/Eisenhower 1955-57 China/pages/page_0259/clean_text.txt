Opposition to “now” in PRC section was very much stronger, implication of word not in accordance facts as far as PRC concerned, could never accept this word etc. I pointed out always included our drafts, while not in PRC drafts, they had not at last meeting or previ­ ously specifically raised issue, did not do violence to even their inter­ pretation facts etc. Clearly his instructions on this very categorical and although I used to maximum my very strong negotiation position was entirely unable shake him.
