Gowen 16. Telegram 250 from Geneva1 Geneva, August 2, 1955, 8 p.m. 250. Some correspondents have learned from CHICOM newspa­ per sources CHICOMS gave me “lists” at this morning’s meeting and will be carrying stories this effect. I have closely questioned them and appears leak probably occurred before Wang had returned from meet­ ing. Correspondents say CHICOM group including press spokesman thus far scrupulously observing secrecy agreement. I have assured correspondents if CHICOMS do not observe I will give them back­ grounder.
