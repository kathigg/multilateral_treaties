UNQUOTE We believe above reformulation, while not dropping any elements we consider essential, provides opportunity for Chinese Communists to abandon previous adamant position without loss of face, should they be inclined do so. From US point of view it has advantage constituting fur­ ther effort on our part find suitable formula and thus permit us maintain strong public position. If this reformulation is adopted we are thinking of having you tell Wang at next meeting that US giving further study to renunciation of force announcement and might have proposal make at following meeting.
