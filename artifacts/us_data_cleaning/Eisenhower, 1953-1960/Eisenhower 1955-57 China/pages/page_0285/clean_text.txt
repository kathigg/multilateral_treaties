Gowen 181. Telegram 715 from Geneva1 Geneva, September 12, 1955, 4 p.m. 715. From Johnson. Request names and other information Americans imprisoned Communist China from whom no letters received, no acknowledg­ ment packages delivered, and no other data on health or whereabouts. Believe if I am able cite specific cases indicating failure Communists provide even these most elementary facts despite their promises of year ago, I will have additional argument support my requests com­ plete information Americans still imprisoned and press for their right to communicate and their early release.
