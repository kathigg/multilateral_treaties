1955 239 182. Circular Telegram 1631 Washington, September 12, 1955 163. Sent to: All American Diplomatic and Consular Posts. For personal attention Chief of Mission from the Secretary. Reports indicate widespread erroneous impression abroad that Ambassadorial talks Geneva signify relaxation US opposition recogni­ tion or UN seating Communist China. US continues oppose recognition Communist China. US recognizes and supports Government Republic of China for reasons set forth CA 7316 of April 23. Continued Communist military buildup South China coastal area and direct and indirect support of threat of force and subversion against Governments of Korea, Vietnam and Laos are serious unsettling factors in general Far Eastern situation.
