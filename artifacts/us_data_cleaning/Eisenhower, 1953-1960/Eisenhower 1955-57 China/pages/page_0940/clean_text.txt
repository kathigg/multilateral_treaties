Herbert Hoover, Jr. Enclosure Memorandum from Adams to Hoover3 Washington, March 19, 1956 SUBJECT Meeting with Krishna Menon and Ambassador Mehta on March 19, 1956 The purpose of the meeting seemed to center upon the suggestion made by Krishna Menon that overtures should now be made toward a meeting between the Communist Chinese Government and the Americans. The rationale of the suggestion was based on the desirabil­ ity of exploratory discussions toward the resumption of diplomatic and trade relations. Prompted by a reference to the possibility of the Formosan ques­ tion being raised, it was readily admitted that the matter would unques­ tionably come up, but that the inclusion of Chiang Kai-­shek in any such meeting was an obstacle which ought not to be permitted to intervene. 2 Confidential. Copies were sent to Murphy, Robertson, and Allen. 3 Personal and Confidential.
